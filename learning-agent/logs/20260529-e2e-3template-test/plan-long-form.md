**What goes here:**
- What Item Management does in plain language: the module where all food and non-food items are created and maintained; correct setup here governs everything downstream in Inventory, Recipes, Menu Planning, and POS.
- Scope: creating items from scratch (food and non-food), configuring all item attributes, publishing to Inventory, enabling items for menus and POS.
- Out of scope in this document: item-to-recipe linking, menu planning workflows, inventory ordering/receiving, vendor contract creation (all handled in other modules — see Related content).
- The one outcome a typical user is trying to reach: a fully configured item that is Inventory-ready, linked to a menu item, priced at POS, and has complete nutritional/allergen data.

### 8. Troubleshooting

**What goes here — symptom → likely cause → fix, from transcript Q&A and known-issue tickets:**

- **Symptom: Tabs (Nutrient Info, Menu Info, Inventory Info) are grayed out.** Cause: Units card has not been saved yet. Fix: complete the Units card (Food/Non-Food selection, at least one serving or pack size for food items) and click SAVE. [Source: unmatched item #4, presenter at [14:12]–[14:30]]
- **Symptom: plus PACK button is grayed out after adding two pack sizes.** Cause: Three-tier limit has not been reached — check if a third tier already exists. Or: three-tier limit IS reached. Fix: review pack hierarchy; remove a tier or restructure. [Source: feature #5, NXT-37110, presenter at [07:05]]
- **Symptom: Inventory Ready alert remains active after entering costing.** Cause (food items only): Serving and Pack sizes are not yet linked. Fix: click Inventory Ready alert, click Edit, enter Sub-Qty to link serving to pack, click SAVE. [Source: feature #14, NXT-37441, presenter at [15:02]–[16:08]]
- **Symptom: Base Unit or Decimals setting cannot be changed.** Cause: Item has already been used in an Inventory transaction (count, receipt, adjustment). Fix: contact your district administrator or Cybersoft support — remediation requires Inventory-level intervention. [Source: feature #16, NXT-54229; feature #11, NXT-33518; presenter at [37:35]–[38:05]]
- **Symptom: Custom allergen does not appear in the dropdown when adding to an item.** Cause: Allergen has not been created in Configuration. Fix: go to Item Management > Configuration > Item Configuration > Custom Allergens > plus NEW, create the allergen, then return to the item. [Source: features #24–25, NXT-24128, presenter at [23:28]–[23:55]]
- **Symptom: Nutrient Details card will not save.** Cause: One or more required (asterisked) nutrient fields are blank. Fix: fill in the required fields, or check Missing Value if the nutrient is not on the product label. [Source: unmatched item #6, NXT-43239, Q&A Karen at [35:02]]
- **Symptom: POS Pricing tab is not visible.** Cause: Show on POS toggle on the Menu Item card is not enabled. Fix: go to Menu & POS Info tab, enable Show on POS toggle, save, then navigate to POS Pricing. [Source: feature #28, NXT-39594, presenter at [27:12]–[27:32]]
- **Symptom: Menu Info tab shows Disabled status.** Cause: Menu Item has not been created yet. Fix: click CREATE MENU ITEM on the Units card banner (only available for food items with at least one serving size). [Source: feature #26, NXT-39594, presenter at [24:55]]
- **Symptom: Costing for lower-tier units is not calculating correctly.** Cause: Cost was entered at a non-standard tier, or Sub-Qty is incorrect. Fix: verify Sub-Qty values in the pack size hierarchy, then re-enter cost for the purchasing unit. [Source: feature #8, NXT-37568, presenter at [08:08]–[08:52]]
