How setup works

Forms configuration is led by our Implementation team. Here's who does what:

Forms Licensing enabled

Cybersoft

Our team turns on Forms Licensing for your district and enables the specific form types you plan to offer.

Form built in Form Configuration

Cybersoft + your input

You share the form's questions, required fields, and help text. Our team configures it in Eligibility → Form Configuration → Forms.

Image template attached (if applicable)

Cybersoft

If your form needs an image template, we attach it under the Form Image Templates tab during configuration.

Qualification rules configured

Cybersoft + your input

You define which responses make an application Qualified. We configure the logic so the Result column populates automatically on every submission, and qualified results flow into Applications.

Form published

Cybersoft publishes Your team operates

Once we publish, the form appears to eligible households in the SchoolCafe Family Hub. Your staff review submissions under Eligibility → Other Forms, and can add submissions manually via Add New Form Application.

Frequently asked questions

How do we add a new form after launch?

Share the form's details (questions, help text, qualification rules) with our Implementation team. We set it up in your environment and walk through it with your team before it goes live to parents.

Can a form be reused across multiple districts?

Cross-district form sharing isn't available today — each district's forms are configured separately during implementation.

Where do submissions appear for our staff?

Eligibility → Other Forms shows every submitted Form Application with applicant, status, Result, and submission date.

Do Qualified submissions feed into meal eligibility?

Yes. The Forms Framework connects Application Result into Applications, so Qualified submissions flow into the downstream eligibility workflow your team already uses.

How do parents submit a form?

A parent signs into the SchoolCafe Family Hub and fills out the form on their dashboard — no separate login or external link.

What if a submission needs to be added manually?

On the Other Forms page, district staff can use Add New Form Application to enter a submission manually. Manually-added applications are tracked in the same grid as parent-submitted ones.

Can we hide Income Surveys from the Family Hub?

Yes. There's a dedicated setting that allows districts to hide Income Surveys from the Family Hub while leaving other forms visible.
