How this was built Jira issues are exported to Markdown, one file per ticket. A frequency analysis surfaces the recurring concepts and phrases. A compiler stitches those into a structured wiki of concepts, workflows, and entities. A nightly sync ingests changes from Jira so the wiki stays current — every claim on every page links back to the source ticket in raw/tickets/.
