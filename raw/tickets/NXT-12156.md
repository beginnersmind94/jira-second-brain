# NXT-12156 — AI/ML - Revenues

## Description
*HOW OFTEN TO CALCULATE:* Monthly, at a minimum.

----

*FACTORS THAT INFLUENCE:* 

* Quality of the school nutrition program
* Federal reimbursement (the percentage of students eligible for free and reduced price meals)
* Average daily participation (ADP)
* Non-program (à la carte) food sales
* District policies (e.g., open/closed campus, adequate meal periods)
* Meal pricing

## Acceptance Criteria
* The system shall be able to predict the revenue for a given district and site for a given period of time.

* The system shall provide recommendations to maximize Revenue for a given district and site using the following as factors:
** Quality of the school nutrition program (Do we have data to support this?)
** Federal reimbursement (the percentage of students eligible for free and reduced price meals)
** Average daily participation (ADP)
** Non-program (à la carte) food sales
** District policies (e.g., open/closed campus, adequate meal periods) (Do we have data to support this?)
** Meal pricing
