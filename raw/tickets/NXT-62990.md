# NXT-62990 — INV - Orders - Receipt Details - Item Price Details Slide-out

## Description
As a Site Manager I want the Charges & Discounts slide-out to show the math so I can see the total charge & discounts multiplied by the number of items I received.

!image-20260219-150133.png|width=523,alt="image-20260219-150133.png"!

## Acceptance Criteria
* Redesign the Charge & Discount slide to instead be the ‘Item Price Details’ slideout
* Relabel the header to ‘Item Price Details'
* Display the received quantity and unit for Received Quantity
* Display the calculated total for Total Charge/Discount
* Add the Item Price to the slide-out
* Follow same theming rules
* Include a ‘Reset’ icon at the top of the slide-out to restore the original values derived from configuration
** provide a user confirmation prompt 
*** “Are you sure you want to reset the item price, charges and discounts to the original values?”

----

* Provide a setting or permission for editing price details on the receipt
** Change the ‘Override Charge & Discounts’ permission to ‘Edit Receipt Price, Charges, and Discounts’
* Enable the price field as editable on the Edit Receipt screen based on the setting/permission
* Enable the slide-out charge and discount fields to be editable based on the setting/permission
* If user does not have the permission, the slide-out will be view-only 
** the Reset icon will be removed, Save button will be removed, etc.

----

* *Applies to both Receipts and Quick Receipts equally*
** only difference is the Confirm column
** otherwise the designs and flow should look very similar
