# NXT-8109 — Production - Production Record - Carryover

## Description
As a site manager, when entering the details on my Production Record page, I should have the ability to enter a quantity of servings that were not served during the day today, as “carry over” items  - and allow me to select a day/meal/menu line to carry them over to. 

In addition. the carryover quantity should also be added into the quantity of “Items Produced” - and in the event that items are carried over, those numbers should be displayed on the page, and added to the total quantity served.

## Acceptance Criteria
As I’m entering the details of what has occurred today into my site cafeteria dish page, I will have the ability to set any leftover quantities to be served on a different day… with the ability to specify the quantity, the date I want to carry it over to, the meal, and/or the specific menu line.

When items have been carried over, the total amount of what was carried over should be added to the “Produced” quantity, and displayed in the “served” box on the production record page.

When I carry over a menu item to another meal service, I enter the quantity of servings that I’m going to carry, and then select the date and meal service that I’m going to carry it to by clicking on the calendar next to the servings box. 

!image-20210625-173749.png|width=1793,height=900!

Selecting that calendar icon opens up a window… which contains dropdowns of Meal Type and Menu Line to select from. Those drop downs will contain the meal services/lines that have been assigned to my site. If both Breakfast and lunch are assigned, then the dropdown will contain both services to select from. If I have more than one menu line for the service selected, then those would be available to select from in the menu line dropdown. I will then select a date on the calendar that will indicate what day I intend to serve the leftover food items. 

!image-20210625-173944.png|width=1018,height=716!

The carry over details will appear on the production plan of the date that I’ve selected, so that I have a reminder on that date… 

!image-20210625-174126.png|width=1793,height=900!



And they will also pull through to the Production Record for me. Because those items were produced on the original date, they are automatically counted as being served on the new date… and are NOT counted as part of the “newly produced” food items on that day.
