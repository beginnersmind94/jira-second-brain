# NXT-70515 — SCTV+ - Overflow Cards - Use full slide design

## Description
*requirements*

* Update overflow slides to retain the design of the existing slide, and only update the widgets impacted by overflow.
* Scenario:
** Slide created with 2 MP widgets.
** 8 beverages, 5 vegs. 
** widgets are set to 5 menu items max. 
** initial slide shows 5 bev, all 5 veg. 
** overflow slide shows the remaining 3 bev, and all 5 veg.
* Scenario:
** Slide created with 3 MP widgets.
** 8 bev, 5 veg, 7 entree.
** initial slide would show 5b, 5v, 5e.
** overslide slide would show 3b, repeat the 5v, 2e.
* Scenario:
** Slide created with 2 MP widgets
** 13 bev, 7 veg.
** initial slide would show 5b, 5v
** overflow slide 1 would show the next 5b, 2v
** overflow slide 2 would show the remaining 2b, the 2v from overflow slide 1

----

Just a note for Sammie 

* we’re going to have the option to wrap / stack the MP cards in the future. Like showing 2 rows in a single MP widget.
* Might be worth keeping this in mind for the work here in case it’s an easy addition, or will otherwise lead to a big rewrite.

## Acceptance Criteria
* Overflow slides updated to re-use the base slide design.
** All widgets are included in the overflow slide.
** Only the impacted widgets are updated with new items, other widgets show the same items as the base slide.
