# NXT-66384 — Fin - Push Meal Sales Revenue from Accountability to Financials for Automation

## Description
*As a* nutrition director
*I want* POS meal sales to automatically post to the general ledger when I close a POS period
*So that* my meal revenue is accurately reflected in financial reports and I can track cash flow by payment method.

## Acceptance Criteria
*Requirements:*

* All Meal Sales Revenue accounts mapped to GL Accounts
* Sub-Accounts created for the different sites (look at how this is worded in the actual account code in financials)
* Ledger Entries are created by site.
* The last day of the POS Period is used as the Posting date. 
* Financial periods must be open from the date of posting to the current date.
* All POS periods that fall in the selected Financial period’s date range need to be closed.
* *When creating these ledger entries in the database, each debit/credit line must be attributed to the originating data point / data source (e.g., the specific meal sales revenue data point or reimbursement data point) so the Profit & Loss report can calculate totals by data point even when one GL account is mapped to multiple data points.* 

*When a POS period is closed:*

* Backend accesses the <meal sales revenue source> from the accountability database
* Ledger Entries are created in the tables associated with the given district with the following details:
* *Transaction Code:* MEALSLS
** *Transaction Description:* Meal Sales Revenue
*** *Accounts Debited:* <Cash In Bank GL Site Sub Account> AND <Prepaid Account GL Site Sub Account>
*** *Amount Debited:*
*** *Accounts Credited:* <Meal Sales AND A La Carte Sales GL Site Sub Accounts>
*** *Amount Credited:*
*** The *meal sales account* must align to the *student revenue source* (e.g., School Breakfast Program), and the *a la carte account* must align to the *student meal type* (e.g., Breakfast).

* Note that there may be multiple accounts involved on either side of the transaction. Validate that debit and credit totals are equal.
