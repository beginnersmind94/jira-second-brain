# NXT-41870 — System - Content Management (Part 1a: Create backend for Onboarding tour text)

## Description
*Note: This story has been split. This part is for creating a backend for the Onboarding* +*Tour step*+ *texts.*

----



As a Customer Success administrator, I would like to be able to quickly update specific content that users are seeing in the Onboarding wizard, for all districts at once, so that I do not need to depend on a production release to push out new information to users.

*Summary*

Create a database of all existing text contained in each screen of the onboarding wizard so that it can be referenced externally now in the backend and updated in the future through a UI.

*Mockup*

N/A

*Details*

* In the Onboarding wizard, get and copy the text of the following content:
** Welcome screen 
*** Body
*** Pop-ups
*** Tooltips
*** Button label (not including Help button)
** Feature selection screen (where Go-Live date is set)  
*** Body
*** Tooltips
*** Pop-ups/Tour steps
*** Error message
*** Button labels (not including Help button, but including Schedule Training button)
** System Tasks screen
*** Body/Task steps
*** Tooltips
*** Pop-ups/Tour steps
*** Button labels (not including Help button, but including Schedule Training button)
** Front Office Tasks screen
*** Body/Task steps
*** Tooltips
*** Pop-ups/Tour steps
*** Button labels (not including Help button, but including Schedule Training button)
** *_Note:_* _We do not need to capture text for redirected steps (such as Site Configuration), only the main hub pages above._
* Put the above texts in either a new table or a secure, external resource. 
** Records should reference the page (i.e. Feature Selection), area or type (i.e. Tour step 1, Column 1, Button 1 or Title, Header, etc.), and the specific text itself. This will be important for the UI later.
* Update the existing pages to reference the corresponding records in the table/resource. Goal: almost nothing in Onboarding should be hard-coded anymore for present or future text.
* *_Note:_* _QA Effort can be low here and higher in part 2, when we create a UI to manage the messages. In this story, QA just needs to work with dev to verify that the messages are being referenced._ 
* DEV: Please add a screenshot of the table structure in the comments when this is complete. It may be helpful in updating the mockup for the UI later.

## Acceptance Criteria
Verify that all Onboarding pages have their texts contents copied and catalogued in a table or external resource

Verify that all of these pages now reference the associated record instead of being hardcoded

Verify that a screenshot/description of the new table structure is added to the comments
