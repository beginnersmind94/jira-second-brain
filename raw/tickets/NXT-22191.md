# NXT-22191 — INV - Physical Inventory Resource Updates

## Description
As an Inventory supervisor I want the ability to control whether or not certain users can see item perpetual quantity or perform physical inventory actions.

*Resource Structure:*

* Physical Inventory - Page (including Search tab) 
** Physical Inventory Details - Sub-page
*** Cancel Physical Inventory - Action
*** Reopen Physical Inventory - Action
** Physical Inventory Status - Tab
*** Review Completed Inventories - Sub-page
**** Reconcile Perpetual Inventory - Action
