# NXT-17417 — INV - Orders - Receipt Detail - Confirm checkbox logic

## Description
As an ordering manager I want a Confirm checkbox so that I can explicitly confirm that I have received each item, and so I can see the calculations for total price.

*Requirements*

* In addition to the existing item-level Confirm checkbox, provide a Confirm checkbox in the column header
** this will function as a “Confirm All” checkbox
** when this is checked, all items on the receipt will be checked (confirmed) as well
** the availability on this will be controlled by a permission
*** if user does not have the ‘Receive All’ (TBD) permission, the checkbox in the header will not be present
* On Receipt Detail screen, when Confirm checkbox is checked, price calculations occur
** Quantity * (Unit Price + Delivery Fee + Processing Fee - NOI) = Total Price
* Price calculations are displayed upon Confirm check
** Show Delivery Fee calculation (Delivery Fee * Received Quantity)
** Show Processing Fee calculation (Processing Fee * Received Quantity)
** Show NOI calculation (NOI * Received Quantity)
** Show Total Price 
*** Total Price for one item (Unit Price plus any charges/discounts)
*** Total Price for all received quantity (Total Price * Received Quantity)
* Update the Receipt Amount as items are confirmed
** each confirmed item will have its total price added to the header-level Receipt Amount label
* Update the Charges & Discounts section as items are confirmed
** The view-only Charges/Discounts (from the contract) will be updated as items are confirmed
*** Delivery Fee (total of all delivery fees for all items)
*** Processing Fee (total of all processing fees for all items)
*** Net Off Invoice (total of all NOI discounts for all items)
* If a checked/Confirmed item is *unchecked:*
** remove the calculation labels for that item
** remove the value of that item from the Receipt Amount
** remove the value of its charges/discounts from Charges & Discounts section
