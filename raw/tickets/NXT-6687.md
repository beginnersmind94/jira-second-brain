Conditionals 

* Household Size differs from Actual count > Show message when number is entered that’s different 
** HH Size not required if ALL students are Categorical status or receive benefits from assistance programs.
** Warning icon - hover text: _“The household size does not match the number of household members.”_
***  i.e. blank entry (no number) or incorrect
**** if they add a number and it’s incorrect > warning 
**** {color:#ff5630}when they enter the page > {color}warning
* Household member name and income matches the name and income of a student
** Warning icon - hover/click text: “_Please verify household member information as it matches a student.”_
* If yes to benefits or programs  > info message will show at the top of the page for Household Members: "_This section is not required due to the basis of the application._"
** HH size not required for categorical apps (so no hard stop here when message is triggered).

Settings

* Total Child Income
** Yes > remove the individual student income section from the student page 
*** Split the table with students and household members 
***  Add a section/row for “Total Child Income” - default is $0.00 and Annual (frequency) and an edit pencil 
**** Edit triggers a popup an Income amount field and frequency field
*** Included in Total Income amount
** No > current process
* Alaska Permanent Fund Dividend (PFD) 
** Enabled > add question to HH member popup > _“Please indicate if a PFD was approved for the following years.”_
*** Show current year and previous year with checkboxes (they don't have to select either) 
**** Checkbox - 2019 \[previous year]
**** Checkbox - 2020 \[current year]
***** {color:#ff5630}*API call or UI?? Haritha >* {color}If UI, we don’t save that data anywhere?
*** Special income type for each year 
**** 2 years at a time (i.e. 19-20 changes each year)
*** Display answers in Household grid
**** i.e. PFD Approval: 2019, 2020 
**** If neither year box is selected show > 'PFD Approved: No' in the grid
