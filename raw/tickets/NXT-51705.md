# NXT-51705 — PR- Grocery List- Prep site and receiving site items on report to consider only consolidated (API ONLY)

## Description
As a prep site, when I am pulling the grocery list for all the items I need to prepare for all other sites, they are currently not showing up on the grocery list for me to know.

## Acceptance Criteria
* All prepared menu item ingredients should show on grocery list on prep site report
** Display total for all menu item counts on the amount needed as a combined total
** Display all additional non prepared menu item ingredients for prep site
** Display total quantity needed for the combined total
* On receiving site, *only* display non prepared item ingredients
* THIS ONLY APPLIES IF PREP SITE AND RECEIVING SITE ARE DIFFERENT
* *ONLY Consider prep site item counts once consolidated plan is complete*
**  *Until consolidated plan is complete, consider plan counts for prep site only from plan tab and not include consolidated totals*
* *If user saves and all numbers appear on report and then they go back and delete the consolidated plan, roll back the calculations to consider totals without consolidated plan*
* *Once planned counts are added for prepared items for receiving sites, they should never appear on their report regardless if consolidated plan is completed or not*
