# NXT-22027 — Platform - BO - Onboarding Permissions Setup

## Description
As a district admin, I should be able to access onboarding pages when I have access to onboarding role.

* When customer logins for first time, only after onboarding role permissions check, they will be presented with district selection and next steps  welcome pages, onboarding pages based on user access
* When customer logs back in, system shall resume where it has been left of after checking the district and onboarding permissions.
* If the user do not have access to onboarding a district, system shall not allow to proceed

## Acceptance Criteria
* Customer shall be able access all onboarding pages & widgets if he/she has onboarding role/permissions access
* Customer is able to select the district and access onboarding pages/widgets based on the district selection
* Customer is able to access welcome pages, activity pages & dashboard summary pages when I have access onboarding role
* When customer logs back in, system shall resume where it has been left of after checking the district and onboarding permissions.
* If the user do not have access to onboarding a district, system shall not allow to proceed
