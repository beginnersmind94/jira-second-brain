# NXT-20062 — INV - Vendor Detail - General Info Card - History 

## Description
As an Inventory manager I want history for all my vendor sections so I can see what has changed.

*Requirements*

* Use the new [standard history component|https://qasharedutilitiesservice.perseusedge.com/swagger/index.html] developed by Front Office
* Add History icon to the top right corner of the General Info card
** provide a hover tooltip ‘History’
* Show the common History slide-out on click of History icon
* Show all historical entries for the General Info section
** Performed By (user)
** Date (of change)
* Show before/after value for each change
** Example:
*** Vendor Lead Time
*** Previous Value: 2 Days
*** New Value: 4 Days
