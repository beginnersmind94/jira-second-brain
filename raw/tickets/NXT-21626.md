# NXT-21626 — Platform - BO - UX - Welcome Preview Screens

## Description
As a district admin, I should be able to setup my district configurations after logging into SchoolCafe for first time which presents a preview tour and selection of activities so that I can understand the complete onboarding process

* Welcome preview screen with onboarding tour video (*sample shown in attachment + wireframe I drew*)
** {color:#ff5630}*Check Welcome - p1.jpg for wireframe*{color}
* Selection questionnaire for activities that customer are presented with based on district modules licenses
* Post selection of questionnaire shall bring up the stepper functionality for customer to complete the activities one step after step (*sequential)*. 
** *THIS MUST be a stepper function with clear indication that they need to complete steps one by one.*
* Once all stepper activities are complete, Onboarding Dashboard is loaded

## Acceptance Criteria
Customer shall be able to:

* Preview welcome screen when district admin logs for the first time and no setup has been started for that specific district
* Preview of the welcome video is available customer to play within the pop-up with expand/maximize video option
* "Jump to Setup" button is available to browse through questionnaire for onboarding & selection of activities
* Once video is complete, customer shall be able to close the video with replay option & animation for "Jump to Setup" should kickoff
* Customer shall be able to set “Target Go-Live Date” in questionnaire screen
* Customer shall be able to set “Readiness Date” in questionnaire screen
