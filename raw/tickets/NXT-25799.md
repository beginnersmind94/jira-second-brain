# NXT-25799 — Inventory Turns KPIs and Metrics - MVP

## Description
+*KPI/Metrics for Inventory Turns*+ 



Inventory turnover ratio measures the rate of sales and replenishment of an item over time and is calculated by dividing the cost of goods sold by the average inventory for the same time period. 

 

Metrics required for INTR:

Beginning Inventory (Start of Month items purchased or inventory) = SMIP

Ongoing Inventory (Purchased during the month) = DMIP

Ending Inventory (End of Month Purchases or inventory) = EMIP

 

Average Inventory Value (AINV) = (SMIP+EMIP)/2

 

Inventory Turnover Ratio (INTR) = ((SMIP + DMIP) – EMIP)/AINV

 

Industry Standards: A school that receives a delivery once every 7 to 10 days should have a turnover rate of 2-3 times a month (i.e. INTR between 2 to 3 is a good standard)
