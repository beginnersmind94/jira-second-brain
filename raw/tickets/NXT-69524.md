## Acceptance Criteria
Business Rule Updates

* Consume all inventory statuses for items added to Bulk Update sessions including Synced and Locked
* Update the Pack-Serving Link sub-step to lock down relevant selections based on the inventory status
* Sub-Quantities
** Provide serving size edit and selection for items with multiple eligible inventory-able servings 
*** ex: 1 Pizza, 1 Slice
*** default to largest eligible serving 
*** only for items not already synced/locked
** ‘Use Serving as Base Unit’ cannot be enabled for Locked (or Synced?) items
** ‘Provide Servings per Base Unit’ can always be enabled if the serving is countable, even for Locked/Synced items
* Pack-Serving Link - Weights
** Base Unit Weight can always be edited, but cannot be removed if it breaks pack-serving link (weight mandatory)
** Whole/Decimal option
*** Locked & Decimals cannot be modified (this is permanent for now) - disable the selection
*** Locked & Whole can be changed to Decimals 

UX Updates

* Use single Item column with *(LI-1234) Item Name* formatting used on other sections
* Add Inventory status column at end of grid
