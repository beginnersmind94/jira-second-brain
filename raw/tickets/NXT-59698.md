## Acceptance Criteria
* Repurpose/modify the Contract Delivery Schedule to a ‘Shipping Schedule’ 
** for internal vendors
* Provide a Shipping Group dropdown on the Site Schedules grid
** default to ALL groups
* Provide an Update Schedules button at the top card
* In Update mode, require a single shipping group to be selected if one is not already selected
* Display the “main” schedule according to the combination of site schedules
** main schedule is _derived_ for internal vendors, not configured
* Add messaging to sticky footer
* Show icons next to sites with unsaved changes (next to Status)
