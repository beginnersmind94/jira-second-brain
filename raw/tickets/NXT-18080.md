# NXT-18080 — INV - Orders - Manage Orders - Receipts - Quick Receipt - Confirm

## Description
As a site manager I want the ability to save or complete a Quick Receipt so I can either received the product into my inventory or save the Quick Receipt for later.

This story is for the back end actions that occur when user is confirming a Quick Receipt. The UI impact is covered on [https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-18083|https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-18083|smart-link] 

*Requirements*

* On the Receive step of the Quick Receipt functionality, provide the Confirm button
** do not enable this button until all required fields are provided:
*** Delivery Location (if applicable)
*** Invoice # (unique)
*** Invoice Amount 
*** all Receive Quantities greater than zero
* When user clicks Confirm button
** This will officially create the receipt and complete the Quick Receipt
** the order will be saved and immediately put into ‘Closed’ status
** Order # will be assigned
*** will also need a Receipt # assigned to it
** The screen will update ([NXT-18083|https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-18083])
** Perpetual Inventory will be increased accordingly
* Update the Status and ‘Entry Method’
** Order Status will be ‘Closed’
** Entry Method will be ‘Quick Receipt'
* Once a Quick Receipt is confirmed, it will appear mostly the same as a regular order ([NXT-18050|https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-18050])
