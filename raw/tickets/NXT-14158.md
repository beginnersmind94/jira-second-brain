# NXT-14158 — POS - Default opening balance for denominations or entered amount

## Description
As a cashier I want the ability to establish a default opening balance based on denominations or by entering an amount so that I do not have to input my standard opening amount each session since my till is always the same.

Recommended design would be an option toggle to enter opening balance by denominations or amount. Based on the selection by the user, information could be entered and we’d need a second toggle to “Save as default”. This flag would remain for this terminal until it is removed.
