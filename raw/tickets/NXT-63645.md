## Acceptance Criteria
* Provide the ability to generate an a “manual” distribution
** add button on calendar page
** differentiate hotshot distributions with hotshot icon
** Require user to select a date to begin hotshot
*** date must be today’s date or later
* Repurpose the Review tab as a step for selecting sites for the new manual distribution
* Allow Assignment to begin once sites are added to the Review
* Generate orders at the same time of receipt pre-population ([NXT-67614|https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-67614])
* Provide a Cancel Distribution button
