# NXT-5709 — INV - Vendor - Add Items to Vendor/Contract - Search Items 

## Description
As a Procurement Specialist I need a way to search items from Item Management and add those items to a vendor & contract so I can begin listing the items that I will be receiving from the vendor.

* +Story 1+ - Effort: *8*
** API: 3 days
*** pull from IM
*** get existing contract items to filter
** DB: 1 day
*** new tables
** UI: 5 days
*** Search/filters
*** Table with items
** QA: 5 days

*Requirements*

* A search function to search for items and assign them to the vendor.
** Can filter by:
*** Item Category
*** Storage Category
*** Data Source
** Can search by:
*** Production Description like*
*** Product # or GTIN like*
** Apply/Search button
** Search will only return *Active* items
* Search will result in a dynamic table that loads the items that were returned by the search
** Item # (this is intended to be District Item Number)
** Item Name
** Item Category
** Storage Category
** GTIN 
** *This table will not display items that are already assigned to the selected contract
