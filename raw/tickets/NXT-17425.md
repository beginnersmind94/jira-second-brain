# NXT-17425 — AI Data Insights – Measure Actionable Events - Eligibility and Participation 

## Description
As a system, AI insights would like to measure actionable metrics and KPIs so that we can evaluate success/failure standards.

*Description:* 
Goals for this data Insights is to maximize participation either through eligibility prediction or by recommendation to districts and schools with affecting factors to increase participation.  AI/ML will provide potential forecast data so that application can outreach and track actionable events.

h3. Measure actionable insights - KPIs and metrics

a.	Goal Completion Rate (GCR)
b.	Parent Effort Score (PES)
c.	Participation Increase Target Level (PITL)

h4. *Goal Completion Rate (GCR):*

Based on the final actionable items defined and responded by district admins, school admins and parents, GCR can be evaluated to a threshold which can be considered as achieved, partial or not achieved categories.

* Response rate analysis on email notifications: number of views, clicks, responds, change rate
* Total number of parents completed, partially completed, and failed to complete and abandoned application (versus outreached per district, per school)
* Total number of parents completed, partially completed, failed or abandoned Income survey 
* Response rate analysis on physical mail notifications responds, change rate
* Response rate from other action items – (refer to insight actionable item story)
(Above may change based on app AI capability design definition) - TBD

h4. Threshold standards

# Achieved:  >23%
# Partial: > 12% and < 23%
# Not achieved: < 11%

h3. *Parent Effort Score (PES):*

PES is an actionable metric used for AI insight improvement

h4. Standards:

#  PES question(sample): “Schoolcafé eligibility application is quick and easy” 
#  Feedback score: 1- 5
# PES calculation: Total score/Total received responses

h3. *Participation Increase target level (PITL):* 

Both the key insight KPIs - Eligibility and Participation increase level percentages and the pre-insight levels are considered. 

h4. Calculation: 

+PITL for Eligibility:+

*       Eligibility Increase target level = achieved target level – pre insight level

+PITL-Participation+

*       Participation Increase target level = achieved target level – pre insight level

## Acceptance Criteria
1.	Participation increase target level (PITL) shall be calculated for achieved eligibility score
2.	Participation increase target level (PITL) shall be calculated for achieved PNA score 
3.	Participation increase target level (PITL) shall be calculated for achieved Participation score
4.	Participation increase target level (PITL) shall be presented as a percentage.
5.	Parent Effort Score (PES) shall be evaluated and presented as a percentage.
6.	Goal Completion Rate (GCR) shall be evaluated based on completion rate score
7.	Parent feedback KPI shall be calculated with PES, GCR, Other KPIS and shall be presented as a percentage.
