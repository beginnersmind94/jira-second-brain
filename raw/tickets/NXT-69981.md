# NXT-69981 — FIN - Backend Posting Logic for FO

## Description
Scope

* This story defines backend posting behavior for transactions that reference FO-related data points.

## Acceptance Criteria
h3. Sales Tax Posting (Taxable Sales)

* Taxable sales include Adult, Staff, Visitor, and taxable A La Carte transactions.
* For each taxable transaction line:
** Total Amount = Revenue Portion + Sales Tax Portion
** Credit Revenue Portion to the mapped revenue GL account selected via Account Mapping UI.
** Credit Sales Tax Portion to the system-configured "Sales Tax Owed" liability GL account.
** Debit is Cash and/or Prepaid based on payment method and settlement logic.
* Sales tax must never be credited to the mapped revenue GL account.

h3. Payment Method / Settlement (Debit Side)

* For POS period close postings:
** Debit includes Cash in Bank and/or Prepaid Account depending on payment method mix.
** Credit includes the relevant revenue account(s) mapped via Account Mapping UI.

h3. Deposit Variances (Cash Over/Short)

* When cash over/short differences are detected during Shift Close:
** Post the variance amount to the "Deposit Variances" data point.
** The mapped GL account for "Deposit Variances" is used for posting (as configured in Account Mapping).
