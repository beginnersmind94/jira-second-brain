# NXT-4916 — AcctMgt - Patron - Adult Details - General

## Description
*User Story*

As a district admin I want to see the general information on an adult’s account.

*Mock-up*

[https://nqyc9v.axshare.com/start.html#p=category_based_on_patron_view_-_stepper&id=tj8d55&g=1|https://nqyc9v.axshare.com/start.html#p=category_based_on_patron_view_-_stepper&id=tj8d55&g=1]

*Requirements*

* User should be able to load an adult off of the search page and see their general information.
* Adult accounts are a subset of the information we’ve put together for students.
* The first panel will be the general information.
* User should be able to view and edit information, like a student.
* We can mostly copy what was done for a Student here, but there are a couple of changes - 
** Display of the name has a suffix that needs to be shown as part of the full name field.
*** Last, First, Suffix
** Prefix/title is also present as a field.
** Enrollment Site becomes Site
** The following fields are not needed:
*** Reason
*** Grade
*** Homeroom
*** Roster
** Eligibility becomes ‘Meal Pricing’ with different options:
*** Staff
*** Program Adult
*** Visitor

## Acceptance Criteria
An adult's information can be viewed while searching / clicking on a card.
Information can be edited appropriately.
Multi-language support enabled (LANGUAGENAME_ENGTEXT ie: FR_Students, FR_ADULTS)
