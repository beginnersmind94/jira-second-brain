# NXT-34759 — Improve User Experience - User Inactive Timeout

## Description
Manage Views - Improve User Experience

## Acceptance Criteria
* An internal setting shall exist that will define the duration of user inactivity that will result in the user being timed-out - {color:#bf2600}*As per the Dev team this cannot be done as we read from KeyCloak*{color}
* When the inactivity duration is reached, 
** The user will first be warned that they will be time-out.  
** Then, they will be given 1 minute to cancel the timeout.
*** If they cancel, their “inactivity timer” shall be reset and they will not be timed-out.
*** If they do not respond to the warning within the 1-minute timer, they will be logged-out and sent to the login UI.
* This shall be enabled in R6.2 and R6.1
