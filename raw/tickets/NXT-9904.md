# NXT-9904 — R&D - MP > Menus > Assign Menus - research and design process for creation and synchronization of Production Records for assigned Menus

## Description
As a Menu Planner
I need to be able to assign Menus
so that I can let my site staff know what to serve.

Research and design what happens when Menus are assigned to the Calendars for Production Record creation.

In the future Menus will be assignable from the Central Realm (or equivalent) to even larger numbers of Sites and Districts. 

Determine how best to coordinate and synchronize these records when a Menu changes (which is allowed until a Record is completed). Menu Items can be added/removed. Serving Groups can be added/removed. Serving Sizes can be adjusted.

## Acceptance Criteria
Research and design has been completed. Design document with code workflow has been approved.
