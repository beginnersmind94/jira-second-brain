# NXT-7571 — INV - Physical Inventory - Add Storage/Item Category totals on Review Screen

## Description
As a district supervisor I want a comprehensive review screen for site physical inventories so I can review item counts, evaluate discrepancies, preview perpetual inventory, etc.

Totals:

* Totals for Storage Category
** total Physical Count for the Storage Category
*** Whole Units
*** Broken Units
** total Perpetual Count for the Storage Category
*** Whole Units
*** Broken Units
** total *NET* discrepancy information
*** Whole Units (+/-)
*** Broken Units 
*** Dollar Amount
*** Percentage 
* Totals for Item Category
** total Physical Count for the Item Category
*** Whole Units
*** Broken Units
** total Perpetual Count for the Item Category
*** Whole Units
*** Broken Units
** total *NET* discrepancy information
*** Whole Units (+/-)
*** Broken Units 
*** Dollar Amount
*** Percentage 

* Totals for Storage Category
** total Physical Count for the Storage Category
*** Whole Units
*** Broken Units
** total Perpetual Count for the Storage Category
*** Whole Units
*** Broken Units
** total Valuation
*** Physical Valuation - the $ value of the physical quantity of that Storage Category
*** Perpetual Valuation - the $ value of the perpetual quantity of that Storage Category
* Totals for Item Category
** total Physical Count for the Item Category
*** Whole Units
*** Broken Units
** total Perpetual Count for the Item Category
*** Whole Units
*** Broken Units
** total Valuation
*** Physical Valuation - the $ value of the physical quantity of that Item Category
*** Perpetual Valuation - the $ value of the perpetual quantity of that Item Category

Additional header information provided for supervisor/district users: (in addition to the header information already on the screen as part of [https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-7567|https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-7567|smart-link] )

* Total Number of Items
* Number of Items Requiring Correction on Physical Inventory
** quantity of items
** percentage of overall itwms
* Average Correction Required in Cases
** quantity
* Total Discrepancy Amount
** dollar value
* Total Valuation

By default the list will be the items and the entered quantity counts. Provide an option to toggle and see discrepancy information.

* List of items with discrepancies by quantity 
* List of items with discrepancy by value

Item List Views:

* Discrepancy View - the Item List is updated to display inventory Discrepancy information
** new table view:
** Items
*** still maintain Storage Category then Item Category organization
** Physical Quantity
*** Whole Units
*** Broken Units
** Perpetual Quantity
*** Whole Units
*** Broken Units
** Discrepancy
*** Whole Units
*** Broken Units
*** Amount in Dollars ($)
*** Percentage 
* Valuation View

Reviewer Tools:

* Customizable view
** allowing different organization of the item list based on user
*** managers may want to view by item category, while financial people may want to view by commodity or non-commodity, or according to valuation group
** viewing item counts vs. count dollar values, also based on user
*** financial people will be less interested in quantity counts, but want to view valuation for any potentially alarming entries
* Analysis Tools
** Discrepancy Search - the ability to filter items by discrepancies
*** Items with discrepancies exceeding a certain $ value
**** by specific $ value
**** by percentage of value
*** Items with discrepancies exceeding a certain quantity threshold
**** by percentage
** Count History - ability to view the history of specific count entries
*** count history across multiple physical inventories
*** past history of item, both on inventory counts and ability to drill down into transaction details
** Perfect entries
*** when too many items on an inventory are being entered exactly according to perpetual (see [Blind Count|https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-7546])
*** when items are being entered exactly as perpetual over multiple physical inventories
* Review Tools:
** the ability to send specific items back to the site for recount
*** ex: this item exceeds discrepancy threshold; please recount
*** district may only request recount for select items, based on analysis criteria
** communication/notifications 
*** when PI status changes
*** when site is done with count
*** when district has reviewed
*** upon reconciliation
