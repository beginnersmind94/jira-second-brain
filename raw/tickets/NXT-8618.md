# NXT-8618 — INV - Physical Inventory - Supervisor View - Items tab - Valuation list (Pending Reconciliation)

## Description
As an Inventory District Supervisor I want an option to view inventory items by discrepancy quantities so I can review all discrepancies on the inventory.

This will be an optional view on the Items tab of the Pending Reconciliation screen.

When user selects the ‘Valuation’ view radio button, the item list will update the displayed columns

!image-20201224-212857.png|width=91.66666666666666%!

+Item List:+

* Same as regular Items view:
** Storage Category
** Item Category
** Item #
** Item Description
* Additions:
** Physical Quantity
*** Whole Units
*** Broken Units
** Perpetual Quantity
*** Whole Units
*** Broken Units
** Valuation
*** Physical Valuation - the $ value of the physical quantity
**** already saved in DB
*** Perpetual Valuation - the $ value of the perpetual quantity
**** save to DB as well
** Provide a setting for which price will be used for the Valuation columns: (create new story)
*** Price used for Valuation column will be determined according to the valuation method for the selected item’s valuation group
