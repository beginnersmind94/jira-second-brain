# NXT-21484 — Income Surveys - Surveys grid - History (Changelog)

## Description
*User Story* 

As a survey processor, I would like see a history of changes made to the survey at the time of entering and thereafter if modified, so that I can properly survive an audit of my surveys when required.

*Mock up* 

[https://www.figma.com/proto/EVGaMgaqmoII4zO9Qff1Cq/Eligibility-4.0?page-id=4781%3A59079&node-id=4781%3A61268&viewport=501%2C48%2C0.13&scaling=scale-down-width&starting-point-node-id=4781%3A61268|https://www.figma.com/proto/EVGaMgaqmoII4zO9Qff1Cq/Eligibility-4.0?page-id=4781%3A59079&node-id=4781%3A61268&viewport=501%2C48%2C0.13&scaling=scale-down-width&starting-point-node-id=4781%3A61268|smart-link] 

*Requirements* 

* In Eligibility → Forms → Surveys, in the Details column, next to each survey details icon, display a *history icon*.
** This change needs to be made in both the ‘All’ and ‘Pending Surveys’ tabs
* When this history icon (button) is selected, redirect the user to that individual survey’s history page.
* Survey History shows changes made to a survey after it was received.
** Every change that a user can make to a survey should be tracked. i.e. 
*** Adding/removing students, 
*** changing household size/income, 
*** changing assistance programs and case numbers
*** changing optional benefits responses
*** leaving new comments
*** adding/removing documents
*** And anything else that can be done to a survey, including marking it as pending, processed, or deleted. 
* Include details on the History page about ‘from/to’ values that changed, with the following fields
** Updated On
** Updated By 
** Previous Values
*** \{section/step} - \{currentValue}. i.e. Assistance - 
*** Blank is acceptable if no data exists
** New Values 
*** \{section/step} - \{newValue}. i.e. Assistance - SNAP - 12345678
* For any questionable syntax, please work with the PO.
* History Section should also have pagination

## Acceptance Criteria
Verify that the Survey page’s ‘All’ and ‘Pending Surveys’ tabs Details column includes a clickable History icon next to each survey, which redirects the user to the History page for that survey 

Verify that all changes made to a survey’s information are viewable on this page, with the columns specified above.

Verify that the page includes pagination
