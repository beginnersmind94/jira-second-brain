# NXT-1899 — MP / Find and Replace

## Description
Find / Replace for Ingredients & Recipes would technically fall into Item Management and needs to be addressed in that area.

Find / Replace for menu items on menus would fall into Menu Planning

As a user, I may have a specific item on my menus that gets discontinued by my distributor. I need a simple method of removing the item and replacing it with another of my choosing.

Example:  I am no longer able to get Diced Pears in a can, but I can get Pear Halves in a can. 

I want a page that will allow me to:
(a.) Search for those Diced Pears in a can that I have already put on my menus
(b.) Select a menu item that I will be replacing the original item with (such as the pear halves)
(c.) See a list of my menus in a results panel
   (i.) to include menus assigned to a production calendar with the dates that are assigned (only for present/future dates)
   (ii.)to include menus not yet assigned to a production calendar
(d.) Allow me to select specific menus in those results (with checkboxes, as I may be selecting several menus from the results)
(e.) The ability to click "Apply" and actually replace those menu items with the new one
