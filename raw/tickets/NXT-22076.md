# NXT-22076 — Retrofitting Mapping page-scroll source fields.

## Description
This story is to cover the tasks related to retrofitting the mapping page. 
Currently in case of less number of fields in source, the mapping is easy. However, in cases where there are large number of fields on source, the mapping gets complicated on the mapped and target side. In order to eliminate this issue, the solution is to design/retrofit the mapping field page where the source side can be scrolled so that mapping to the target is easy.

## Acceptance Criteria
# Verify and validate that the retro-fitment does not impact the mapping.
# Verify and validate mappings are preserved even after the source is scrolled.
# Verify and validate mappings are preserved once Verify and validate mappings are preserved once the mapping page is saved.
# Verify and validate that the mappings are saved even after the mapped page is saved and proceed further and then backed again.
