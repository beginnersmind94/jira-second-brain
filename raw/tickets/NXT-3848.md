# NXT-3848 — AcctMgt - Homerooms - View Homeroom Details

## Description
*User Story*

As a district admin I want to see who is in a homeroom and any other details.

*Mockup*

[https://nqyc9v.axshare.com/start.html#p=homeroom&id=spdi2j&c=1|https://nqyc9v.axshare.com/start.html#p=homeroom&id=spdi2j&c=1]

*Requirements*

* User can click on a home room from the split view cards and have the information on that homeroom load on the right side.
* Information to display:
** Homeroom Name
** Homeroom Description (optional field)
** Homeroom Site
** Homeroom Grade(s) (Added 8/20/20)
** Table showing a list of students
*** Columns:
**** Name
**** ID
**** Guardian & Contact Info
***** Info should be pulled from the Primary Guardian
***** Name, Address, Phone #s, Email, etc.
**** Date of Birth
**** Allergens / Restrictions (placeholder column)
***** -Show as chips, if more than 2-3 are tagged then show them as a +X (ie: +5) tag that expands.-
*** Chevron present on each line to expand the information shown.
**** Expanding shows the full contact information, along with the full allergens / restrictions if spilling over.
*** If no students are present, indicate that no students have been added and show the Add button.

## Acceptance Criteria
Homerooms load off the Split View cards.
Name, description, site, grades, list of students available for that homeroom.
Students can be expanded to reveal full information.
Multi-language support added.
