# NXT-2099 — Production - Reports - Post Production Costing (UI)

## Description
The Post Production Costing Report gives the district information regarding the cost of foods in relation to the completed Production Records from their sites. It looks at the quantity of ingredients used to generate the servings, and pulls the costing from the Fair Market Value listed with the products associated to the ingredients. 

Example:  If my Production Record states that I served 23 apples, then the software would calculate the Fair Market Value of those apples, divided by how many broken units contained in the whole unit, and multiply that broken unit cost by those 23 apples.

## Acceptance Criteria
The Post Production Costing Report looks at the quantity of ingredients used to generate the servings, and pulls the costing from the Fair Market Value listed with the products associated to the ingredients.

The district should be able to pull this report with the following details:

1.) Date or Date Range of their choosing

2.) By site or by the full district

3.) By the Meal Type or All Meals (i.e. breakfast, lunch, dinner, special milk, etc.)

And with the following options:

1.) Summary by Site

2.) Summary by District

3.) Details by Site
