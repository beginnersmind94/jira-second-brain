# NXT-31886 — Manage Periods - Enable Open and Close All for Periods

## Description
As Vera the Director I want to close all my POS Periods in 1 action as I have 250 sites and closing them 1 by 1 will drive me insane.



Mockup example for UX guidance:

!image-20220614-124014.png|width=1312,height=694!

## Acceptance Criteria
* As Vera the Director I want to open and close all my POS Periods in 1 action as I have 250 sites and closing them 1 by 1 will drive me insane.
* Only when “All” is chosen from the Site dropdown, both an “Open All” and “Close All” action shall be made available for the end-user
** The tooltip for “Open All” shall be “Open all closed periods for all sites”
** The tooltip for “Close All” shall be “Close all open periods for all sites”
* When an action is executed by a user, a confirmation window shall display asking the user if they would like to continue.  The user shall have the option of continuing or cancelling.
** Open All confirmation text:  Are you sure you would like to open all closed periods?
** Close All confirmation text: Are you sure you would like to close all open periods?
* When an “Open All” action is performed, all closed periods for all sites shall be opened.
* When a “Close All” action is performed, all open periods for all sites shall be closed.
* If there are no periods to open, the “Open All” button shall be disabled
* If there are no periods to close, the “Close All” button shall be disabled
