# NXT-54920 — MP- Menu Planner/Menu Cycle- Change Monday to Sunday to Day 1 to Day 7 and search enhancement

## Description
As a menu planner, I would like for menu planner to show Day 1 through Day 7 instead of showing the days of the week and also a search to show items within a category without having to search a particular item

## Acceptance Criteria
* Change the display to show Day 1 through day Day 7 instead of the days Monday through Sunday for each week
** Apply on Menu Planner, Menu Cycle Standard and any reports that may show days of the week
* On the Menu item search, allow users to search with no characters and show results in alpha order
** User can use categories to further narrow search down so that they can for example look at all vegetables without having to search a particular type of vegetable
** Show items in alpha order
** Display 10 items at a time and display a load more if users would like to continue to browse 
*** This should apply even if the search for an item has more than 10 items only display 10 at a time and load more with a extended scroller for them to see the next 10 items
