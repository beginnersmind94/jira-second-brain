# NXT-16999 — Accountability - Reports - Participation - Non-Participation Version

## Description
*User Story*

As Vera the Director I want to see who is not participating / using their meal benefits so we can encourage them to use them.

*mockup*

*business info*

There is a ‘non-participation report’ version of the participation report which looks for students with no meal sales, and brings up their contact information for outreach / encouragement to use the program:

!image-20201204-001621.png|width=1374,height=418!

*Requirements*

* Add an option to change between the Participation and Non-Participation versions of the report. 
** Non-Participation = student did not have a meal that day.
* Filters/options for the Non-Participation version:
** Almost the same as the Participation version, but the Report Type and Options sections aren’t needed.
** !image-20211102-155244.png|width=1170,height=722!
* The information on the report:
** Headers
*** Report name
*** Generation Date
*** Selected date(s) + site(s)
** Grid
*** Patron ID
*** Patron Name
*** Grade
*** Homeroom
*** Eligibility
*** Reason
*** Non-Participation Days
**** This is new - a count of how many service days (exclude weekends) the patron has not had a meal on.
*** Guardian Name (Primary)
*** Address
*** Phone
*** Email
** Grouping
*** Area
*** Site
* Additional HTML report option
** Export
