# NXT-14270 — Team Work - Employment Status - Unlink from Active/Inactive status

## Description
From Ron:

“Employment Status in Teamwork is the same as Account Status. ie If they have an Active Account their Employment Status is also Active, If their Account is Inactive then their Employment status is Inactive. I feel those two statuses should be separate. You can be an active employee without having an active spending account, or vice-versa.”
