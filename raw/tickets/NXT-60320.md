## Acceptance Criteria
* Create the second Distribution tab: Assignment
* Display the same Distribution Plan header
** update the status chip
* Provide a Distribution Items grid
** Item #
** Item Description
** QOH
*** this may be PQOH or actual QOH depending on settings
** Quantity Ordered (total of all sites)
** Quantity Assigned (total)
** Action - to view site level details
* Emphasize items with insufficient QOH
** Display the QOH and Quantity Assigned values in red
** color the action icon red
* Provide grid options/actions
** Show only QOH errors toggle
*** this will show only the items in the grid with QOH conflicts
** Auto Assign button
*** this will automatically assign all the items with the ordered quantity
**** only for items with 0 assigned quantity
*** this is usually done once in the beginning
** View Options (slideout) 
*** copy from the Physical Inventory page, where Item Category can be toggled as a column or a grouping
*** SVG attached
* Update the sticky footer
** Back to Calendar
** Update messaging to communicate item details
** provide a Save Assignment button
*** this will save progress, but Picking cannot be started until all assignments are within available QOH
