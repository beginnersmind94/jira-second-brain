# NXT-20754 — Acc. - Dynamic Menus - Accept data from Back Office modules

## Description
As an administrator, I want to be able to enable dynamic menus at the site level and not just the district level so that I can use Food detection in only some locations.

The following data is needed from the Back Office modules to populate the menu grid for Dynamic Menus

* Menu Planning
** Selection Criteria
*** Menu Calendar Date 
*** Meal Type (optional)
*** Serving School (optional)
*** Serving Line Id (optional)
** Returned Data
*** Serving Line Id
*** Menu Id
*** Meal Type
*** Minimum Count
*** Serving Size
*** Menu RDA Group Id
* Production
** Selection Criteria
*** Menu Id
** Returned Data
*** Item Id
*** Production Counts
*** Carryovers
* Item Management
** Selection Criteria
*** Item Id
** Returned Data
*** Item Details
**** Item Image (Optional by device type)
*** Contributions
*** Allergen Info
*** POS Info

There is overlap on the availability of data, it is preferable to get the data from the source of the data rather than the secondary source.

If available 2 weeks of dynamic menu data is saved locally.

## Acceptance Criteria
The data needed to display the dynamic menu is stored locally on the device.
