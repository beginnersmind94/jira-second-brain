# NXT-71430 — SC - MT - District - District Boundaries Tab

## Description
As a *Customer Support or Technical Support user*, I want to manage boundaries for a selected district, so that I can configure district-level access while clearly understanding whether boundaries and roles originate locally or are inherited from a parent.

----

h1. Background / Notes

We are introducing a *District Boundaries* tab on the *System > Districts* screen.

This tab allows support users to configure boundaries for a *single district (child realm)*.

Boundaries can originate from:

* *Self (district-level configuration)*
* *Parent (applied from Central Boundaries tab)*

Each boundary includes two independent inheritance controls:

# *Boundary Requirement* (Optional / Mandatory)
# *Role & Permission Requirement* (Optional / Mandatory)

Roles and permissions are treated as a *single unit* and are propagated together.

h3. Key Behavior

* *Mandatory Boundary*
** cannot be disabled at the district level
* *Optional Boundary*
** can be enabled/disabled at the district level
* *Mandatory Role & Permission*
** inherited roles are locked (cannot edit/remove)
* *Optional Role & Permission*
** roles can be modified or replaced locally

----

h1. Current Navigation

*System > Districts > District Boundaries tab*
