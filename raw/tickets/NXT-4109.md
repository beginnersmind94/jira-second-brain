# NXT-4109 — Security - Device Whitelist

## Description
*User Story*
As a PrimeroEdge admin, I have the ability to enable device whitelist and add individual devices that are allowed to access the organization's instance.

*Requirements*
* Device whitelisting can be turned on or off
** If on, only whitelisted devices can access the site
* Devices can be added to the whitelist
** If added, the device can access the site
** Devices that can be added:
*** PCs
*** Macs
*** Apple Phones and Tablets
*** Android Phones and Tablets
* Devices are identified by their MAC address
** Name and IP address should also be present, but MAC address is the differentiator

## Acceptance Criteria
* Device whitelisting can be enabled and disabled through the backend
* Devices are able to be whitelisted through the backend
* Whitelisted devices can access the site when enabled
* Non-whitelisted devices cannot access the site when enabled
