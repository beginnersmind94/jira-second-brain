# NXT-60958 — INV - Distribution - Assign & Pick - Complete Picking

## Description
As a warehouse distribution manager I want a step for Picking within the Distribution, so I can have reports and options for locating items and pulling the necessary quantities.

## Acceptance Criteria
* *Withdraw* items at time of Complete Picking
** at this point the FIFO logic is executed to select batches
** create a Withdrawal transaction associated to the Distribution
*** populate Withdrawal Reason 
**** add new system reason called Distribution
*** add comments 
**** “Withdrawn for Distribution #12345 on 4/14/2025”
** If any insufficient QOH is determined at the time of Complete Picking, refresh the screen to show the red items and disable the Complete Picking button
* Modify the Picking tab appearance once the Distribution is in Picking Complete or Shipping In Progress status
** remove the edit actions from the Picking slideout
** remove the QOH column
** (do not remove Pick Tickets)
* Provide a ‘Restart Picking’ button if no orders have been shipped
