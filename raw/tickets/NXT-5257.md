# NXT-5257 —  Direct Certification - Unmatched - Reviewed - Actions & History

## Description
*user story*

As a district admin I want a way to act on a potential DC match, or to unmatch a previously matched student.

*mockup*

[https://www.figma.com/proto/yrkJeGfd3f9hWjqm0qvfir/Edge2.0_V1?node-id=324052%3A161&scaling=scale-down-width|https://www.figma.com/proto/yrkJeGfd3f9hWjqm0qvfir/Edge2.0_V1?node-id=324052%3A161&scaling=scale-down-width|smart-link] 

*requirements*

* Add actions to the Reviewed page.
** View History
*** Show information about the match, history of it, similar to how we did Extensions.
*** This would also include the scorecard that was used for matching.
** Match / Unmatch
*** Depends on the current status, just like with Extensions. 
*** User needs to enter a comment if they are changing their mind.
*** Unmatch will take that particular potential match and make it available on the the Search & Review tab again.

## Acceptance Criteria
User can reverse their decision.
If the user is reversing an extension, then students should return to their prior eligibility accurately.
