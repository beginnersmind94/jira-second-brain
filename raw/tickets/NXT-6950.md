# NXT-6950 — Application Entry - Processed: Benefit Extension (BE/QA)

## Description
*User Story* 

As an applicant, i can see the benefits are extended to students part of this application upon submission of a _complete_ application for _matched_ students. 

*Mock Ups* 

*Requirements* 

* Application must be in ‘*Processed’* (Complete) state
** *No pending reasons in the section*
* Updating Eligibility/Basis for student already on apps
** *_Student receives the better or latest benefit based on setting > Application - Precedence_* _(Better Status = student retains best eligibility despite more recent applications; Latest Status = student always receives eligibility based on the current application)_ 
*** Examples:
**** Currently Reduced > outcome of submitted app is Free > student’s eligibility changes to Free with the new basis 
**** Currently Free > outcome of app is Reduced > Eligibility remains Free and basis stays the same > application still shows in the app list as processed 
**** Currently Free >  outcome of app is Free but with a different basis > new basis added with the Free eligibility 
** interacts with the Students' eligibility when new app entries are added and/or back dated 
*** I.e. terminating existing and making the processed the active version 
*** Consider the Received Date/Processed (Setting) 
**** Look at current AY price types for student 
**** Start/End Dates for eligibility window should update accordingly 
* Complete applications entered via manual entry extend benefits to matched students included in app
** extension to pending students once they are matched in the system

## Acceptance Criteria
- applicant can submit application  
- submission of a complete application extends benefits to matched students
- Multi-language, ADA compliant.
