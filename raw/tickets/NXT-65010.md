# NXT-65010 — Individual GL Accounts (Proposed #3)

## Description
*As a* district financial manager
*I want to* view and manage my GL accounts in the "GL Accounts" section
*So that* I can keep my chart of accounts organized and ready for mapping

## Acceptance Criteria
* I see one grid showing all my GL accounts with columns: account code, description, base type, account category, and actions
** Sorting on every column
* Tooltip at the top that says “📊 GL Accounts: Manage your general ledger accounts imported from your Chart of Accounts. Individual GL accounts must be linked to Account Categories for proper financial reporting. For each account, you can configure site-specific sub-accounts or use a single account district-wide. Transactions are posted to these accounts, not to categories.”
* Account descriptions are limited to 50 characters
* I can search accounts by code or description
* I can filter accounts by base account type dropdown with options: All Types, Revenue, Expenditures, Assets, Liabilities, Fund Balance or with account categories
* I can click “Add New Account” to manually create an account by entering:
Account Code, Description, Account Category, Base Type (Asset / Liability / Revenue / Expenditure / Fund Balance) (comes from category and is greyed out, blank while no category is assigned)
* I can click "Edit" on any account to update its details
* -I can click "Delete" on any account to remove it-
** -If I try to delete an account with posted transactions, I see a warning message: "This account has posted transactions and cannot be deleted"-
* If any accounts have validation issues during import or after manual creation, I see a persistent banner: "8 accounts need structure corrections" with a count like "42/50 accounts validated"
* Invalid accounts show a warning triangle icon next to their account code
* Once I fix an invalid account, the warning icon disappears and the banner count updates
* When all accounts are valid, the warning banner disappears
* -I can clear filters to see all accounts again-
* -If an account category is deleted elsewhere in the system, accounts that were assigned to that category show a warning triangle icon under the account category column indicating they are orphaned-
