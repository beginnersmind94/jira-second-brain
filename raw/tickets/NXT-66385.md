# NXT-66385 — Reimbursement Posting

## Description
As a *district business administrator*,
I want reimbursement claims and payments from state and federal sources to flow into the general ledger,
So that my program’s revenue and receivables are accurately reflected in financial statements.

## Acceptance Criteria
* *Preconditions for posting*
** All required GL account mappings for reimbursement system accounts are configured:
*** _Due from Federal Funds_ (Asset, DUEFEDF)
*** _Cash in Bank_ (Asset, CASHBNK)
*** _Reimbursement Program System Accounts_ (Revenue, RMBRSMNT)
** Financial period is open from posting date through current date.
** POS reimbursement programs are configured for the district.
* *Posting date rules*
** _Reimbursement Claim_ (GLEntryTypeCd = RMBRSCLM) entries use the last day of the claim period as posting date.
** _Reimbursement Received_ (GLEntryTypeCd = RMBRSRCVD) entries use the current date as posting date.
* *Ledger entries (by site)*
** *Reimbursement Claim*
*** *Debit:* Due from Federal Funds (Asset)
*** *Credit:* Reimbursement Program System Accounts (Revenue)
** *Reimbursement Received*
*** *Debit:* Cash in Bank (Asset)
*** *Credit:* Due from Federal Funds (Asset)
* *By-site creation*
** Ledger entries are created by site for each claim and reimbursement.
* *Visibility and reporting*
** Entries are visible in the General Ledger journal and View Ledger pages.
** Users can drill down to see site-level breakdowns.
** Reimbursement claim and received amounts flow into financial statements as revenue and assets.
* *Rollbacks*
** If a claim is recreated and resubmitted, rollback ledgers are automatically generated before new postings.
** Rollback entries reverse the original postings:
*** For a *Reimbursement Claim* rollback:
**** *Debit:* Reimbursement Program System Accounts (Revenue)
**** *Credit:* Due from Federal Funds (Asset)
*** For a *Reimbursement Received* rollback:
**** *Debit:* Due from Federal Funds (Asset)
**** *Credit:* Cash in Bank (Asset)
** After rollback entries are posted, the new Claim and Reimbursement entries are created using the updated data.
