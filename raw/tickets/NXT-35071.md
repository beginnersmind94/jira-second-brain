# NXT-35071 — PR-pre and post production- Food Component report for Milk (USDA CORRECTIONS)

## Description
USDA has requested that we need to be more clear on how the variety of milk is passing or failing. They have also asked for additional changes on the variety of milk indicator and the reduced fat 2% non allowed milk

* When a variety of milk is offered, instead of marking as pass or fail with the number, we are now going to simple put “served”
* If a particular variety of milk is not served, display blank for that particular type of milk
** In the case that both the Skim/Fat free unflavored and low fat (1% or less unflavored are both not served, display both as “Not served” in red border
** If one of them is served only show “served” on the one that is served and blank on the other
* Varieties of Milk should show pass/fail along with the number of varieties only with no decimals. There will never be decimals in number of varieties
* Rename Unflavored Low Fat or Skim Milk varieties to “Low- Fat (1% or less), Unflavored”
* *If reduced fat (2% Fat) is served display as “served” and in parentheses (Non-Allowed) all in red border to indicate that this type of milk is not allowed to be served (API change Needed)*
** *This does not apply to PK, Adult and Ala Carte* 
** *This only applies to 14+ meal Pattern*
* Remove all weekly totals from varieties of milk and the different types of milk varieties, there is no weekly total or calculation. Only the fluid milk needs a weekly calculation
