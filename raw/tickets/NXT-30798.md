# NXT-30798 — Import - GTIN - Orchestration with Back Office (3/3)

## Description
{color:#ff5630}This story is intended to cover the Platform team’s portion of the import.  The module team will have to complete their pieces for the import to work end-to-end.{color}

{color:#ff5630}UX is not needed.{color}

## Acceptance Criteria
* The Platform orchestration mechanism shall perform the following steps in the order listed:
*# Shall copy the contents of the import file to the Back Office staging table
*# Upon success of the above step, shall call the Back Office “Global Catalog Sync” API/Mechanism
*# Upon success of the above step, shall call the Back Office “GTIN Business Rules” API/Mechanism
*# Upon success of the above step, shall call the Back Office “Item Image Sync” API/Mechanism
*# Upon success of the above step, shall call the Back Office mechanisms that will be used to sync their updated tables/data to their various micro DBs.
*## If there is more than 1 of the Back Office table to be synched, the Platform orchestration mechanism shall call these sync mechanisms in parallel, if possible (to be determined by the Back Office engineering team).
* The Platform team will not be developing any of the mechanisms identified in blue font within the attachment.  These will be the responsibility of the Back Office team. The Platform team will simply orchestrate these APIs/Mechanisms.
