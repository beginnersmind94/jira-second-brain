# NXT-3838 — Goals

## Description
*User Story*
As an Insights user, I can view a list of all previously created goals.

*Mockup*
[https://qvv7ze.axshare.com/#id=z3mm5s&p=goals|https://qvv7ze.axshare.com/#id=z3mm5s&p=goals] (Mockup created before having visibility into existing 2.0 mockups)

*Requirements*

* Goals are separated into 3 distinct categories
** Meal Counts and Participation
** Financial and Inventory Management
** Productivity and Labor
* Goals display multiple points of information
** Name
** Definition
* Goals can be Enabled or Disabled from this screen.
** Enabled Goals will be tracked and displayed on multiple screens.
*** Home
*** Dashboards
*** Reports
*** Notifications
*** Recommendations
* New Goal button
* View/Edit button
** Because of the amount of detail that can go into a Goal, the details must be viewed on a separate page.
