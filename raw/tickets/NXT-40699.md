# NXT-40699 — IM - Recipes - Deactivate Recipe

## Description
As a Menu Planner I want the ability to deactivate a recipe so I can remove recipes we no longer produce from our operations.

!image-20231121-011647.png|width=100%!

## Acceptance Criteria
* Add the item type drawer panel to Recipe Details screen (like items)
* Provide the Active flag for Recipes
* Provide a prompt on click of Deactivate button
** “Do you wish to make this sub-recipe inactive? [Yes, Deactivate], [No, Cancel]
** On click of ‘Yes, Deactivate’ check for all active recipes containing the sub-recipe
* If the sub-recipe is not used on any recipes, move the status directly to *Inactive*
** *Note:* Custom Menu Items will not be considered as an ingredient usage
** [https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-45473|https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-45473|smart-link] 
* If the recipe is used on other active recipes
** move the status into *Pending Deactivation* 
** update the lower section to contain help text “To complete deactivation, remove from active recipes (XX)” where XX is the number of matching recipes
** Provide the ‘View Recipes’ button to open a new tab and navigate the user to the Recipes list page with the ‘Contains Ingredient' filter pre-populated and applied with that sub-recipe
* Use a job to automatically convert ‘Pending Deactivation’ ingredients to ‘Inactive’ status once all recipe assignments are cleared 
* Once the status is Inactive, provide a small button for reactivating
