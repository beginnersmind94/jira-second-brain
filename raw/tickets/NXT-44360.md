# NXT-44360 — Acc/POS - Use Tax for Summary Sales

## Description
As Dora the Director I want to charge tax to adults and need the POS to properly add sales tax based on a percentage.

*requirements*

* Tax charged for Summary Sales for Staff, Visitor, Program Adult if:
** the setting for tax is enabled
** the menu item is marked as taxable
* Sessions updated to show tax amounts where applicable
* User can edit the tax amount if they have the special permission

## Acceptance Criteria
* Tax added as part of Summary Sale if the requirements are met
