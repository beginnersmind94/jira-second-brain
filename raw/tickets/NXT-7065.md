# NXT-7065 — Average Daily Participation (ADP) & ADP Rate (Summary, Chart & Table)

## Description
*Definition :*

_ADP_ : The ADP is the average number of student reimbursable meals served in a school nutrition program on a daily basis.

_ADP Rate_ : The ratio of students eating a school meal to the average number of students attending the school

 

*Objective :  If forecasted,*

·        Allows to determine labor requirements, food-purchasing and non food-purchasing projections

·        Allows to establish participation goals, and to create objectives for meeting those goals.

·        Allows to assess the popularity of menu options, evaluate productivity and guage customer satisfaction.

 

*Frequency to calculate* : Daily, Weekly, Monthly, or as often as needed.

 

*How to calculate:*

·        ADP Breakfast= Number of Breakfasts Served in a Month/Number of Operating Days in a Month

·        ADP Lunch= Number of Lunch Served in a Month/Number of Operating Days in a Month

·        ADP Rate for Breakfast= Breakfast ADP/Avg Daily Attendance

·        ADP Rate for Lunch= Lunch ADP/Avg Daily Attendance

*Need to deduct any students without access to lunch from daily attendance, for example, half-day kindergarten students.

 

*Metrics needed to calculate :*

 

|*Metric*|*Source*|
