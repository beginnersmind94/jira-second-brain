# NXT-49785 — IM - Recipes - Ingredients - Direction Options

## Acceptance Criteria
* Provide a new edit mode within the Recipe > Steps & Ingredients > Ingredients card
** Add a new icon to the right side of the card header
*** SVG (Direction_Options) attached to story
* This will open a special edit mode in the Ingredients card
** enable Cancel/Save buttons on the Ingredient card
** disable edit/action icons from Steps card
* Display a checkbox for each recipe ingredient
** for items, provide an option to *Include Standard Recipe Directions*
*** disabled by default
** for sub-recipes, provide an option to *Print Sub-Recipe Directions*
*** enabled by default
** for ingredients which do not have any referenceable directions, such as CN items, display _Standard Recipe Directions Missing_
*** tooltip for items missing standard recipe directions: This ingredient is missing Standard Recipe Directions.
*** tooltip for sub-recipes without any steps: This recipe is missing Steps & Ingredients
*** SVG (Directions_Missing) attached to story
* In the view mode of the Ingredients card, display the icon next to each ingredient which is selected to be included on the Print Recipe Report
** SVG attached to story (Directions_Yes)
