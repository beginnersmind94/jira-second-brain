# NXT-5203 — Applications - Full List

## Description
*User Story*

As an application processor I want to see all FRE applications that have been entered into the system.

*Mockup*

[https://www.figma.com/proto/yrkJeGfd3f9hWjqm0qvfir/Edge2.0_V1?node-id=87%3A2599&scaling=min-zoom|https://www.figma.com/proto/yrkJeGfd3f9hWjqm0qvfir/Edge2.0_V1?node-id=87%3A2599&scaling=min-zoom]

*Requirements*

* Top Filter
** Program Year (All and other years based on data the district has available)
* Add a table showing information about FRE applications >> {color:#36b37e}_see 2.0 Direct Certification List as reference****_{color}
** Pagination 
*** default 20
*** can increase using the page selection at bottom of grid
** Some fields could be blank if the determination hasnt/cant be made (i.e. Pending applications)
** Table columns can be re-ordered, sorted, and columns hidden/revealed >> 5527
*** Default sort is by Application # 
**** Unique numbers
**** Smallest to Largest
** Table columns (default, all visible):
*** Application #
*** Entry Method
**** Manual, Online, Scanned
*** Eligibility
**** Free, Reduced, Denied, or Blank 
***** The field could be blank if the determination hasnt/cant be made (i.e. Pending applications)
*** Basis
**** Income, \[Source], Refused Benefits, blank
***** The field could be blank if the determination hasnt/cant be made (i.e. Pending applications)
**** Categorical “*Source*”:
***** Assistance Program: SNAP, TANF, FDPIR, etc 
***** Other Source Categorical: Foster, Head Start, Migrant, Homeless, Runaway
**** Multiple will be separated by comma
*** Application Status
**** Submitted, Pending, Processed, Notified, Deleted
*** Received Date
**** Date app was submitted, entered, etc. 
*** Tags (story 6318) > P2
** Additional columns (hidden)
*** Processed Date 
*** Student Name - First Last
*** Student ID
*** Guardian Name -First Last
*** Household Income (Amount + Frequency)
*** Household Size
*** Language
*** Program Year (submitted year)
*** Grade 
*** Site Name 
*** Site Code
