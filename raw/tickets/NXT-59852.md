# NXT-59852 — INV - Distribution - Populate calendar according to Shipping Schedule

## Description
As a Distribution Specialist I want the Distribution calendar to pre-populate the shipping days according to the shipping schedule so our distributions can be visualized.

!Distribution Calendar v3-20250328-002217.png|width=91.66666666666666%,alt="Distribution Calendar v3-20250328-002217.png"!

## Acceptance Criteria
* Use the Shipping Schedule information to split orders into their respective distribution days
* Populate the day on the calendar with the corresponding status and chip
* Insert records on the calendar based on calculated ship dates
** Delivery Date of order + Vendor Lead Time + Production Lead Time (if applicable) + Item Lead Time (if applicable) + internal vendor Shipping Schedule = Ship Date
