# NXT-3803 — AcctMgt - Patron Details - Allergens

## Description
*User Story*

As a district administrator I want to see and interact with any allergies an account has.

*Mockup*

[https://www.figma.com/proto/yrkJeGfd3f9hWjqm0qvfir/Edge2.0_V1?node-id=418732%3A10&scaling=min-zoom|https://www.figma.com/proto/yrkJeGfd3f9hWjqm0qvfir/Edge2.0_V1?node-id=418732%3A10&scaling=min-zoom|smart-link] 

*Requirements*

* Add an Allergen section to Patron Details.
* Section should show the active allergens for this patron as chips. 
** Info: Allergens are split between primary allergens + Gluten (The Big 8 - [https://www.fda.gov/food/buy-store-serve-safe-food/what-you-need-know-about-food-allergies|https://www.fda.gov/food/buy-store-serve-safe-food/what-you-need-know-about-food-allergies]) and any Custom Allergens that have been added by the district.
* Section also needs to show a disclaimer under a ‘Read More’ link.
** Allergen Feature Disclaimer
*** The standard list of allergens available in the “Allergen” feature is taken from the Food Allergen Labeling and Consumer Protection Act of 2004 (FALCPA) plus the addition of gluten. FALCPA allergens include: milk, egg, fish, crustacean shell fish, tree nuts, wheat, peanuts and soybeans.
*** The “Allergen” feature is a tool to help SFAs identify FALCPA allergens and gluten to aid in restricting POS menu item sales.
*** This product does NOT identify any allergens.
*** The effectiveness of this “Allergen” feature is dependent upon the data provided by authorized users within the system.
*** No allergen information within this product should ever be considered a guarantee or recommendation, but simply a best faith effort to serve our customers.
*** This product is not liable for inconsistent, incorrect, or missing allergen information.
*** Custom Allergens may be added for district tracking needs. Custom Allergens may include sensitivities or religious preferences. Additionally, any Custom Allergens are not required by FALCPA to be listed on nutrition fact labels and the district assumes all liabilities for any unmarked or improper allergens.
* Add an Edit button that allows the user to adjust the allergens for the account.
** *Can essentially follow the ‘Add Student’ UI layout for adding allergens.*
** The Big 8 allergens are available to be selected. 
** Already selected allergens are indicated as such. 
** Custom allergens (not the Big 8) are shown separated from the main allergens.
** User can add custom allergens and multi-select from a list.
** Save / cancel buttons.
** History tracking on these changes is a must. Who did it, when, old and new values.

## Acceptance Criteria
