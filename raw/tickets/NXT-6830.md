# NXT-6830 — INV - Vendor - View Vendor Items - Update Contract Additional Attributes

## Description
As a Procurement Specialist I need an update/edit option for items so I can edit certain attributes of my vendor supplied items.

Please reference story and prioritize the story requirements over the mockup, as the mockup is not exact.

*Requirements*

Continued from [Story 6829|https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-6829]

* A side panel that will display additional contract information
** additional attributes
*** Vendor Product Code - Mandatory
*** Purchase Price - Mandatory
**** (SEPARATE/FUTURE STORY) Pricing Conditions - a table containing a list of different prices that are date range based
*** Minimum Order Quantity
*** Maximum Order Quantity
*** Product Lead Time 
*** Charges/Fees
**** Delivery Fee
**** Processing Fee
*** Discounts
**** Net Off Invoice
*** an *Update* button that will save any changes made to the attributes
*** a *Remove* button for removing the item from the Contract
**** prompt before deletion
**** this will set the contract item as inactive 
**** alert the user if the item is on any open orders (future) 

Technical details:

* New APIs required for Update, Delete 
* some UI dependency on components created in Story 7245
