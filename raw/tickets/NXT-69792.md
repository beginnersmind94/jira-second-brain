# NXT-69792 — WS - Insights - KPI - Inventory Turnover Rate - Details Drawer

## Description
The Inventory Turnover Rate slide-out provides a quick snapshot of a school’s food inventory efficiency for a selected date range. It displays overall inventory metrics and categorizes items by movement speed — fast-moving, slow-moving, or zero turnover rate.

*Overview Section*

* Displays the selected school name and analysis period.
** Shows key metrics:
*** Beginning Inventory ($)
*** Purchases ($)
*** Ending Inventory ($)
*** Calculated Turnover Rate
*** Target Turnover Rate
* *Fast-Moving Items Table*
** Lists top 5 items with the highest turnover rates by sorting the turnover rate >0 items  in descending order 
** Displays columns:
*** Item Description
*** Item Category
*** Inventory Turnover Rate
* *Slow-Moving Items Table*
** Lists 5 items with lower but nonzero turnover rates. by sorting the turnover rate >0 items  in ascending order 
** Displays columns:
*** Item Description
*** Item Category
*** Inventory Turnover Rate
* *Items with Zero Turnover Rate Table*
** Lists items that have not been used during the reporting period.
** Displays columns:
*** Item Description
*** Item Category
*** Inventory Turnover Rate (set to 0.00)
* *Metric Explanation Section*
** Includes a descriptive paragraph (“About this metric”) explaining:
** *About this metric*
This metric helps you understand how efficiently your schools are using and replenishing their food inventory. A higher turnover rate means items are being used more quickly, indicating well-aligned purchasing, production, and menu planning. A lower rate may signal overstocking, slow-moving items, or excess waste.
By monitoring this metric, you can identify schools or inventory categories where stock levels are too high or items are not being used as planned. Regularly tracking turnover helps reduce waste, optimize purchasing frequency, and ensure fresh food for students while maintaining budget efficiency.

* *Usability*

* Slide-out should open from the right when a user selects “Inventory Turnover Rate” in the dashboard.
* Data should dynamically reflect the selected date range and school.
