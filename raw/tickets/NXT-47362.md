## Acceptance Criteria
* Once menu items are loaded by the Find action, display the Replace With section
* Until a replacement menu item is selected, disable the Replace button and display the *Remove* button
** once a menu item is selected, hide the Remove button and show enabled Replace button
* If user clicks Remove button, enable the menu item grid for selection (checkboxes)
* Enable Remove icon in sticky footer pending selection of at least one menu item
* Show counter in sticky footer
** ‘Removing from X menus’
* Display Confirm Removal once user clicks remove button as one final check before confirming selections
** Give option “Back to Menu Selection” instead of cancel when on the confirm screen
