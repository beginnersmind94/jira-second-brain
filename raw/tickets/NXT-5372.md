# NXT-5372 — Deposits - Deposit Details - Denominations

## Description
*User Story*

The use can enter denomination counts or an amount for the denomination. 

*Mock-up*

[https://www.figma.com/proto/yrkJeGfd3f9hWjqm0qvfir/Edge2.0_V1?node-id=5834%3A730&scaling=min-zoom|https://www.figma.com/proto/yrkJeGfd3f9hWjqm0qvfir/Edge2.0_V1?node-id=5834%3A730&scaling=min-zoom]

*Requirement*

* Denominations can be entered by amount or count.
* When an amount is entered the corresponding count is calculated.
* When a count is entered the amount is calculated.
* The total cash amount is entered in summary section cash entry of the deposit slip

## Acceptance Criteria
Denominations can be entered by amount or count.
When an amount is entered the corresponding count is calculated.
When a count is entered the amount is calculated.
The total cash amount is entered in summary section cash entry of the deposit slip and added to the Deposit Total
When amounts are entered that result in a partial coin amount the user will be displayed a message "The entered amount results in a incorrect coin count. Please recount and enter the correct amount" The amount is not accepted until corrected. Example Entering $5.20 for Quarters results in 20.8 as the count for quarters.
