# NXT-70001 — ACC - Reports > Revenue Report > Square Integration > New column and option

## Description
As a site manager I would like the ability to see my Square Credit card transactions neatly on the Revenue report. Although I would like to keep it separate from cash seeing it as a break out greatly helps my financial outlook and analysis.

On the Revenue report we will be adding an option to add a credit card column in between the cash and check columns on the totals section.

## Acceptance Criteria
Add Checkbox “Include Credit Cards Breakout” > Adds Credit Card Column after the Cash and Debit Total Columns. (default determined by {color:#4c9aff}system setting{color} Credit Card setting on = Default checked, setting off = unchecked)

When {color:#36b37e}checked{color} show the new column “Credit Card Sale +CC Tax”
Appropriately include the credit card sale data breakout per patron and meal type if not already doing so.
Calculate totals appropriately.

{color:#ff5630}When Not Checked{color}
Credit Card Sales are included in the Additional Income Column.
Add a foot note. “Credit Cards amount, if any, are included in Additional Income Column.”
