# NXT-37068 — IM - Items - Units - Inventory Readiness

## Description
As an Item Management user I want the concept of Inventory Readiness so I can track which items are available to the Inventory module as well as manually control it.

*Requirements*

* Create the concept of Inventory Readiness or inventory publishing
** this will be a more explicit control of which items from IM can become available within the Inventory module
*** right now this is determined by whether or not the Product Info card is populated
** Use Pack Size information to drive the concept of Inventory Readiness
** Inventory Readiness will be displayed as a type of header level status on the Units card

+View Mode+ 

* Maintain the following statuses for Inventory readiness
** Do Not Publish - *manual control*
*** User may have some information, but they are holding the item from being transferrable to Inventory yet
*** UI element TBD [~accountid:5f99ce5044658b0071265de9] 
**** Alert icon becomes checkbox once valid pack size data is added
** Inventory Ineligible - pack sizes are incomplete
*** Pack sizes missing / not added
*** -Pack sizes are added but not linked to- *-one another-* (preventing through UI)
*** Pack sizes exist but are not properly linked to *servings*
** Inventory Incomplete - missing categories, but pack sizes are configured 
** Tiers - when pack sizes are completed, show how many tiers are configured
*** this implies ‘Inventory Complete/Ready'
* Display status in the Units header using an icon or symbol
* Display tooltips for the various statuses explaining what data is required
** Do Not Publish (hold from Inventory)
*** “This item is not Inventory Ready, per manual user control”
** Inventory Ineligible
*** “Pack size units must be configured for Inventory eligibility”
*** “Pack size units must be linked to servings for Inventory eligibility”
** Inventory Incomplete
*** “Item categorizations must be provided for Inventory readiness”
** Inventory Complete*
*** *Note: this status is not explicitly displayed. Instead, display the number of pack size tiers 
**** Ex: {color:#36B37E}*[ 2-TIER ITEM ]*{color} 
*** Tooltip: “This 2-tier item is Inventory ready”
*** Tooltip if Pack Size locked: “This 3-tier item is published to Inventory”
* Leverage the status/tooltips to draw focus to the appropriate corrective actions 
** This is more appropriate for the [Edit/Update story|https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-37452]
