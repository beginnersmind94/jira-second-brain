## Acceptance Criteria
If the period is closed, all sessions in the period are reconciled, and all edit checks resolved the claim can be generated.

SNP
Claim data is generated for each site that participates in the NSLP program and has been set as being a reimbursable site.
There are 3 programs associated with the SNP Claim, School Breakfast Program SMB, National School Lunch Program NSLP, and After School Snack Program ASP
The SBP program is broken into two types of participation, Severe Need and Non-Severe Need
The NSLP program has three types of participation, 60% or More, Less than 60%, and Maximum rate.
The ASP program has two types of participation, Area Eligible Snack, or Non-Area Eligible Snack.
The Approved Number of Free and Reduced students will be included in the claim = (sum of approved eligibility count / # of serving days)
All of the transactions for the period will be analyzed to determine the Reimbursable meal count. A count for each of the above 7 types will be calculated for each site.
A count of all sites participating in each of the 7 types will be calculated.
A count of  the days in operation will be calculated
The Average Daily Attendance will be calculated (total student attendance for the period/days in operation)
Total Reimbursement is the sum of Federal and State rates
Federal Reimbursement Rate will include configured Performance Based Incentive Rate for lunch

SFSP/SSO
There are 5 Summer Feeding programs 
Lunch, Breakfast, Snack(AM), Snack(PM), Supper

There are 2 options for each program
Seamless Summer, Traditional
