# NXT-11023 — Notifications - Automation - Emails (Applications and DC)

## Description
As a district admin I want the system to automatically send email notifications after approvals are complete for applications and direct certification.

*mockup*

n/a

*requirements*

* User has a way to turn on automatic email notifications for FRE Applications.
** If turned on, the system will generate and send a notification to the email of the applicant.
*** Discussion point: do we also send to the primary guardian? They are not always 1 and the same in the case of separated parents. 
* User has a way to turn on automatic email notifications for Direct Certification matching.
** If turned on, the system will generate and send a notification to the email address of the primary guardian.

## Acceptance Criteria
User has the ability to turn on automatic email notifications.
Emails are stored appropriately on the application (if a FRE app) and on the student(s) involved.
The user for history tracking should be 'System Generated' or similar.
Household versions of approval letters should be sent whenever possible to minimize spam.
