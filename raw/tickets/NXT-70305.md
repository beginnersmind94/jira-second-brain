# NXT-70305 — FIN - Automate Prepaid Account Liability Posting via System-Defined Entry Types

## Description
As a Child Nutrition Director I want student prepayment transactions to automatically post to the general ledger split by source (POS vs. Online/3rd Party) So that I can easily reconcile physical bank deposits against merchant processor reports before exporting to the district ERP

*BUSINESS CONTEXT:*

* *Current State:* All prepayment transactions post to a single "Prepaid Liability" account regardless of how the funds were collected, mixing physical cash and digital payments in the same GL bucket.
* *Problem:* District Finance requires these to be separated. The daily cash drawer deposit needs to reconcile independently from the monthly merchant processor settlement.
* *Pain Point:* When they're lumped together, the finance officer cannot match the GL credit to either the bank deposit slip or the processor report without doing the split manually.
* *Solution:* Replace the single "Prepaid Account" configuration data point with two distinct data points — one for POS (physical funds) and one for Online (digital funds) — so that Period Close automatically posts to the correct liability account based on the source field on each transaction.

*SUCCESS METRICS:*

* *Reconciliation Time:* Reduction in time required to match GL credits to bank deposits/settlement reports.
* *Posting Accuracy:* 100% of physical cash deposits match the "POS Prepaid Liability" ledger balance.

----

*Requirements:*

*Mapping Updates*

* Remove the single "Prepaid Account" data point from the account mapping list.
* Add new data point: "POS Prepaid Liability"
** Source: POS Period Close
** Type: Automatic
** Description: Liability account for tracking physical funds accepted at the register.
* Add new data point: "Online Prepaid Liability"
** Source: POS Period Close
** Type: Automatic
** Description: Liability account for tracking digital funds received via 3rd party or web.
* System requires both new data points to be mapped to GL accounts before posting can occur.

*Entry Logic: POS Prepayments (Cash/Check)*

* Identify all positive prepayment transactions originating from POS terminals.
* Credit (Liability): Credit the mapped "POS Prepaid Liability" account.
* Debit (Asset): Debit the mapped "Cash in Bank" account.
* Posting Amount: Equals total cash/check deposits added to student balances at the POS for the period.

*Entry Logic: Online Prepayments (Digital)*

* Identify all positive prepayment transactions originating from Online/Web/App sources.
* Credit (Liability): Credit the mapped "Online Prepaid Liability" account.
* Debit (Asset): Debit the mapped "Cash in Bank" account.
* Posting Amount: Equals total digital deposits added to student balances for the period.

*Entry Logic: Bonus / Adjustments*

* Bonus/Incentive transactions post to the "Bonus Account" (Debit) and the specific Liability account based on where the bonus originated (Credit).
* Refunds/Adjustments reverse the logic based on the original source of the funds (Debit Liability, Credit Cash).

*Entry Description & Format*

* POS Format: POS Prepayments | [Period Date Range]
* Online Format: Online Prepayments | [Period Date Range]
* Example POS: POS Prepayments | 106 - Lincoln Elementary | Jan 2026
* Example Online: Online Prepayments | 106 - Lincoln Elementary | Jan 2026

*Aggregation & Trigger*

* Entries aggregate per site, per period, per source (POS vs Online).
* Post triggering occurs on period close.

*Validation Rules*

* Posting fails if either "POS Prepaid Liability" or "Online Prepaid Liability" is unmapped.
* Total Debits must equal Total Credits for each entry type.

----

*OUT OF SCOPE:*

* Integration with specific bank APIs for auto-reconciliation
* Handling of "Bad Debt" write-offs (separate user story)
* Sales tax liability calculations

*DEPENDENCIES:*

* Existing "Cash in Bank" account mapping
* Core POS and Online Payment transaction data availability

## Acceptance Criteria
*AC1 — Configuration: Replace Single Data Point with Two*

_Given_ a user opens the account mapping page _When_ they view the Customer Accounts subgroup under Liabilities _Then_ the single "Prepaid Account" data point no longer exists and has been replaced by two new data points: *POS Prepaid Liability* and *Online Prepaid Liability* (site *=* {{Enrollment Site or Central Office, per district setting)}}, each with the following attributes:

||Data Point Name||POS Prepaid Liability||Online Prepaid Liability||
|Source|POS Period Close|POS Period Close|
|Type|Automatic|Automatic|
|Description|Liability account for physical funds (Cash/Check) received at the Point of Sale|Liability account for digital funds (Credit/ACH) received via Online or 3rd Party sources|

----

*UPDATED: AC2 — Entry Logic: POS Prepayments (Physical Funds)* 

_Change: Defined Aggregation by Enrollment Site._

* *Given* a POS Period Close is triggered,
* *When* the system identifies prepayment transactions where {{Source = 'POS'}} and {{Amount > 0}},
* *Then* the system retrieves the *Student's Current Enrollment Site* for each transaction.
* *And* generates journal entries grouped by that *Enrollment Site*.
* *Structure per Entry:*
** *Site:* [Student Enrollment Site]
** *Debit:* Cash in Bank (Asset)
** *Credit:* POS Prepaid Liability (Liability)
** *Amount:* Sum of all POS transactions for students enrolled at this specific site.

----

*UPDATED: AC3 — Entry Logic: Online Prepayments (Digital Funds)* 

_Change: Defined Aggregation by Enrollment Site._

* *Given* a POS Period Close is triggered,
* *When* the system identifies prepayment transactions where {{Source = 'Online'}} and {{Amount > 0}},
* *Then* the system retrieves the *Student's Current Enrollment Site* for each transaction.
* *And* generates journal entries grouped by that *Enrollment Site*.
* *Structure per Entry:*
** *Site:* [Student Enrollment Site]
** *Debit:* Cash in Bank (Asset)
** *Credit:* Online Prepaid Liability (Liability)
** *Amount:* Sum of all Online transactions for students enrolled at this specific site.

----

*AC4 — Entry Logic: Bonuses*

_Given_ bonus transactions exist for a site within the current period, _When_ Period Close fires, _Then_ the system aggregates all bonus transactions for that site and period by Source, and generates one entry per Source value according to the following decision table:

||Scenario||Source Field Value||Debit||Credit||
|All bonuses for period originated at POS|{{'POS'}}|Bonus Expense Account|POS Prepaid Liability|
|All bonuses for period originated Online|{{'Online'}}|Bonus Expense Account|Online Prepaid Liability|
|Source is null, blank, or missing on any bonus|{{null}} / {{''}}|Bonus Expense Account|POS Prepaid Liability _(default)_|

*Additional rules:*

* *Posting Amount:* Sum of all bonus transactions for the site and period matching that Source value.
* If the site has POS bonuses and Online bonuses in the same period, two separate aggregated entries are generated — one per Source. *They are never combined into a single entry.*
* *Entry Description:* {{Bonus Prepayments | [Period Month Year]}}
* *Example:* {{Bonus Prepayments POS | Jan 2026}}
* *Example:* {{Bonus Prepayments Online | Jan 2026}}

----

*AC5 — Entry Logic: Refunds*

_Given_ refund transactions exist for a site within the current period, _When_ Period Close fires, _Then_ the system aggregates all refund transactions for that site and period by the Source on the *original prepayment transaction being reversed*, and generates one entry per Source value according to the following decision table:

||Scenario||Source on Original Prepayment||Debit||Credit||
|All refunds for period traced back to POS originals|{{'POS'}}|POS Prepaid Liability|Cash in Bank (Asset)|
|All refunds for period trace back to Online originals|{{'Online'}}|Online Prepaid Liability|Cash in Bank (Asset)|
|Original Source is null or missing|{{null}} / {{''}}|POS Prepaid Liability _(default)_|Cash in Bank (Asset)|

*Posting Amount:* Sum of all refund transactions for the site and period matching that Source group.

*Entry Description:* {{Refunds at Source | [Period Month Year]}}

*Example:* {{Refunds at POS | Jan 2026}}

*Example:* {{Refunds at Online Sources | Jan 2026}}

----

*UPDATED: AC6 — Aggregation Rules* _Change: Clarified the "Site" definition._

* *Given* Period Close is triggered,
* *Then* prepayment entries must aggregate at the following granularity: one entry per *configured site level* (Enrollment Site or Central Office, per district setting), per Period, per Source

----

*AC7 — Balance Validation*

_Given_ an entry is generated, _When_ the system calculates debits and credits, _Then_ Total Debits must equal Total Credits for each individual entry.
