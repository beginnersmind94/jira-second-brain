# NXT-61886 — Planner AI - Menu Item Participation Score (District/Site)

## Description
As a district or site level admin, I would like to have a dashboard that allows me to see information about my menu item participation to gauge popularity.

## Acceptance Criteria
* Provide a District view of Menu Item participation with the best 5 performing and worst 5 performing menu items in a chart grid to allow for quick overview of best and worst performing
** Provide a site selection to narrow down this information by site
* Provide a filter to allow users to look at YTD, Academic Year, Last Month, Last Week
* Provide a filter to allow users to choose Menu item Category
* *Site* calculation for Item Participation is calculated by capturing the 
** *Served Ratio =* Served Count/Produced Count (Each Menu Item Serving Instance)
** *Normalized Served Ratio = (*Served Ratio-Minimum *Site* Served Ratio)/(Max *Site* Served Ratio- Minimum *Site* Served Ratio)
** *Average of Normalized Served Ratio =* Add ALL Normalized Served Ratios for a Particular Menu Item & divide by the number of instances with in the site
* To calculate the *Site* Participation Score for EACH menu item
** *Weighted Score* = Use *Enrollment* count and multiply against the *Average Normalized Served Ratio* 
*** If enrollment count is not available, use Served Meal Count
** *Site Participation Score* = Weighted Score/Enrollment*100
* *District* calculation for Item Participation is calculated by capturing the 
** *Served Ratio =* Served Count/Produced Count (Each Menu Item Serving Instance)
** *Normalized Served Ratio = (*Served Ratio-Minimum *ALL* Served Ratio)/(Max *ALL* Served Ratio- Minimum *ALL* Served Ratio)
** *Average of Normalized Served Ratio =* Add ALL Normalized Served Ratios for a Particular Menu Item & divide by the number of instances with in the district
* To calculate the *District* Participation Score for EACH menu item
** *Weighted Score* = Use *Enrollment* count and multiply against the *Average Normalized Served Ratio* 
*** If enrollment count is not available, use Served Meal Count
** *District Participation Score* = All Weighted Scores X All Enrollment
