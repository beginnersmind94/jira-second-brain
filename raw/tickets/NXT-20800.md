# NXT-20800 — INV - Withdrawals - Withdrawal Details - History tab

## Description
As a Site Manager I want a history on the Withdrawal Details so I can view the changes made to the transaction.

*Requirements*

* Add a History tab to the Withdrawal Details
* Provide the History grid on the tab
** Transaction #
** Transaction Details
*** system-provided description of transaction update
** Modified Date
** Modified By (user)
** Comments
** Action
*** to open the historical Withdrawal Details 
* List of Transaction Details events:
** Original Transaction (created)
** Withdrawal Adjusted (edited)
*** change in header fields, OR
*** change in quantity
** Withdrawal Cancelled
* This History tab could be controlled by permission
