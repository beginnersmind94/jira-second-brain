# NXT-65082 — PR - Pre and Post Production Analysis - Update and Combining Fruits & Veg for Daily and Weekly (SBP 14+)

## Description
Based on new requirements, analysis for breakfast should be combining the Fruit and Veg values when making sure a district is compliant with USDA standards.

## Acceptance Criteria
* On the *Pre and Post Production Analysis*, update the Component of Fruits to Fruits or Vegetables (cups) and Fruit Juice to Fruit or Vegetable Juice
* Slide-out
* Nutrition Report
* Food Component Report
** No longer display Vegetable or any of the subgroups as there are no standards for them
* Calculations for this will be a combination of both components together (Use same logic as before but combine the calculations) as the same for juice accounting for no more than half the offerings 
** Per USDA, “Schools must offer 1 cup of fruit daily and 5 cups of fruit weekly. Schools may substitute vegetables for fruit at breakfast”
