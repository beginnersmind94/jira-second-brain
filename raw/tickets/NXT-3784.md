# NXT-3784 — AcctMgt - Add Patrons - Add New Student - Contact Information

## Description
*User Story*

As a district admin I want to add essential information to a patron while creating them.

*Mockup*

[https://nqyc9v.axshare.com/start.html#p=category_based_on_patron_view&id=jytwv5&g=14|https://nqyc9v.axshare.com/start.html#p=category_based_on_patron_view&id=jytwv5&g=14]

*Requirements*

* Add the Primary Contact Information section to the Add Student drawer per the wireframes.
* Fields to add:
** Contact Title (Mr/Mrs/Ms/Dr)
** Primary Contact First Name
** Primary Contact Middle Name (optional)
** Primary Contact Last Name
** Contact Suffix (text field, optional)
** Relationship *
** Street Address
** Apt./Suite #
** City
*** This is a text field, not a dropdown. We don’t want to populate a giant list of cities. 
** State (dropdown)
*** Can we default to the State the district is in, but still allow users to select elsewhere as required?
** Zip
** Additional +4 digits for Zip
** Home Phone Number (optional)
** Work Phone Number (optional)
** Cell Phone Number (optional)
** Preferred Contact Method (dropdown)
** Email Address

** Address Line 1
** Address Line 2

** City
** State
** ZIP
*** Always 5 digits.
*** Example: 77377
** Additional +4 digits for ZIP
*** Example: 8000
*** Full ZIP would be 77377-8000
** Preferred Contact Method *
** Home Phone Number
** Work Phone Number
** Cell Phone Number
** Email * 
** Primary or Secondary Contact
*** Only 1 Primary contact can exist.
** Created Date

** Update Date
