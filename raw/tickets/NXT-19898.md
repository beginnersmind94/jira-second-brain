# NXT-19898 — IM - Items / Recipe Consolidation (MAIN)

## Description
As a Nutritionist, I would like to be able add an items to my Items  quickly (no more 2 min) by simply submitting my answers to key questions.

* Items can either be Food or Non Food 

* Food Items can take the form of:
** Single Item Recipe
** Menu Item (If item is single item  recipe)
** POS Menu Item (If item is single item  recipe)

* The user will have to answer these questions with a YES or NO answer.
** Is the item: 
*** Food Item
*** Is it a Single Item Recipe
*** Is it a POS Menu Item
*** Is it a Menu Item (Only ask question if single item recipe is YES)
