# NXT-46163 — Onboarding - User - Add onboarding experience for all new users (Part 2)

## Description
As a Cybersoft or district admin, I would like for users to be able to have an onboarding experience of their own which helps them easily transition to using SchoolCafé in a personalized way, so that is easier for them to use right away and results in fewer support tickets as a result. 

*Summary*

Onboarding of individual users to help them get setup within their profile (details and preferences), workspace, understanding overall usability (take a look around), and then reviewing their role/responsibility assigned to them (central office, nutritionist, site manager, etc.). This requires two changes.



*Part 2:*

*Users who can log in and immediately begin using SC (meaning onboarding is complete):* Display a series of new shepherd tours explaining navigation shifts (for multi-district users, how to change district. For all users, how to change their preferences, how to access chat/help, etc.). Each will highlight a different portion of the page:

!image-20240103-212806.png|width=100%!

*Details*

* Step 1:
** Title: *Tip 1 of 3: Create & view personal tasks or messages*
** Body: Your Workspace allows you to view and send messages to other SchoolCafé users in your district, use Tasks to set goals for yourself or others, and more. 
** Footer: *Next* ({color:#97a0af}*Gray* {color}button)
* Step 2: 
** Title: *Tip 2 of 3: View helpful guides, change preferences*
** Body: To find contextual help guides, select the '?' button. To view or change your profile details, select the button on the right.
** Footer: *Next* ({color:#97a0af}*Gray* {color}button)
* Step 3: 
** Title: *Tip 3 of 3: Quickly navigate between pages*
** Body: Finally, if you’re unsure where to find a particular page in the navigation, use the Search field to quickly find what you’re looking for. Thanks for using SchoolCafé!
** Footer: *Finish Tour* ({color:#36b37e}*Green* {color}button)
* Users who click Skip should never have the tips appear again, and should also have the the tips show up as a completed tour. 
* Texts should also be editable in the Content Management screen.
