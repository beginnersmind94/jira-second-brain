# NXT-18083 — INV - Orders - Manage Orders - Receipts - Quick Receipt - After Confirm (UI)

## Description
As a site manager I want the ability to save or complete a Quick Receipt so I can either received the product into my inventory or save the Quick Receipt for later.

This story is for what happens to the UI after user Confirms a Quick Receipt ([NXT-18080|https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-18080])

*Requirements*

* After user clicks the Confirm button on a Quick Receipt
** Display success message
*** Confirm: “This Quick Receipt is closed and the items have been received with corresponding Order # 12345.”
** Keep user on this screen
** Remove all buttons
** Provide a new button ‘Return to Receipt List’ 
*** this will navigate to the Receipts tab of the Manage Orders page
** Change screen to view-only mode
*** all fields are saved as labels / not editable

## Acceptance Criteria
* On click of Confirm button:
** Success message will be displayed
** Buttons will be removed
** Editable fields will turn to labels
** ‘Return to Receipt List’ button will be provided
