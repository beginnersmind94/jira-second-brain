# NXT-6083 — Application Entry - Guided Process (7. Submit)

## Description
*User Story* 

As an applicant, the last step of the wizard allows me to enter an SSN if i have one, and electronically sign the application.

*Mock Ups* 

[https://www.figma.com/proto/yrkJeGfd3f9hWjqm0qvfir/Edge2.0_V1?node-id=1306%3A27072&scaling=min-zoom|https://www.figma.com/proto/yrkJeGfd3f9hWjqm0qvfir/Edge2.0_V1?node-id=1306%3A27072&scaling=min-zoom]

*Requirements* 

* ‘Last 4 Digits of SSN’ field > must enter 4 digits > “_invalid SSN_” warning/validation if not
** "Applicant has no SSN" checkbox
** (Either they can can enter 4 digits or check this box - one or the other)
*** fields are dependent
* "Application signature confirmed" (checkbox)
** default not checked
* Application Language 
** Drop down selection for configured languages for the district (system module)
*** default: English
* Documents Section
** {color:#ff5630}The document upload tool will be part of Phase 2 (6996){color}
* Application Received Date 
** Received Date > Date selector > can go as far back as the start of the program year 
** Default: Today’s date
* *Pending Reasons section will be hidden until Phase 2* 

* Eligibility Determination panel 
** ‘Eligibility’ designation
*** Free
*** Reduced 
*** Denied 
**** ‘Paid’ will only show for the applicant info, Denied is the terminology for applications 
** ‘Basis’ Determination
*** Income
*** Categorical: \[Source]
**** Assistance Programs 
***** (Configuration for local name) - SNAP, TANF, FDPIR
**** Other Source 
***** Homeless
***** Migrant
***** Head Start
***** Runaway 
***** Foster
*** Refused Benefits
** ‘Start Date’ of eligibility 

* Applicant can go to previous page or Submit 
** Submit enabled when fields are complete 
* Shows History tracking in Application Details 
** timeline piece (Application Details) 
** comments

## Acceptance Criteria
- applicant can submit application  
- If any reasons exist, the application is tagged as pending because it needs attention
- applicant has option to go to previous step 
- applicant can enter SSN, upload optional image and sign
- submit info includes sections listed above
- Multi-language, ADA compliant.
