# NXT-37982 — IM - Items - Units - Edit/Update - Pack-Serving link help step

## Description
As an Item Management user I want the Inventory Ready status to guide me to specific corrective actions so I can have guidance while configuring my packs and servings.

*Unit card edit scenarios*

* Inventory Readiness (in edit mode)
** This story continues from [https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-37452|https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-37452|smart-link] 
** make the icon expandable into a banner which provides the corrective action for the status
*** Inventory Ineligible examples:
**** Pack Sizes not present - “Pack size units must be configured for Inventory eligibility”
***** clickable action could emphasize the +Pack button
**** Pack Sizes not linked to serving - “Pack size units must be linked to servings for Inventory eligibility”
***** clickable action could emphasize the linking fields (Sub-Qty or Weight)
***** Ex: “Please enter weight for [base unit] or sub-quantity for [inventory-able serving size]”
* +Example Units and Scenarios+
** Pack sizes: Case, x6 Pack (Pack is _current_ Base Unit)
* Scenario 1 (existing business rules)
** Serving size: 8 nuggets (185 grams)
** “Please enter weight of the Pack”
* Scenario 2 (user has option but still chooses weight)
** Serving size: 1 Apple (Weight: 85 grams)
** “Please enter weight of the Pack or sub-quantity for Apple”
*** Show the user entering weight of the Pack (as 3 Pounds) (Scenario 1a)
* Scenario 3 (same as Scenario 2, except user chooses Sub-Qty)
** Serving size: 1 Apple (Weight: 85 grams)
** “Please enter weight of the Pack or sub-quantity for Apple”
*** Show the user entering Sub-Qty for the Apple
*** do not show any weights on any of the pack sizes 
* Scenario 4 (multiple serving sizes with '1' as Amount, aka “potentially inventory-able”)
** Serving Sizes: 1 Slice, 1 Cup
** “Please enter weight of the Pack or sub-quantity for Slice or Cup”
** As soon as user types in one field, disable the other fields

Notes

* +*Scenarios related to “linking”*+
** 1.) Base unit linked to serving via weight (current)
*** serving size amount != 1
** 2.) Base unit linked to serving via weight (choice between old and new options)
*** serving size amount = 1
** 3.) base unit linked to serving via sub-qty
*** serving size amount = 1
*** Note: from this scenario, Dual becomes _possible_ (out of scope)
** 4.) pack sizes only
** 5.) serving sizes only
