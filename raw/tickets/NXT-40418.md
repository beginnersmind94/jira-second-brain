## Acceptance Criteria
* Strict Batching needs to move to Serving row level
** only enabled after Serving Size and # of Servings are provided
* Step 0 should be loaded in edit mode on the first time Steps is edited
** Need to do this on SIR as well
** don't show Save unless there's something to save
* Need to flag and deprioritize CN items on Ingredient search
** show CN icon next to CN items
** always display CN items after local items and recipes
* In the Step Ingredient list, all local measures should be displayed before weight/volume measures in the serving size dropdown
* for all measures we still need to show the weight or volume
** Weight example: 25 Cups (1182 grams)
** Volume example: 25 Cups (24 Fl Oz)
*** another example: 1/2 Cup (118 ml)
* Need tooltips on all icons
* (Enhancement) Add the Recipe Report to the SIR Summary card More Actions
