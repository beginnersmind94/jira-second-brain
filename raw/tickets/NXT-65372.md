# NXT-65372 — MP- Menu Planner- CACFP - Use MMA as Grain (Breakfast)

## Description
As a USDA requirement for CACFP. districts are able to use MMA as grain for breakfast up to 3 times a week. Currently, our analysis does not check against this or provide the correct calculations for the substitutions.

## Acceptance Criteria
* On the *Daily  and weekly Analysis*, if user toggles MMA to use as grain, consider the component amount towards the grain calculation and not the MMA, currently the analysis is considering it as part of the MMA calculation
** Daily Check should be a pass/fail based on if the menu cycle has passed every daily standard for the week
*** Any daily fail should trigger the overall fail for required components
* Districts are only allowed to use MMA as a grain up to 3 times a week
** Provide a *weekly check* on the M/MMA acting as a grain to ensure users can see if they are using MMA as a grain more than 3 times a week
*** This applies to the overall pass or fail fly out and provide reason under MMA, “MMA being used as grain is exceeding 3 times a week”
**** This will also fail the entire analysis
* Grain Based Desserts may not be used to meet the grains requirement but may be offered in additional to a grain or MMA acting as a grain
