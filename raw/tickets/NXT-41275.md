## Acceptance Criteria
* Remove the grid based approach in favor of a simple label statement for each applicable Allergen Form
** in every applicable screen: Item Details, Recipe Details, Menu Item Details, POS-Only Details
** Make the card a reusable component so it can be updated in one place in the future
* Prioritize allergen forms in the following order
** ‘Contains’ form
** ‘Processed….’ form
** ‘May Contain’ form
** ‘Free From’ 
* List each allergen after the appropriate allergen form label
** put each allergen in bold
** show custom allergens after standard allergens
** sorta alphabetically ascending within each row
** For ‘Free From’ put the label in bold and the allergens in regular font
* Use icon for each appropriate status
** octagon alert with red for Contains
** circle alert with orange for May Contains or Processed
** green “not applicable” icon for Free From
* Move the Disclaimer to the more actions menu option
** it will still open the slideout
