# NXT-26925 — INV - Apply Period check to transactions - Additions & Withdrawals

## Description
As an Inventory user I want transactional checks for Period Status so the system can alert me if I’m trying to make adjustments to transactions in a closed period.

*Requirements*

* Enforce period rules on Addition and Withdrawal
** whenever a period is closed, all transactions should be dated _after_ the end date of the most recently closed period
*** ex: August 2021 is closed, and is the most recent closed period. Any further receipts should be dated in September or later
*** ex: August 2021 is *Open*, but September 2021 is closed. Still, all transactions should be dated October or later
* Transaction List:
** Creating Addition or Withdrawal
*** Message: “The Period for the selected date has been Closed. Please select a valid date.”
** Editing Addition or Withdrawal
*** Message: “The Period for this Addition/Withdrawal date has been Closed. This transaction cannot be edited.”
** Cancelling Addition or Withdrawal
*** Message: “The Period for this Addition/Withdrawal date has been Closed. This transaction cannot be cancelled.”
** *Old stories for reference:
*** Physical Inventory transactions were done as part of [https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-19330|https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-19330|smart-link] 
*** Receipts & Transfers: [https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-19328|https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-19328|smart-link]

## Acceptance Criteria
* Apply period check to Addition transactions
* Apply period check to Withdrawal transactions
* Prevent CRUD operations if the period is closed
