# NXT-11337 — SchoolCafe Integration - Real-time purchase restrictions/limitations synchronization

## Description
* Purchase limitations/restriction information made in SchoolCafe shall be kept synchronized with Perseus in real-time.

## Acceptance Criteria
Changes to purchase limitations/restriction information in SchoolCafe shall be synchronized with Perseus within 5 seconds.

If SchoolCafe or Perseus are unavailable to be synchronized for a period of time, there shall be a mechanism to synchronize them once the 2 systems are available.

This mechanism shall be performant and scalable.
