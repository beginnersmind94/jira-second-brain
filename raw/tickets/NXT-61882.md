# NXT-61882 — INV - Distribution - Resources & Permissions

## Description
As a District Administrator I want Permissions for the Distribution functionality so I can limit access to only the correct site personnel.

## Acceptance Criteria
Update the Permissions page with the updated resources and new permission hierarchy for the *Distribution* page and sub permissions.

* Regardless of permission, users should only see the Distribution page in the navigation if they are associated with the site that is linked to the internal vendor

h4. Permission Structure:

* +Distribution Calendar+ - Page (view only)
** +Distribution Details+ - Subpage - *View, Edit*
*** *View*: includes all reports
*** *Edit*: just edit icons and action buttons
*** +Modify Shipments+ - Action (for Modify Shipments button/slide-out)
*** +Revert Distribution Status+ - Action (for all revert/rollback functionality – the red buttons – Unassign, Reset Assignment, Restart Picking, Restart Shipping)

h4. Resources:

* +Distribution Calendar+ - Page
** +Distribution Details+ - Subpage
*** +-Review-+ -- Tab-
*** +-Assign & Pick-+ -- Tab-
*** +-Shipping-+ -- Tab-
*** +Modify Shipments+ - Action 
*** +Revert Distribution Status+ - Action
* _any other resources needed to support permissions_
