# NXT-10260 — Template Integration - Sending - Email Queue Management

## Description
As a district admin I want to see what emails are bound to be sent out automatically at a specified time, and interact with the queue.

*mockup*

tbd

*requirements*

* Emails out of 2.0 should be configurable to all be sent at a particular time, or at least a delay. 
** Some modules may use this, some may not - likely need that level of configuration.
** Eligibility, for instance, may be setup to work on a delay so that customers can correct mistakes.
* Customers need visibility to see what’s:
** Already been sent.
** Currently in the email queue.
* Customer needs to see:
** Destination (Address, Name if possible)
** Timestamps
** *Read receipt*
** Origin
*** What user sent the email, when?
** Template Used
** Email Subject Line
* For the outbound queue, customer needs the option to pull an email before it is sent.
** Delete option with audit trail on the action.
* Role permissions will be needed for access to view/interact with this.
