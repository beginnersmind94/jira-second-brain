## Acceptance Criteria
* Show the transferred batches from Distribution on the Site Receipt
** using the Date on Product slide-out/-sub-grid-
*** always use slide-out
* Disable Edit/Add/Delete functions in the slide-out
** sites cannot modify transferred batches or delete records
** this is always true for internal vendors
* Replace sub-grid with slide-out for external vendors as well
** for external vendors, enable Add/Edit/Delete batch functionality only when in Edit Receipt mode
