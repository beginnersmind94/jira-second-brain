# NXT-62271 — INV - Distribution - Calendar Cleanup

## Description
As a Distribution Site Manager I want the calendar to provide dynamic label info so I can get the most accurate info from the calendar without opening individual distributions.



!Distribution Calendar final maybe-20250529-195537.png|width=91.66666666666666%,alt="Distribution Calendar final maybe-20250529-195537.png"!

## Acceptance Criteria
* Change “deliveries” to “orders”
** display in Medium font, to differentiate from the status text
*** after Assignment (Regular font for Unassigned)
** display singular vs. plural
*** order vs. orders
* Display the New Orders warning icon next to the # of orders label
** display as number of new orders instead of “New Orders” label
** ex: *4 orders* (!) _+1 new_
* Display routes & stops for distributions in Shipping stage
** X routes (X stops)
** ex: 3 routes (17 stops)
** display in Medium font, to differentiate from the status text
** display singular vs. plural 
*** route vs routes
*** stop vs. stops
* Display a shorter chip for Closed distributions
** just show the number of orders in Light Italic font
** do not display ‘Closed’ status like other chips
* If multiple distributions exist for a day, display by status descending
** Display later status distributions above earlier status
** example:
**# Shipping
**# Picking
**# Unassigned 
** Except for Closed, which should always display at the bottom
