## Acceptance Criteria
Currently, the GTIN fields are shown as change even if there is single digit reflected. 

As a change, show them as updates only if the target GTIN is greater than 8 digits

If the GTIN length is less than 8 digits - do not list them as update (basically don't show them updates dialog)

Ex: JeffCo Data in Online 2 UAT 

Scope: Ingredient Tab (GTIN Base Unit) and Stock Item Tab (GTIN Purchase Unit)
