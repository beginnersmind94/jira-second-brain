# NXT-16098 — Multi District User Access

## Description
When a user has access to multiple districts, they can switch districts but no clear indication on the screen what district they are in. They only know by clicking on their profile picture.

{color:#ff5630}See attachment for video showing the issue.{color}

## Acceptance Criteria
* As a user, I would like to easily identify which district I currently have set as the context under which I am working without having to click on and open my profile.
