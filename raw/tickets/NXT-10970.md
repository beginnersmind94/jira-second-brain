# NXT-10970 — Dashboard (Insights) - Accountability - Liability (Unpaid Balance) Widget

## Description
As a central office user I want to see the total liability dollar amount of unpaid balances for my district so that I can see if I’m within my planned budget for the year and can plan to manage my charge limits if needed.

* Total Amount ($)
* If possible, show the amount per month of the SY (show trends)
* Top 5 sites of highest amount (used for management)
* In release 3, we can provide a quick setup/link to the debt collection campaign

Note: This widget may be repeated on the Account Management module dashboard.
