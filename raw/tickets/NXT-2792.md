# NXT-2792 — View Inspections - Actions

## Description
*User Story*
As an Inspections user, I have actions I can take on individual Inspections on the View Inspections screen based on my permissions.

*Mockup*
[https://m5uloa.axshare.com/#id=c7gcdx&p=inspections&view=default&g=1|https://m5uloa.axshare.com/#id=c7gcdx&p=inspections&view=default&g=1]

*Requirements*

* Actions are accessed through the menu button on individual Inspections
* Actions
** View Inspection - Opens Inspection
** Activity - Shows a list of actions that have been taken on the Inspection in a pop up window
** Start Inspection - Puts Inspection into In Progress and allows questions to be answered
*** Can only be done when Inspection is in Scheduled or Re-Inspection status
*** Can only be done by the assigned Inspector
** Reschedule - Change the Inspection Date
*** Requires Role Permission
** Reassign - Allows Inspection to be assigned to a different user
*** Can only be done when Inspection is in Scheduled, In Progress, or Re-Inspection status
** Approve - Approves and archives Inspection
*** Requires Role Permission
*** Only available when Inspection is in Pending Approval status
** Re-Inspection - Sends Inspection back to Inspector to be redone
*** Requires Role Permission
*** Only available when Inspection is in Pending Approval status
** Send Email - Allows user to email the inspection results to any email groups or email addresses
** Export PDF - Creates a PDF of the Inspection
** Export Excel - Creates a XSLX file of the Inspection
** Parent Inspection - Shows the prior Inspection results for Re-Inspection
*** Available once an Inspection has been submitted, sent for Re-Inspection, and resubmitted
** Delete - Deletes the Inspection
*** Requires Role Permission
*** Cannot be done once Inspection has entered Pending Approval Stage or beyond
*** Pop up asking user to provide a reason (short text) and confirm yes or no.
**** “Warning: This action cannot be undone. Are you sure you want to delete this inspection?”

## Acceptance Criteria
* Actions are present and can be selected in the View Inspections screen
* All actions except for reporting and email are functioning
