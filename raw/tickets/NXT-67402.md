# NXT-67402 — LMA - License Keys - Limit Characters in Notes Fields

## Description
As a Customer Support (CS) role member, I would like to limit the amount of characters in the notes field so that I don’t see too much text.

*Requirements:*

* Everywhere we have the Notes fields in a drawer, do the following:
** Put a limit of max characters to 2000
** Add a text next to it like n / 2000 that shows how many characters they have typed
* 
* At a minimum, put it on these screens:
** Generate License Drawer
** Edit License Drawer
** Any of the other drawers that have the same field

## Acceptance Criteria
* Users cannot enter more than 2000 characters on any notes field
