# NXT-9093 — [TaggedReport] - Accountability - Reports - Activity Report

## Description
*User Story*

As a district admin I want to see live information on meal counts and sale figures to keep an eye on the operation for a single site, or the entire district. I also want to use this report to have confidence in the reimbursement claim that the system is generating for me.

*Business info*

How is the user using this report / why

* typically used to check if sales/transactions are happening at sites
** they check if meal counts or a la carte figures are incrementing
* check credits vs debits for cross-comparison with revenue reports
* used as a way to cross-check their meal counts with the Edit Check report, and then against their Reimbursement reports.
** meal counts are submitted to the state - they are given back $$ for the number of meals sold so their operation can break-even.

How frequently should the data be updated

* this should be a live report, or at least <5 mins for data to refresh.

*Requirements*

*Inputs*

* Site(s)
** What site(s) do they want to see data for?
** Multi-select should be an option here.
** A summary for the entire district should be an option also.
* Date ranges
** Single date, date range, by period / monthly.
*** Periods can be configured by the user; it's either weekly or by calendar month.
* Program
** Single selection from the below programs.
** Multiple service programs are available to users.
*** We would only want to show them programs that they are configured for.
** Programs: SNP, CACFP, SFSP
* Meal type(s)
** Multi-select.
** Multiple meals are available under each program.
** We'd only want to show them the meals they've configured, not every meal type.
*** SNP: Breakfast, Lunch, Snack
*** CACFP: Breakfast, Lunch, Supper, AM Snack, PM Snack, Evening Snack
*** SFSP: Breakfast, Lunch, Snack, Supper
** N.B: A SNP Breakfast is not the same as a CACFP Breakfast.

*Main information to collate / pivot on:*

* Meal Counts
** Total number sold.
* Sale Figures ($$)
** Total $$ for the following:
*** Meals
*** A la carte items
*** Tax
*** Cash vs Debit
* Reimbursement Estimate (Meal Counts * Reimbursement Rates)
** Reimbursable meal types are for students only.

*Information is broken down / nested / pivoted with multiple levels:*

* Region / District
** Site
*** Program (SNP / CACFP / SFSP / SSO)
**** Meal Type (mentioned above)
***** Price Type (Student vs Non-Student)

*Totals*

* Sub-totals are available for each of the categories above.
** ie: Subtotal by price type (student vs non-student), subtotal by meal type.
* Grand totals are available for the pivot of data above.
* Summary information is also available 'at a glance' vs having to check the big pivot table.
** Total payments done, broken down by cash or online.
** Total reimbursement (this is otherwise broken out by meal type in the pivot table)
** Total sales, cash vs debit, tax.
** End of Day figures from POS terminals vs Deposit Slips
*** Expected cash (calculated cash)
*** Actual cash (closing balance)
*** Deposited amount (from deposit slips)
*** Variances as a result of these figures
**** Expected vs Actual
**** Actual vs Deposited
