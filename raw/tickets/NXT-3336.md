# NXT-3336 — Employee Dashboard - Compliance Score UI

## Description
The compliance score % and icon show the user the employee's compliance at a glance. 

* Add the compliance indicator to grid and card view 
* the CEU hours determine compliance percentage 
* the percentage determines the indicator display 
* Not Started (0 hours complete), In Progress (1+ hours complete), In Compliance (All required CEU hours complete)

FYI - consider hire date bc only have the hours are needed for employees hired on or after Jan 1

## Acceptance Criteria
The compliance score is accurate - indicator, percentage, and ratio
considers hire date when determining required hours 
hours are based on CEU level
