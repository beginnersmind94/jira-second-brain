*Information:*

_Household Notifications (Letters)_ are utilized to accompany applications distributed to households. These letters are typically known as “letters to households.” The format for each Household notification includes a list of the student(s) in the household as per the most recent household grouping information. There are various circumstances where these letters are generated separately for each student. These scenarios are as follows: when it is the District’s first year with our service, when there are no past processed applications, and when there is no history of using the Direct Certification (DC) Sibling Match function. Parents/guardians receive their letters via email and/or printed letters. District Student Eligibility personnel generally send Household notifications (and Applications) to the parents/guardians of the households in the summer on or around July 1. This timeframe assists in providing District Student Eligibility personnel plenty of time to begin processing submitted applications before the new school year begins.
