# NXT-3547 — Configuration - Add Job Title

## Description
Admin can configure job titles under system settings. 

* CEUs are standard to USDA
 
!USDA CEU Requirements.PNG|width=291,height=126!


* Job titles are what the state/district calls that CEU role 
** (i.e. Program Manager CEU = Kitchen Manager Job title) 
** CEU and Job titles can be the same name, but we give the option to customize
* Link the CEU and Job title 
* Each district has to have at least one of each USDA CEU level
* They can add more job titles and tie to a CEU if they want (with permission)

## Acceptance Criteria
Demo that Employees can customize job titles associated with CEU levels. CEUs requirement levels are tracked to ensure employees are meeting professional standards throughout the academic year.
