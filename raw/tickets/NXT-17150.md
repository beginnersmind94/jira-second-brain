# NXT-17150 — QA and Integration -  Production - Planning - Grocery List

## Description
As a Site Manager, I need to order food and supplies for serving students and/or staff at my location (and possibly other locations), based on my completed production plans.

!image-20210706-154031.png|width=100%!



Having a report - a Grocery List - that will compile my planned counts for a date range that I select, and give me a total quantity of product needed in order to provide service those days, would make it much easier for me to create my orders to the vendors.

Requirements

* Only pull information from ‘Plan Complete’ Status for the selected dates
* Item information needs to be pulled from Item Management
* PDF / HTML report will be in another story. This is just the UI portion under the report section

## Acceptance Criteria
A Grocery List should allow the user to select a site, a date range that has production plans in a “Plan Complete” status, and obtain a report that lists needed inventory ordering and/or ingredient name information, based on the recipes/menu items that are assigned to my plans, with the quantities needed, and why.

For instance: I offer milk at Breakfast, Lunch, and Snack, every day.

This report should state the quantities needed for each of the services that my site has, the quantity of milk that I need for each of those services in the date range, and give me a total quantity needed for all of the services combined.

If I need 40 milk for breakfast, 60 milk for lunch, and 25 juice for snack… the report should list those, and then should also tell me that I need a total of 125 milks (or 2 cases + 25 eaches), in order to fulfill those services.
