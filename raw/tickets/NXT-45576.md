# NXT-45576 — IM - Menu Items - Advanced Filters

## Description
As a Menu Planner I want a consolidated list page for all variations of menu items, including POS items, so I can see all items as they will be served and sold to students and end customers.

## Acceptance Criteria
* Provide advanced filters for the Menu Items page
* +First Row+
** _Menu Item Status_ 
** _Menu Item Category_
** _Menu Item Type_
** Nutrient Status
*** Missing (Incomplete)
*** Manually Entered
*** System Calculated
* +Second Row+
** Tags
** Image & Document Tags (dropdown w/ all tag options *applicable to Menu Items*)
** Menu Item Flags (AND condition)
*** Favorite
*** Prep Site Item 
*** Show in Summary
*** POS enabled
** Leftover Category (OR condition)
* +Third Row+
** *Allergens* - advanced dropdown with two sections and a radio toggle
*** first section options - if either of these are selected, disable the second section
**** Missing Allergens
**** None Indicated
*** second section contains the individual allergens for multi selection
**** include custom as well (assuming performance is not negatively impacted)
*** IF a selection is made from the second section, display a radio option immediately below the dropdown
**** Including (default) - display recipes with those allergens
***** consider all allergen forms (except for ‘Free From’ obviously)
***** consider AND condition for multiple selections
**** Excluding - display recipes *without* those allergens
** *Contributions* - advanced dropdown with two sections
*** first section options - if either of these are selected, disable the second section
**** Missing/None
**** -Unapplied Recommendation-
***** stretch goal if logic is too complicated
*** second section options contain the 5 individual food components for multi selection
**** include any recipes containing the selected food components (AND condition)
