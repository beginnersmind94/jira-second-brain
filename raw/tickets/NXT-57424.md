## Acceptance Criteria
* Remove the ‘Menu Serving’ option from the Units card Add button
* replace it with a new ‘Menu Servings’ option in the Update button
* Upon clicking ‘Menu Servings,' load a sub-step screen which allows servings to be selected for the menu item
** basically re-use the initial Menu Serving selection screen, but make it permanent
* Allow the sub-step screen to be used to also *_disable_* menu servings
** prevent certain servings from being disabled (or deleted?) if they are locked per MP/Prod modules rules (check with MP team)
*** in this case, disable the menu serving identifier button and display a tooltip: “Menu Serving cannot be disabled due to [menu assignment… or whatever else]”
** *Will research* on MP/PD business rules 
* Once a purple serving is selected for the menu item, change it to the blue serving identifier
** consider the impact on identifying measures for use on recipes
