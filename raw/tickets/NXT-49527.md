# NXT-49527 — MVR - Dashboard - Email Now

## Description

*Notes:*

* Copy the Email Now from Custom Reports as much as possible and the requirements included here assume you will do that

* Action column updates:
** Email Now
*** If button not there yet, add it
*** On click, open the email popup
*** Do just like you do for CR, same way and rules

* 
* Security:
** User must have view rights to Manage Views Report dashboard to use this drawer
