# NXT-33262 — MP- Assign Menu- Deleting Menu confirmation pop 

## Description
As a menu Planner, I love the idea of being able to quickly delete menus from the assign menu screen, however I need some sort of confirmation to ensure I know what action I am performing and what menu and date I am deleting

## Acceptance Criteria
Give confirmation for user to confirm the deletion of the menu 

Give user information as to what menu they are deleting and what date they are deleting

Are you sure you want to delete Menu : “ Menu Name” on “Date”? Yes/ No along with the site and site group information

IF user deletes on Select All also add what sites will be effected based on this deletion  (Use similar look as assign menu confirmation for sites effected)
