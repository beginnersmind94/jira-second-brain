# NXT-5206 — Application Entry - Guided Process (2. Benefits)

## Description
*User Story* 

When i enter app details, the guided process takes me to step 2 of the application. 

*Mock Ups* 

[https://www.figma.com/proto/yrkJeGfd3f9hWjqm0qvfir/Edge2.0_V1?node-id=1306%3A26124&scaling=min-zoom|https://www.figma.com/proto/yrkJeGfd3f9hWjqm0qvfir/Edge2.0_V1?node-id=1306%3A26124&scaling=min-zoom]

*Requirements* 

* Benefits Questions:
** Do any household members receive benefits from SNAP, TANF, or FDPIR?
*** *Yes* > pick program > Enter Case Number (required) > Recipient Name (optional)
**** Program selection > uses local assistance program names (see eligibility description page)
**** Enter Case Number for program selected
**** Recipient Name
***** optional
***  If yes >  proceed to household step 3 > we will show an info message at the top of the page for Household Members: "_This section is not required due to the basis of the application._"
**** No dismiss option
*** No > recipient name and benefit question are hidden
* Refused Benefits 
** Has the applicant refused benefits? 
*** If yes >  proceed to household step 3 > we will show an info message at the top of the page for Household Members: "_This section is not required due to the basis of the application._"
*** No > proceed to household step 3
* Both questions have to be answered to proceed
* Default: Benefits - No, (if yes) SNAP, refuse - No 
* option to go to previous step (1)

## Acceptance Criteria
- Applicant transitions from step 1 to 2 
- option to go back to previous step 
- application can enter assistance information above 
- recipient name is optional
- refused benefits or programs question answered yes > show message
-Both questions have to be answered to proceed
- if yes on benefits, case number is required 
- Multi-language, ADA compliant.
