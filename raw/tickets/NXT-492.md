# NXT-492 — Team Work App - Employee

## Description
downloadable application for TW 

* Employees can
** add personal info (profile pic, phone, etc.) 
** view available trainings
** RSVP up for trainings
** view schedule of assigned trainings
** See badges of accomplishments
** track CEU requirement compliance

## Acceptance Criteria
