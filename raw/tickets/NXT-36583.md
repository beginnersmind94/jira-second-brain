# NXT-36583 — IM - Recipe Rework - Recipe Steps - HACCP Process (Step 0)

## Description
As a Nutritionist I want the HACCP options available on recipes so I can easily assign required control points for my recipe preparation.

*Requirements*

* Provide a small section on the recipe Steps & Ingredients tab above (or on, inside, next to, etc.) the Steps card
** For now, this could be just “HACCP Process” but may be expanded on later
*** This may eventually be more accurately called “Pre-Steps” or something, and could contain other fields like Pre-Preparation Instructions
** In the ‘[Edit Recipe Steps|https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-36358]' mode, enable the HACCP Process selection 
*** This will reflect the configured processes on the config page, but will just be No Cook, Same Day Service and Complex Food 98% of the time
*** This is the only required selection for this section
** Provide independent Save/Cancel
* Validate Saves against the added control points
** Allow independent editing
** Allow Process to be changed
*** If the user has CCPs added to the Steps which are not associated with the Process selected, warn the user
**** “This will clear some of the existing Control Points. Are you sure to change the HACCP Process?”
*** If the user switches Processes but the CCP is still associated, retain the existing CCP
* In the same Edit Recipe Steps mode, provide an ‘Add CCP’ button/action on the same level as the ‘Add Step’ button
