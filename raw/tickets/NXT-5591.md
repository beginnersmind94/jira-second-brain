# NXT-5591 — Application Details - Eligibility 

## Description
*User Story* 

An applications processor can quickly see pinned information outlining basic application details and determination.

*Mock Ups* 

[https://www.figma.com/proto/yrkJeGfd3f9hWjqm0qvfir/Edge2.0_V1?node-id=87%3A2599&scaling=min-zoom|https://www.figma.com/proto/yrkJeGfd3f9hWjqm0qvfir/Edge2.0_V1?node-id=87%3A2599&scaling=min-zoom]

*Requirements* 

* Eligibility (section)
** Determination
*** Free, Reduced, Denied
** Basis 
*** If basis is income, then show > household size and income/frequency
*** if basis is categorical: \[Assistance Program] > show case type and case #
*** if basis is categorical: \[Student Category] > display that as Categorical: Homeless/Migrant/whichever-, with a (Confirmed) next to it.-
** Household Size (required for income applications)
*** Numerical value of household size
*** HH size not required for categorial: assistance program applications, therefore this section is not required to show
*** If HH size is indicated on app show it
** -Annual Income (income apps)-
*** -Show calculated income for the year-
* Income Eligibility Guidelines (IEGs) Graphic
** Display for income apps (combined with Other Source Categorical)
*** Show where income falls in the Eligibility Guidelines chart 
** Hide for Categorical: Assistance Programs and Single student - Other Source Categorical

## Acceptance Criteria
- shows eligibility data listed above 
- Multi-language, ADA compliant.
