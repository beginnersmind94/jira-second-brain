# NXT-70372 — FIN - Configure Prepaid Liability Posting Level (Enrollment vs. Central)

## Description
As a Child Nutrition Director, I want to toggle my Prepaid Liability posting strategy between "Student Enrollment Site" and "Central Office," So that I can align the system's automated ledger entries with my district's balance sheet strategy (Site-Based vs. Pooled).

*Requirements*

* *Settings Page Scope and Structure* Add a Settings page (Financials › Settings) with the tab *Posting Rules*.
* *Posting Rules Content and Help Text* In Posting Rules, show a *Prepaid Liability, Bonus & Refund Posting* control with two mutually exclusive options:
** *Distributed (Enrollment Site)* — _Per-site postings_ | Tooltip: Posts Prepaid Liability, Bonus, and Refund at each enrollment site.
** *Centralized (District Office)* — _One district-level posting_ | Tooltip: Posts one district total for Prepaid Liability, Bonus, and Refund transactions to the Central Office. 

*Decision Behavior and Warning* Changing posting mode must require confirmation, auto-toggle the opposite option off, and show a success toast:

{quote}_"Future Prepaid Liability, Bonus, and Refund ledger entries will now post at the [enrollment site / central office] level."_{quote}

Update on {{2026-03-06}}: Clarification: *Prepaid Liability, Bonus & Refund Posting is a radio-button choice* with two options, *Distributed (Enrollment Site)* and *Centralized (District Office)*; users can select only one at a time, and the existing card layout, confirmation prompt, and success toast remain unchanged.

## Acceptance Criteria
h3. AC1 — Configuration UI

* Given a user with Director/SchoolCafe Administrator permissions navigates to Financial Oversight › Configuration › Settings
* When they view the *Posting Settings* section,
* Then they see a control labeled *"Prepaid Liability, Bonus & Refund Posting"* with two mutually exclusive options:
*# *Distributed (Enrollment Site)* — _Per-site postings_ | Tooltip: Posts Prepaid Liability, Bonus, and Refund transactions at each enrollment site. 
*# *Centralized (District Office)* — _One district-level posting_ | Tooltip: Posts one district total for Prepaid Liability, Bonus, and Refund transactions to the Central Office.

h3. AC2 — Logic: Distributed (Default)

* *Given* "Distributed" is selected,
* *When* Period Close generates entries,
* *Then* the debit and credit sides of the entry use the relevant site’s sub-accounts and entries are made per enrollment site. 

h3. AC3 — Logic: Centralized

* *Given* *"Centralized"* is selected,
*When* Period Close generates entries,
*Then* the debit and credit sides of the transaction use the *Central Office sub-account* and *one entry* is made combining amounts for all sites in that district, where the Central Office site code is determined as follows:
*# *Primary:* Fetch the district’s *Central Office site code* from *Site Configuration* (district/site setup).
*# *Fallback:* If no Central Office site code exists in configuration, use a *default site code* of *all zeros*, where *zero-count = district site code length*.

h3. *AC4 — Warning*

* Given a district switches from Centralized to Distributed or vice-versa,
* When they toggle the option,
* Then the system must:
*# Display a confirmation dialog: _"Are you sure? This will change how Future Prepaid Liability, Bonus, and Refund ledger entries are posted."_
*# Upon confirmation, auto-toggle the opposite option off.
*# Show a success toast: _"Future Prepaid Liability, Bonus, and Refund ledger entries will now post at the [enrollment site / central office] level."_

Note: This includes prepaid liability, refunds and bonuses. Only post sites that have a *non-zero net amount* for the period.
