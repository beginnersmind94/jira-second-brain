# NXT-65091 — MP - Menu Planner - Update and Combining Fruits & Veg for Daily and Weekly (SBP 14+)

## Description
Based on new requirements, analysis for breakfast should be combining the Fruit and Veg values when making sure a district is compliant with USDA standards. 

!Fruitsandvegetables.svg|width=123,height=95,alt="Fruitsandvegetables.svg"!

Example: 

!image (21).png|width=132,height=134,alt="image (21).png"!

## Acceptance Criteria
* On the *daily analysis* , update the Component of Fruits to Fruits/Veg
** No longer display Vegetable as a separate component
* On the *weekly analysis*, update the Component of Fruits/Veg
** No longer display Vegetable as a separate component
* Calculations for this will be a combination of both components together (Use same logic as before but combine the calculations) as the same for juice accounting for no more than half the offerings 
** Per USDA, “Schools must offer 1 cup of fruit daily and 5 cups of fruit weekly. Schools may substitute vegetables for fruit at breakfast”
** If fail, provide message in fly out under Fruit or Veg “Juice Offering exceeding weekly limit”
