# NXT-1905 — MP - Menu Cycle Costing

## Description
As a Food Service Director, one of my concerns is the potential cost of food that I’m using in my district. 

There should be a method of accessing and viewing this information on the Menu Cycle page, as well as the ability to print a report with the information, should the user desire.

This should give me a list of the menu items on my menus that are contained in my cycle, the planned quantities, the per serving cost, and the total cost. This information will be based on the product/information costing data from the Item Management Details page. 

(This would be expected to be for each week, and also for the entire cycle)

You will see that the report gives the cost for the individual serving, then multiplies it by the number of serving that have been entered on the menu page. 

(If no costing information is available or “compatible” for a menu item, then it’s expected that $0.00 would be the result, and it is up to the district to remedy those occurrences by entering or correcting data.)

“Standard Pricing” will be the costing information to utilize from Item Management in regards to the ingredient costs.

## Acceptance Criteria
As a Food Service Director, I will be able to select a button or icon on the menu cycle page, and get the full costing information based on the criteria I’ve entered on my items and menus. 



Disregard Variety Menu item when it comes to menu costing/menu cycle costing if no cost is available or there is a null value
