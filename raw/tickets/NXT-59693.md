# NXT-59693 — IM - Bulk Item - Nutrients - Add logic for calculation validations

## Description
As an onboarding district admin I want the Bulk Nutrients step to validate the math of the nutrient data so I can ensure I’m entering valid nutrient data for my items.

## Acceptance Criteria
* Add logic to Nutrient steps for additional data validations
** Total Fat should be equal to or greater than the sum of Saturated and Trans Fat
** Total Carbs should be equal to or greater than the sum of Fiber, Total Sugar and Added Sugar
** Added Sugar should not exceed Total Sugar
* Validate the calories
** Calories = (9*Gram of Fat) + (4*Gram of Carb) + (4*Gram of Protein)
** Allow some variance: 10-15%
*** Show soft warning on Calories if the difference exceeds
