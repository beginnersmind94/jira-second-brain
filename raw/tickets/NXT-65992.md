* Remove the Contact tab of the Vendor Details page
* Redesign the Vendor tab to contain all info from removed Contact tab
* Split the info into 4 distinct cards
** Vendor Info (name, #, type, status)
** Ordering Preferences (mode, lead time, weekends, prod lead time)
** Contact Info (phone, email, website, address)
** Contact Persons (one record for each contact)
*** remove limit on number of contact persons, if limit exists
*** increase height of card or use in-line scrollbar to show 5+ persons
* Provide an edit icon for each applicable card (Vendor Info, Ordering Preferences, Contact Info) to allow separate in-line editing
** Only allow one card at a time to be edited
* Display History icon only on Vendor Info card 
** include history from other cards
* Provide the Add Contact button within the Contact Persons card
* Occupy the full Contact Persons card when adding or editing an individual contact
* Remove unused fields
