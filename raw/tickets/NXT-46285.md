## Acceptance Criteria
* Replace Site Type columns with Delivery Groups
* Replace Site Type grouping in slide-out with Delivery Groups
* Do not allow a site to order from itself
** hide the internal vendor from being configurable for that linked site
** indicate it’s the linked internal vendor site
** enforce on linked site (not on Distribution site type)
*** Distribution sites can be allowed to order from _other_ Distribution sites
* *Freeze column headers at top of page*
** the grid headers should *dock* at the top of the page so that when the user is scrolling through all their items, they can still see the grid headers
** consider implementing pagination instead
*** but only if this resolves the issue of not being able to see the headings
* Implement Manage & Saved Views
** so users can save different columns for their view
** ensure column adaptivity remains as-is
* Ensure page performance is good for up to 1000 items
