# NXT-20444 — Onboarding - Send Reminder email when certain criteria are met

## Description
As a Cybersoft Administrator, I would like to send reminders to people who have begun but not completed Onboarding after a certain amount of time or progress, so that they are more likely to finish by their Go-Live date.

*Summary*

Trigger an email to go out to the Contact Person of a district if they are within 3 days of their Go-Live date and have less than 90% progress completion for System or Front Office. This email should only go out one time.

*Mockup*

Email template:

{quote}Hello!

This is just a reminder that your Go-Live date of *{{goLive_Date}}* is approaching and your district has one or more SchoolCafé features where onboarding is not yet complete. Please log in to SchoolCafé at your earliest convenience so that you are able to finish onboarding on time. You can also move your Go-Live date back or contact your Success Mentor for assistance!

Thank you for using SchoolCafé!

-Your SchoolCafé Team{quote}

*Details*

* When a district has entered a go-Live date in Onboarding that is *>= 4* days in the future, do the following:
** If they complete less than *90%* of their available System _or_ Front Office tasks within *3* days of this date, send an email using the above template to the Contact Person of the district.
** This email should only go out +once+, even if the go-Live date lapses. We may add a second email about that at a later time.
* This template should be editable in System → Templates like the other System emails.

## Acceptance Criteria
Verify that an email goes out to the Contact Person of a district when they are within 3 days of their go-live date and < 90% of System or Front Office tasks complete.

Verify that the email only goes out once.

Verify that the template contains the go-live date field and can be edited in System → Templates in the System module section.
