# NXT-3825 — Account Management - Advanced Search - Demographics Section

## Description
The Demographic Section is a collection of five subsections, Ethnicity, Race, Gender, Language, and Birthday. 

!Screen Shot 2020-04-29 at 1.46.41 PM.png|width=277,height=256!

## Acceptance Criteria
When the Demographics section is expanded the 5 sub sections are available.

When values are selected from multiple subsections they are 'AND'ed together

When a filter is selected from one of the subsections the filter selection count is increased by 1.

When a filter is removed from one of the subsection the filter selection count is decreased by 1.
