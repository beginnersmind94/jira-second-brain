# NXT-47451 — PR- Production Plan- Autofill- Based on Menu Planning Counts calculations

## Description
As a production manager, there are plenty of times that I repeat the same menus over and over throughout the course of the school year. I would like to quickly fill in my production plan counts based on menu planning counts. The options could be to copy the menu item counts straight from the menu along with the meal counts or to scale the counts based on the meal counts entered on the production side.

## Acceptance Criteria
* Follow these calculations for selection *Adjust Menu Item Counts to Meal Counts for this Site*
* Example: 
** Meal Counts on *menu* are 6-8= 100, 9-12= 250 and user enters on *production plan* 6-8= 300, 9-12= 600
** Menu Item 1= 75,100
** Menu Item 2= 25,150
** Menu Item 3= 50. 200
*** To calculate the numbers to autofill into the production plan for the menu items, divide the planned meal counts by the menu meal counts and multiply by the menu item counts. 
**** 6-8 300/100=3, 9-12 600/250=2.4
**** Autofill numbers:
***** Menu Item 1= 75*3=*225*, 100*2,4=*240*
***** Menu Item 2= 25*3=*75*, 150*2.4=*360*
***** Menu Item 3= 50*3=*150*, 200*2.4=*480*
