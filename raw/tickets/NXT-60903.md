## Acceptance Criteria
* Modify the Shipping tab in relation to the Complete Shipping button
** replace this button with a ‘Ship Selected’ button
* Change the ‘Ready for Shipment’ column to ‘Shipment Status’
** allow shipments to be individually marked as Shipped
* Enable the ‘Ship Selected’ button when one or more shipments are checked as ‘Ready for Shipment’
* After Ship Selected, change the selected shipments to Shipped status
** replace checkbox with Shipped truck icon 
* Allow shipments in Shipped status to be reverted
** clicking on the truck+label will provide a ‘Revert Shipment’ prompt
** this would revert the shipment to Not Ready
* After a shipment is marked as Shipped, add 2 columns to the Stop/Site sub-grid
** Delivery Status 
*** Pending Receipt, or Received
** Action
*** view receipt
*** view receipt report
* Update the Shipment Status once any receipts are created
** *Partially Received* if 1 or more sites have received, but not all stops are received
** *Received* if all stops have confirmed receipts
* Remove Action buttons for shipments after Partially Received status
* Disable Modify Shipments button once all shipments are in Shipped or later status
* Enable Close Distribution button once all shipments are in Shipped or later status
** change Delivery Tickets button to gray
** Close Distribution button in outlined purple
* Update Distribution Status based on shipment statuses
** Shipped - if all shipments are Shipped or later status
** Shipping in Progress - if any shipments are still Not Ready
