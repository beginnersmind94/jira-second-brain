# NXT-60008 — TH - Base - Home Screen

## Description
*As a* TeamHub user *I want* a visually appealing home screen that greets me by name, provides quick access to key features, and displays an overview of important metrics *So that* I can efficiently navigate to the areas of the app I use most and stay informed about my current tasks and notifications.

h2. Comments

* Screen: Login screen ({{app/(tabs)/home.tsx}})
* App home screen

h2. User Flow

* User opens the application and is directed to login screen
* On login success, user is redirected to home screen
* User can see key metrics from workspace, custom reports, insights and production
* User can go to their profile page
* User can click on quick links to certain places in the app

h2. Requirements

* Ensure the screen supports dark mode
* 

_Header & Greeting_

* *Dynamic Greeting*
** The top section displays a time-based greeting (e.g., “Good Evening, Sarah”) that personalizes the user experience
** It calculates the verbiage as such:
*** If between 6:00 am and noon, it says Good Morning, [first name]
*** If between noon and 6:00 pm, it says Good Afternoon, [first name]
*** If between 6:00 pm and 6:00 am, it says Good Evening, [first name]
* *Profile Icon*: 
** A circular profile icon (or avatar) appears in the top-right corner
** Tapping it directs the user to their Profile screen, where they can view and edit personal details.
*** Note profile screen is covered in another story

_Quick Actions_

* *Section Title*: “Quick Actions” indicates this area is for frequently used features.
* *Action Cards* (3 Cards):
** *Daily Insights*
*** Title: “Daily Insights”
*** Subtitle: “View today’s KPIs”
*** On Tap: Opens Insights screen (covered in another story)
** *Production KPIs*
*** Title: “Production KPIs”
*** Subtitle: “Update today’s records”
*** On Tap: Opens production screen (covered in another story)
** *Workspace*
*** Title: “Workspace”
*** Subtitle: “Team collaboration center”
*** On Tap: Opens workspace screen (covered in another story)

_Overview_

* *Section Title*: “Overview” provides a snapshot of the user’s current status across various app modules.
* *Overview Cards* (6 Tiles):
** *-Unread Messages-* 
*** -Shows count of unread messages from the Messages feature-
*** -Note: we may not be able to do messages, TBD-
** *Unread Emails*
*** Shows count of unread inbox messages from the Inbox feature
** *-Unread Announcements-*
*** -Shows count of unread announcement messages from the announcement feature-
*** -Note: we may not be able to do announcements, TBD-
** *Unread Reports*
*** Shows count of unread custom reports from distributed tab in the CR feature
** *Open Tasks*
*** Shows count of open tasks from the tasks feature
** *Record Complete*
*** Shows Percent complete from “Today’s” record
* Each tile displays a count or percentage relevant to the user’s account. Tapping a tile may take the user to a detailed list or screen
* Note: each one of these cards has a dependency on other stories being completed, so just hard code the number to 1 (1% for Record) until those stories are done and then we will update cards

## Acceptance Criteria
# *Header & Greeting*
#* The greeting text dynamically updates based on the user’s profile name and/or time of day.
#* Tapping the profile icon always navigates to the Profile screen.
# *Quick Actions Section*
#* The section label “Quick Actions” is visible and easily distinguishable.
#* Each action card is clearly labeled with an icon/title and a short subtitle.
#* Tapping any card navigates to its respective detailed screen (Daily Insights, Production KPIs, Workspace).
# *Overview Section*
#* The section label “Overview” is clearly displayed.
#* Six tiles show up-to-date counts or percentages (e.g., Unread Messages, Unread Emails, etc.).
#* Each tile’s label and count/percentage reflect real-time or last-known data from the user’s account.
# *Navigation Menu*
#* The menu displays at least four items (Home, Workspace, Insights, Production).
#* The currently active screen (Home) is visually highlighted.
#* Tapping another menu item smoothly transitions the user to that section.
# *Performance & Usability*
#* The home screen loads within 2 seconds on standard devices.
#* All navigation actions (card taps, menu taps, profile icon tap) respond within 1 second.
# *Security & Access*
#* The home screen is accessible only to authenticated users.
#* Menu is security trimmed based upon license and role permissions
