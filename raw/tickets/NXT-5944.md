# NXT-5944 — Account Management - Accounts Search - Phase 2 Filters

## Description
*User Story*

As a district admin I want a better UX experience and sharper filters when pulling up a list of accounts, so that I can refine my search to better create custom lists for exporting.

*Mock-up*

[https://www.figma.com/proto/vEncfcQPT3DYGVKOVAagis/System-%26-Platforms-5.0?node-id=62%3A2835&scaling=scale-down-width&page-id=62%3A2834&starting-point-node-id=62%3A2835|https://www.figma.com/proto/vEncfcQPT3DYGVKOVAagis/System-%26-Platforms-5.0?node-id=62%3A2835&scaling=scale-down-width&page-id=62%3A2834&starting-point-node-id=62%3A2835|smart-link] 

*Requirements*

* Move the current filters from being an expansion panel to instead displaying in the *Columns & Views* button drawer. 
** The team wants the drawer to contain all 3 of the following as tabs, in this order: 
**# Filters
**# Columns
**# Views
* Add the existing filters into the filters/columns tabs
** Exceptions:
*** Remove the ‘quick search’ field at the top of the filters. Replace this with a First Name and Last Name drop-downs
*** These new drop downs can act as 'Like'/'Contains' searches, i.e. searching for first name 'b' should search for 'b*'
* Add these new filters as well. These would be considered advanced filters, off by default (Not pictured in mock-up)
** Tags (Self-explanatory)

** -_Area_-
*** -_Default is blank, aka All._-
*** -_Any areas for the district should be available for multi-select._-
*** -_The site dropdown has to update based on the selected Area(s)._-
*** -_Column should be sortable._-
** -_Reason (Multi-select)_-
*** -_Only available for Student searching._-
*** -_Default: Blank/All_-
*** -_Reason list should be fueled by the selected eligibility dropdown._-
*** -_Column should be sortable._-
** -_As of Date_-
*** -_Default: Today’s date._-
*** -_Dates in the past can be selected, likely need a calendar selector._-
*** -_The resulting information (Eligibility, Balances, grade, site, status) all need to comply with this date._-
*** -_Column should be sortable._-

* Grid change for existing filters:
** Make Special Instructions visible in field search
** Allow the user to field search for any phone number, not just Cell Phone (ignore if already handled)

## Acceptance Criteria
Verify that the filters have moved into a newly renamed drawer, in the order of Filters, Columns, and Views tabs

Verify that the Filters tab contains both the old and new section (Tags), with the old quick search being replaced by a ‘First Name’ and ‘Last Name’ fields

Verify name fields act as ‘Like’/'Contains' searches

Verify the columns can be sorted as best as possible

Verify that Special Instructions can be filtered within field search

Verify that any phone number (not just Cell Phone) can be filtered within field search
