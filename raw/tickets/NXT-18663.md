# NXT-18663 — Platform - BO - View & Update District Accounts

## Description
*Persona*: CyberSoft/ISD Administrator

*Access level:* Central Office/District Admin

*Description*:

As a Biz admin, I should be able to create new district account and add modules/products that was purchased for specific school district

*Mockup:*

[https://www.figma.com/proto/doHue5kUA34R8vhLaUxHQ2/New-District-Account?node-id=1%3A198&scaling=scale-down-width&page-id=0%3A1&starting-point-node-id=1%3A5|https://www.figma.com/proto/doHue5kUA34R8vhLaUxHQ2/New-District-Account?node-id=1%3A198&scaling=scale-down-width&page-id=0%3A1&starting-point-node-id=1%3A5|smart-link] 

*Requirements*:

* Central biz admin logs into System/Onboarding page for list of district accounts onboarded in “District Accounts” section
** +District Accounts table+
*** District Name,
*** Number of Modules Licensed
* EACH district account has VIEW icon which opens Licensing Summary of the that specific district account
* All functionalities within Licensing Summary should be included in the District Account onboarding

## Acceptance Criteria
* User shall be able to see List of district accounts are displayed in the district account tab in Onboarding Page
* User shall be able to click on VIEW icon to see licensed products and details
* User shall be able to see District account detail page integrated with all functionalities of Licensing Summary page
