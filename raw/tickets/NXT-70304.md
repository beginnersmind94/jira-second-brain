# NXT-70304 — FIN - Automate Sales Tax Liability Posting via System-Defined Entry Types

## Description
As a Child Nutrition Director I want sales tax transactions to automatically post to the general ledger using standardized entry types So that my tax liabilities are accurately recorded and ready for remittance without manual tracking

h4. Definitions

* *Meal Sales Revenue* = the tax-exclusive revenue amount (does *not* include sales tax).
* *Sales Tax* = the tax amount collected on taxable sales (a *liability*, not revenue).
* *Total / Gross Amount* = Meal Sales Revenue *plus* Sales Tax (may exist in POS, but must not be used for revenue posting).

## Acceptance Criteria
h3. AC1 — Trigger & aggregation

*Given* a POS period is closed for a site
*When* the posting job runs
*Then* the system creates *exactly one* TAXCLCTD journal entry for that *site + POS period*.

h3. AC2 — Posting date

*Given* a TAXCLCTD entry is created
*Then* Posting Date = *last day of the POS period*.

h3. AC3 — Amount calculation (net tax)

*Given* POS provides tax collected and tax reversed (void/return) for the site and POS period
*When* TAXCLCTD is generated
*Then* Posted Amount = *Tax Collected – Tax Reversed* (same POS period).

h3. AC4 — Entry structure (2 lines only)

*Given* a TAXCLCTD entry is generated
*Then* it contains *exactly two* lines:

# *Debit:* Cash in Bank = Net Sales Tax
# *Credit:* Sales Tax Owed (Liability) = Net Sales Tax

h3. AC5 — Debits equal credits

*Then* Total Debits = Total Credits for TAXCLCTD.

h3. AC6 — POS Period Close

Appears as ‘Sales Tax Collected’ in POS Period Close job corresponding to the period.

h3. AC7 — Inactive account handling

*Given* Cash in Bank or Sales Tax Owed is inactive
*When* posting runs
*Then* skip TAXCLCTD
