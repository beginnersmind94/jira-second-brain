# NXT-59072 — INV - Vendor & Contract Items - Update Price - Save All logic on Effective Date

## Description
As a food service admin I want the Save All option to ignore items without a price so I can make Effective Date updates to some items without having to update all items.

h2. Contract Items - Update Price

* In the mockup example, Bagel and Tomato Paste would *not* present an error; they would just be skipped

## Acceptance Criteria
*Update Effective Date logic*

* Update the Save All logic regarding Effective Date
* Revise the appearance of the ‘Prices’ column
** Show the dates in a shorter format
** options: 
*** $74.99 (1/1/2022 - 12/31/2024)
**** this is just flipping the current display
*** $74.99 (until 12/31/2024)
*** $74.99 (current)
**** $80 (7/1/2025) - example of future price
*** Or just show $74.99 (current) and then show the date range on a tooltip
* Ignore items which have an Effective Date but do *not* have a Price when doing the Save All
** currently, using the header date field will populate every item with a Date, but the user may not be intending to update every single item price. right now they have to clear the date to proceed otherwise those items provide errors.
* After saving the valid items, leave the other items on screen with the Effective Date still applied
* Provide a ‘clear’ option in the header Effective Date field, which would clear all on-screen dates 
* Don’t save new price records if the Price did not change 
** even if a new Effective Date is selected
