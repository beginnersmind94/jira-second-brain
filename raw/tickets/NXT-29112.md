# NXT-29112 — IM > Recipe Page > Nutrients on page

## Description
Requirement:  For food items and recipes, ensure missing nutrient values are consistently marked and zero valuese show “0”

a.) At the recipe level, some nutrients are missing the (M) designation in the per 100g column, while the (M) designation is showing in the per serving column. (See LR0098 USDA Recipe Salisbury Steak on Preview environment)

b.) At the recipe level, some zero values are not showing “0\{“ in per 100g column; they have blanks in the rows. (See LR0036 Broccoli, Raw USDA Recipe for Schools)
