Requirements

* Add a New Forecast
** Forecast Description
*** Name can be changed
** Forecast Period
*** Cannot be changed after initial ‘Save’
** Forecast Type
*** Cannot be changed after initial ‘Save’
** Meal Type
*** Selection of all  meal types - Can be changed (Updates the information below)
*** After creation, you can only add more, but you cannot remove once the forecast has been saved
** Site Groups
*** Selection of all Site Groups available - Can be changed (Updates the information below)
*** After creation, you can only add more, but you cannot remove once the forecast has been saved
* Select Dates
** Start Date
*** Default to the ‘Start date’ from forecast period
*** Allow the user to select ‘Start Date’ and change it
**** This can be a date not in the stated forecast period
** End Date
*** Default to the ‘End date’ from forecast period
*** Allow the user to select ‘End Date’ and change it
**** This can be a date not in the stated forecast period
** Multiplier
*** Allow the user to multiple the values in the table below
**** Use case: If they want to see a larger or smaller increase based on their usage, they can ‘play’ with the data to see the differences to check with costs
*** Default of 1.00
*** Only affects Total Projected Meals
** Apply
*** Apply the changes and dates
*** After applying, the Site Groups appear below

** Next (Shows up after clicking ‘Apply’ and the data below populates

*** Menu Line
*** Daily Average Meals
*** Total Menu Days - Total number of days there were active menus
