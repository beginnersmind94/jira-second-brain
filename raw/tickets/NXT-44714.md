## Acceptance Criteria
* Remove SIR as a concept from Item Management
** now it will be either a Direct Menu Item with recipe steps, or a DMI without recipe steps
** Remove all text/button/error references to “Single Item Recipe” 
*** Item Type slideout, Units card identifiers, etc.
* Merge the functionality of SIR into the existing Menu & POS Info tab 
* Provide the Steps card as an optional feature of the direct menu item
** can only be enabled once the Menu Item is created
