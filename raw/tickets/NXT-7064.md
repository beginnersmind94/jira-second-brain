# NXT-7064 — Meal Equivalents (MEQ) & Meal Counts (Summary, Chart & Table)

## Description
*Definition :*

 The MEQ is the conversion of different meal services (i.e., breakfast,supper and snacks) and non-program food sales to the equivalent of one federally reimbursable student lunch for comparison purposes.

 

*Objective :*

Ø  Allows to calculate 3 other KPIs, Revenue oer meal equivalent, cost per meal equivalent and meals per labor hour

Ø  To measure effectiveness of the program by evaluating financial performance against both internal and external benchmarks

 

*Frequency to calculate* : Weekly, Monthly, and annually

 +*Out of scope :*+  Goals, export, drilldown, more actionable insights, recommendations, ability to drill down into the details of the meal counts.
