# NXT-25349 — Allergen Import-Exceptions Email

## Description
Notifications must be sent to the user who is uploading the files incase if exceptions via emails.

The email must be addressed to the group of users (currently to the person / user who uploads the file).

The email must contain below details.



Subject : Allergen Import “\[FileName]“ Failed.

Body : 

Hello \[UserName],

Allergen Import for the file “\[FileName]“ failed. Below is the list of accounts that failed to update, as these allergens are missing:

!Email Alert.png|width=1696,height=674!

Please add the missing allergens to the allergen list and reprocess the file.

*“Disclaimer verbiage“

As handled in nxt-29772, _"_*_No record found in POS"_* should not be visible in Exception tab as well as Email.

## Acceptance Criteria
Verify and Validate that the email is sent incase of missing allergens.

Verify and Validate that the emails contains the subject, body as per requirement.

Verify and Validate that the disclaimer is present

Verify and Validate that the count of students in the exception email is same as the ones on Exception Log

+*The disclaimer verbiage shall be:*+

Allergen Feature Disclaimer

 1. _The standard list of allergens available in the SchoolCafé “Allergen” feature is taken from the Food Allergen Labeling and Consumer Protection Act of 2004 (FALCPA) plus the addition of gluten. FALCPA allergens include: milk, egg, fish, crustacean shell fish, tree nuts, wheat, peanuts and soybeans._

_2. The SchoolCafé “Allergen” feature is a tool to help SFAs identify FALCPA allergens and gluten to aid in restricting POS menu item sales._

_3. SchoolCafé does NOT identify any allergens._

_4. The effectiveness of the SchoolCafé “Allergen” feature is dependent upon the data provided by authorized users within the system._

_5. No allergen information within SchoolCafé should ever be considered a guarantee or recommendation, but simply a best faith effort to serve our customers._

_6. SchoolCafé is not liable for inconsistent, incorrect, or missing allergen information._

_7. Custom Allergens may be added for district tracking needs. Custom Allergens may include sensitivities or religious preferences. Additionally, any Custom Allergens are not required by FALCPA to be listed on nutrition fact labels and the district assumes all liabilities for any unmarked or improper allergens._
