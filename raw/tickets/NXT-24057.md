# NXT-24057 — POS - Summary Sales - Support for Free/Reduced/Paid

## Description
As Vera the Director I want my cashiers to be able to enter summary sales with the individual student price types to save time and increase accuracy for reimbursements.

*requirements*

* Update the Summary Sale section of the POS to replace the Student count with:
** Free
** Reduced
** Paid
* Change the ordering:
** Free
** Reduced
** Paid
** Second Meal
** Program Adult
** Staff
** Visitor
* Add the option to enter an Ala Carte amount with any of the lines.

## Acceptance Criteria
POS' summary sale updated:

* Student replaced with Free, Reduced, Paid. 
* Ordering updated.
* Ala Carte $ amount can be entered.

Data needs to flow up and be part of reporting.

Free/Reduced/Paid counts and A la carte $ need to show up as part of reimbursable meal counts on the Sessions, Session Summary, and Reconciliation pages.

A la carte $ can be edited inside of Reconciliation.

A la carte $ should show in the following reports:

* Activity, Cash Collection, …
