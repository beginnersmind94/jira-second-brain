# NXT-30729 — INV - Orders - Quick Receipt - Consider Holiday Calendar

## Description
As a FSD (Food Service Director), I would like my district Holiday Calendar to be referenced for Quick Receipts so that schools can not circumvent holiday schedule by creating quick receipts.

*Note:* perhaps a setting should exist for this

*Requirements*

* Prevent holidays from being selected as Date for new Quick Receipts
