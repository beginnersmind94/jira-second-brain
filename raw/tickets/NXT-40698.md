## Acceptance Criteria
* Add Ingredient as an option in the Item Type slideout
** this replaces the existing toggle for Ingredient Info card
* Update the Item Type slideout UI
** Display Active filters for any item type once it is officially created, and remove the ‘Enable as’ part of label
** For unenabled types, leave the toggle and ‘Enable’ text
** For active item types, display a small Deactivate ('Discontinue ') button
*** *only for type(s) in scope of story
* Provide a prompt on click of Deactivate button
** “Do you wish to make this ingredient inactive? [Yes, Deactivate], [No, Cancel]
** On click of ‘Yes, Deactivate’ check for all active recipes containing the item
* If the item is not used on any recipes, move the status directly to *Inactive*
** *Note:* must consider custom Menu Item “ingredients”
* If the item is used on active recipes
** move the status into *Pending Deactivation* 
** update the lower section to contain help text “To complete deactivation, remove from active recipes (XX)” where XX is the number of matching recipes
** Provide the ‘View Recipes’ button to open a new tab and navigate the user to the Recipes list page with the ‘Contains Ingredient' filter pre-populated and applied
** (*future*) -Provide the Find & Replace shortcut-
* Use a job to automatically convert ‘Pending Deactivation’ ingredients to ‘Inactive’ status once all recipe assignments are cleared 
* Once the status is Inactive, provide a small button for reactivating
