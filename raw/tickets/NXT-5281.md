# NXT-5281 — System - Add Income Survey system settings

## Description
As a district admin I want to be able to be able to easily customize our Income Survey offering, so that we do not have to adjust to the default SchoolCafé Income Survey.

*No UX Required*

*Requirements*

* Within System → Configuration → Settings, add a new category to the Eligibility module
* Within that category add the following settings:
** *Enable Income Surveys (with License)*
*** Setting Code: ADDINCSUR
*** Tooltip: Set to ‘Yes’ if parents only submit Income Surveys. Set to ‘Mixed’ if parents submit a Meal Application or Income Survey based on the enrollment sites of their students. Set to ‘No’ if parents submit Meal Applications only.
*** Is Internal: No
*** Type: Dropdown 
*** Possible values
**** Yes
**** Mixed
**** No _(default setting value)_
** *Income Range Type*
*** Setting Code: INCSSTYPE
*** Tooltip: This setting indicates how survey income can be entered: based on USDA-defined ranges, based on district-defined ranges, or as exact income.
*** Is Internal: No
*** Type: Dropdown
*** Possible values
***# USDA Income Range _(default setting value)_
***# Household Income
***# District Income Range
** *Show ‘Details’ step*
*** Setting Code: ENAISBENE
*** Tooltip: Enable this setting to show the ‘Details’ step on the Income Survey, which displays fields for Race, Ethnicity, and Other Benefits.
*** Is Internal: No
*** Type: Toggle 
*** Default setting value: Disabled
* Actual implementation of these settings is not required in this story, only that they be made available to view/modify by the user.
