# NXT-33394 — Scheduled Notifications - Trigger Notification at Scheduled Time

## Description
The current implementation of scheduled notifications uses a scheduled job that runs every 30 minutes.  This job then pulls candidate notifications to see if their defined run-time falls within the last 30 minutes (See step 2 in the attached pdf).  Because of this, the notification could potentially run 29 minutes late.

The objective of this story is to have the notification candidate sent to the module for evaluation at the scheduled time.

## Acceptance Criteria
* A scheduled notification shall be sent to a module for evaluation (See step 3 in the attached pdf) at the time that the notification is scheduled.
