# NXT-4683 — ItemMgmt - Footer - Cancel/Back/Next/Save&Close

## Description
*User Story*

As a Item Management User, I want to be able to cancel, go back, go forward, or save and close during adding an item.

*Requirements*

* Standardize the footer on ‘Add item’ step for food and non food items
** First step - When there are no steps before
*** CANCEL, NEXT, SAVE & CLOSE
** Second step
*** CANCEL, BACK, NEXT, SAVE & CLOSE
** Last Step
*** CANCEL, BACK, SAVE & CLOSE
* CANCEL - Cancels the interaction
* BACK - saves and goes to previous step
* NEXT - saves and proceeds to next step
* SAVE & CLOSE - Saves the record and returns to the previous screen before adding the item

## Acceptance Criteria
* Each of the three steps need to have the buttons listed in the requirements
* These buttons will be on the 'add new' screen
