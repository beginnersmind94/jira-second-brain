# NXT-18546 — Income Survey - View Survey Details

## Description
As a district admin, I would like to be able to view submitted Income Surveys, so that I can view individual details as needed.

*Summary*

Activate the details icon on the Income Surveys grid and allow people to view the details of the survey. -Then, allow users to view and edit individual details on the survey, and then save it.- {color:#ff991f}*This will be deferred to a separate story.*{color}

*Requirements*

* In Eligibility → Forms → Surveys, when a user clicks the ‘Details’ icon next to a survey, open a *Survey Details* page.
* Display the following sections that correspond to the Survey data (we can either display edit pencils for each section as disabled or hide them for now):
** Students
*** Display students who have been added to the survey along with their basic details
** Household
*** Display the household size and income frequency+ range/amount indicated on the survey
*** Otherwise, display the Assistance Program (SNAP, TANF, etc.) and Case Number
**** *(NOT PICTURED IN MOCK-UP but discussed in grooming:)* 
***** In place of ‘Household Size', display ‘Assistance Program’. The value would be SNAP, TANF, etc.
***** In place of ‘Household Income’, display ‘Case Number’. The value would be the 10(+/-) digit case number.
** Contact Info
*** Display the contact info indicated on the survey’s Contact Info step
** Details
*** Display the optional info from the Details step (*if* applicable--otherwise we can hide this section or display ‘N/A’ within it)
** Comments
*** Display comments left in the Survey and their details. 
**** We can wrap the text if needed so that the user can view the full comment of up to 250-500 chars.
*** Display the documents attached to the survey as a hyperlink that the user can view/download.
** Adult Signature
*** Display the signature status. If it is exists, show ‘Confirmed’. If not, show ‘Not Confirmed’.
** Side-navigation
*** Display Survey #, status (if applicable), Received Date, Language, and Survey Image (if it exists)
*** Display shortcut buttons to jump user to a given section on the page
*** Display ‘More Actions’: 
**** Mark as Pending Survey
**** Process Survey
**** Delete Survey
** Back Button
*** -Return to retain search filters- (Removed, since this is not currently possible) 
*** User should be able to go back to the Surveys grid when they are done viewing information about the selected survey.
* +*Note:*+ Edit pencil behavior, as well as saving a survey as pending or deleting it, will be handled in a future story.

## Acceptance Criteria
Verify that when the user goes to the Income Surveys grid, they can find individual surveys with Details icons

Verify that when selecting a Details icon, the user is redirected to the Survey Details for that particular survey

Verify that the Survey Details page displays individual sections with information corresponding to each step of the survey

Verify that the page also shows overall survey number and other details, as well as a side-nav to jump to individual sections on the page

Verify that all buttons/edit buttons/drop-downs are either shown and disabled OR hidden entirely

Verify that the user can go back to the Survey grid-, with any previous filters saved-
