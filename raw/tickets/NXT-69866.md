# NXT-69866 — Menu Analysis - Site Performance - Food Waste % Metric and Slide out

## Description
As a District Manager, I would love when I see the Food waste percentage, there was a way that I could see the breakdown of how the calculation is happening. 

!image-20260203-054857.png|width=916,alt="image-20260203-054857.png"!

## Acceptance Criteria
* Based on the new calculation revision done in this story ([https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-69861|https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-69861|smart-link] , update the calculations on the School Performance List page
* Provide an information Icon on Food Waste % column to open a slide out for the calculations
** The columns should include Site, Wasted Portions, Carryover to Next Service, Return to Stock (RTS), Served Portions and Food Waste % with a 50% page slideout
* When user clicks on a particular row, display the calculations at the bottom in the formula as to how it is calculated 
* Display the formula at the top of the slideout as a standard like on other slideouts
* Allow sorting within the slideout 
* Freeze Calculation at the bottom and formula at the top, also freeze header row and allow scrolling for the sites
