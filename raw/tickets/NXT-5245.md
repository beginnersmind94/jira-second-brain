# NXT-5245 — Direct Certification - Matched/Summary - Actions - Preview Letter  - [RTG]

## Description
User Story

As a district admin I want to preview a student’s notification letter for direct certification before I send it off to print.

*Mockup*

[https://www.figma.com/proto/yrkJeGfd3f9hWjqm0qvfir/Edge2.0_V1?node-id=133668%3A0&scaling=scale-down-width|https://www.figma.com/proto/yrkJeGfd3f9hWjqm0qvfir/Edge2.0_V1?node-id=133668%3A0&scaling=scale-down-width|smart-link] 

*Requirements*

* Add an action to the following grids:
** Direct Cert Matched (List)
** Direct Cert Summary (Students section)
* Action: Preview (letter icon?)
** Clicking this should launch a preview of a populated notification letter for the student.
** This would use the following letter template from the System module:
*** Direct Certification
*** The language will depend on the selected language of the student.
**** Student is marked Spanish, pull the Spanish letter.
**** If no template exists for that language, default to English.
** The preview should have an option to email or print this letter out.
* Template Fields will likely need to be linked up to populate appropriately.
** Fields:
*** Child Name
**** Student’s First Name + Last Name. Don’t forget the space.
*** City, State, Zip
**** Pull the city, state and postal code from the student’s primary guardian.
*** Street Address
**** Pull from the student’s primary guardian, include Apartment Number.
*** Current Date
**** Format: MM/DD/YYYY
*** Determining Official
*** Determining Official Address
*** Determining Official Email
*** Determining Official Phone Number
**** DO Data in System Module > Templates > SE Module
*** Disclaimer
*** District
*** District Address
*** District City, State, Zip
*** Guardian Name
**** First + Last. Pull from primary guardian of student. 
*** Guardian First Name
*** Guardian Last Name
*** Hearing Official
*** Hearing Official Address
*** Hearing Official Email
*** Hearing Official Phone Number
**** HO Data in System Module > Templates > SE Module
*** Homeroom
**** Current homeroom of the student from AcctMgt.
*** MedicaidText
*** SFA Correspondence Address
*** SFA Physical Address
*** Verifying Official
*** Verifying Official Address
*** Verifying Official Email
*** Verifying Official Phone Number
**** VO Data in System Module > Templates > SE Module
*** Youngest Child Grade
**** If multiple students are going to be on the letter, grab this from the youngest child (most recent date of birth)
*** Youngest Child School
**** If multiple students are going to be on the letter, grab this from the youngest child (most recent date of birth)
** Table Fields
*** Child Name
*** Current Status
*** Effective Date
*** Grade
*** Homeroom
*** Previous Status
*** Reason
*** School #
*** School Name
*** Student ID #
*** Student PIN #

## Acceptance Criteria
User can preview a direct cert approval letter.
Fields populate data appropriately. Format of the letter is correct.
User can dispatch the letter from the preview.
