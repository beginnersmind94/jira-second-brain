*Requirements*

* Information icon (text on hover) for non-pending students that have a basis for direct certification or already on an application for the program year
** Already on an application - _“This student is already on application #\[555] with \[free] status from \[start date]”_
*** Start Date will be *Application - Effective Date* based setting (Processed or Received)
** DC - _“Student is currently assigned \[free] eligibility through DC \[basis]”_
* CEP or P2 site indication > Only once the student is matched (not for pending students) show CEP or P2 sites in italics within the student grid and trigger message
** Show icon - _“This student is part of a CEP site with Free eligibility” or “This student is part of a P2 site with Free eligibility”_
** The entire site is ‘free’ eligibility (community eligibility program - special provision)
* \[already in design] Student Details > Current Eligibility 
** For Active/Inactive students matched in the DB, show the current eligibility for the student in the details (expand/collapse of popup)
* Add Checkbox “Confirmed Categorical Status” when Categorical question answered ‘Yes’
** Student grid expansion will show “Categorical Status: Homeless (Confirmed)” or (Unconfirmed)” based on checkbox selection
*** _exception: Foster reason will not have a confirmation checkbox. They do not require confirmation of eligibility status but all other categories do._
* \[no mockup] Duplicate check > active student and pending student are both in the student section 
** Check with next? Reference ID field if it’s the same?
** Show message - _Looks like a student was added twice on this application. Do you want to review the student information or proceed to the next step?_  
*** Review Information> stay on this page
*** Proceed to Next Step > Takes them to step 2

Settings

* Total Child Income
** *Enabled* > income section removed from student popup.
*** Split the table with students and household members 
*** Add a row and edit pencil for “Total Child Income”
**** Default is $0.00 and Annual 
**** Edit triggers a popup > _Enter the Total Child Income:_  Income amount field and frequency field
** Disabled > current process > add income for each student 
* Alaska Permanent Fund Dividend (PFD) 
** *Enabled* > add question to student popup > _“Please indicate if a PFD was approved for the following years.”_
*** Show current year and previous year with checkboxes (they don't have to select either) 
**** Checkbox - 2019 \[previous year]
**** Checkbox - 2020 \[current year]
*** Special income type for each year 
**** 2 years at a time (i.e. 19-20 changes each year)
** Displayed in grid for Household and Student sections
*** i.e. PFD Approved: 2019, 2020 
*** If neither year box is selected show > 'PFD Approved: No' in the grid
