**** always try to show the ‘Contains’ values first

***** e.g. ‘M/MA’ instead of ‘Meat/Meat Alternate' or ‘Meat/MA’
*** Set the outline color of the chip to match the Menu Planning food component colors
* These display approaches will be implemented on Recipes (and perhaps Items) upon completion (future)
