# NXT-32874 — MP- Menu Cycle Analysis- Pass or Fail indication for daily component contributions (Food Component Report)

## Description
As a Auditor for Analysis, I need to be able to see clear indications of whether Daily components are passing or failing 

Currently, we are indicating daily values as low, pass, high , however when an item is low or high, we are not indicating that it is passing or fail for the day. 

There needs to be a clear indication that there is a pass or fail on the daily component level.

## Acceptance Criteria
* Display Currently marked “low” as Fail with red indicator and Fail verbiage on a per day basis
* Display Currently Marked “pass” as Pass with Green Indicator and Pass Verbiage on a per day basis
* Remove Low From legend as a an indicator on food component report
* DO NOT Indicate any coloring for Fruit Juice on the daily level as there are no daily requirements for fruit Juice
* DO NOT Indicate any coloring for veg Juice on the daily level as there are no daily requirements for veg Juice
* DO NOT Indicate any coloring for Whole Grain Rich or Grain Based Dessert as there are no daily requirements for either
* *DO NOT indicate any coloring for any vegetable sub-components since there is no daily requirements for them. They are only checked on the weekly level (Pass if whole week passes the weekly requirement or fail if does not meet the weekly requirement)*
** The pass or fail for the sub components does not affect the pass or fail of the Total Veg component
* DO NOT indicate any coloring for any milk that has no daily requirements such as: 
** Skim/Fat free, flavored
** Low Fat (1% or less) flavored
** Reduced fat (2% fat) or whole, unflavored and flavored 
* DO indicate coloring for pass or fail on:
** Varieties of Milk
** Unflavored Low fat or Skim Milk Varieties
** Fluid Milk (cups) 
* REMOVE DAILY PASS/FAIL ROW and Only indicate pass or fail on the day header
* Remove the 1b association with all components. There is no association with components when it comes to sodium. 
* The 4 association should only relate to Grains, WGR and Fluid Milk and all milk sub categories
