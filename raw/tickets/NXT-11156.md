# NXT-11156 — SchoolCafe Integration - Eligibility application synchronization - Batch support

## Description
+*Acceptance criteria*+

* If SchoolCafe or Perseus are unavailable to be synchronized for a period of time, there shall be a mechanism to synchronize large amounts of eligibility applications once the 2 systems are available.
** This mechanism shall be performant and scalable.
