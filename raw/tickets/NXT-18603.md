# NXT-18603 — IM - Recipe Nutrition Report - need Percent of calories from Macronutrients

## Description
The Recipe Nutrition Report already exists, but per the USDA Requirements for software approval, is missing some specific data.

It is a requirement to display the Percent of Calories from the Macronutrients (Protein, Carbohydrate, Total Fat, and Saturated Fat.)

The percentage of calories from protein, carbohydrate, total fat, and saturated fat for the recipe should be calculated as follows:

Protein = 4 Calories per gram
Carbohydrate = 4 Calories per gram
Fat = 9 Calories per gram
Saturated Fat = 9 Calories per gram

Multiply the total grams of protein by four and divide by the total calories. This total equals the percentage of calories from protein.
Multiply the total grams of carbohydrate by four and divide by the total calories. This total equals the percentage of calories from carbohydrates.
Multiply the total grams of fat by nine and divide by the total calories. This total equals the percentage of calories from fat.
Multiplн the total grams of saturated fat by nine and divide by the total calories. This total equals the percentage of calories from saturated fat. 

NOTE: The total of the first three percentages (protein, carbohydrate, and fat) should approximate but may not equal 100%
