# NXT-18547 — Income Survey - Survey Details - Notify Household

## Description
As a district admin, I would like to be able to notify households that their Income Survey has been received/accepted, so that I can have a paper trail to close the communication loop.

*Summary*

Allow a user to email and/or print an Income Survey Notice for any completed survey

*Requirements*

* When the user goes to Eligibility → Forms → Surveys → ‘Survey Details’, display a ‘Notify’ button.
** This button should only be enabled if the survey is in ‘Processed’ or ‘Notified’ status.
* When the user selects this button, open the standard Notify drawer and display the options to print or email the Survey Notice.
** The user should be able to send a notice.
** The email option should only be selectable if the Contact Info for the survey includes an email address.
* When the notification is sent out (via print/email options): 
** Close the drawer
** Display a success message: “Survey Notice sent successfully\!”

## Acceptance Criteria
Verify that a ‘Notify’ button is added to the Survey Details page

Verify that when selected, the button opens the Notification drawer

Verify that a user can print or email a ‘Survey Notice’ for the survey

Verify that when the user prints or emails that notice, the print dialog opens and/or the email goes out.
