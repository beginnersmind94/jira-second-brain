# NXT-5646 — INV - Add Vendor Information

## Description
*User Story*

As an inventory user, I want to be able to add Vendor information

*Mockups*

!image-20200928-141339.png|width=100%!



[https://material-ui.com/components/selects/|https://material-ui.com/components/selects/]

*Requirements*

* Vendor Number - MANDATORY - Alphanumeric
** Minimum length - 1
** Special characters allowed - Hyphen ( - )
* Vendor Name - MANDATORY - Text
* Vendor ID - not visible on UI (backend vendor identifier by datasource) - Unique identifier for the vendor - Cannot be changed after creation.
* Vendor Status - Radial Button
** Active (Default)
** Inactive
** (database purpose only) Suspended (Not available to select for new vendor)
* Datasource - MANDATORY - dropdown - Can’t be changed after the record is created
** Local (Default)
** Shared
** Global
* Preferred Ordering Mode - Dropdown - Default will be -
** Phone
** Email
** Fax
** E-Ordering
** Website
* Minimum Order Amount
** $ amount with 2 decimals
* Maximum Order Amount
** $ amount with 2 decimals
* Vendor Lead Time
** Number in Days
* Production Order Delivery Lead Time
** Number in Days
* Strategic Partner
** Strategic
** Non-Strategic (Default)
* Vendor Type - Optional
** Dropdown - Default as blank - Default sorting from A-Z
*** Dry Good Supplier
*** Kitchen Supplier
*** Office Supplier
*** Service Provider
*** Meat Supplier
*** Product Supplier
*** Baked Goods Supplier
*** Processor
*** Distributor
*** Broker
*** Manufacturer
*** Vending
* Comments - Comments if any…
** Free form multiline text
** 100 character limit

*Rules: Assuming local datasource (or same datasource)*

# No duplicate vendor Vendor ID allowed per datasource
# No Duplicate Vendor Name allowed per datasource
# No Duplicate vendor numbers per datasource
# No vendor record can be deleted
# Once a vendor is created, can’t change the datasource

The vendor status are: Active, Inactive, Suspended.

When clicking NEXT, it will create the vendor record in the database and move to step 2.

## Acceptance Criteria
User will be able to add Vendor Info to the first step
