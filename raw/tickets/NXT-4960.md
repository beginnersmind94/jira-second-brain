*Notes from another item:*

* The side-nav links are meant to take the user to the Split View search page, or at least that was my understanding based on how the Item Management module has done theirs.
** I added a comment about this on 8/18 in this story.
* As it stands, you end up with some weird nav happening.
** Click into Account Management, you’re on the Home > Patrons page. You can click on Rosters from the side-nav and it takes you to Home > Rosters tab. You can click on Patrons in the side-nav and it takes you to Home > Patrons tab.
** Do a Patron Search. You get taken to Split View.
** Click on Rosters on the side-nav, and you go back to Home > Rosters tab.
** Click on Patrons, and you’re taken back to Split View with your search results, and no search query.
** Rosters behaves the same way if a search is done. I am guessing this has something to do with the way nav is handled, which may indeed be resolved once 5150 is taken care of?
