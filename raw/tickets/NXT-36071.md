# NXT-36071 — IM - Recipe Rework - Steps & Ingredients - View Steps

## Description
As a Nutritionist I want the Recipes page to have a dedicated Steps & Ingredients tab so I can make all the recipe preparation changes in one place.

[Figma - View/Add/Edit Steps|https://www.figma.com/proto/OAZ7qTRgdG5bFFGuhDmkoZ/Item-Management-6.0?page-id=3238%3A54123&node-id=3315%3A60028&viewport=411%2C-15%2C0.03&scaling=min-zoom&starting-point-node-id=3315%3A60028&show-proto-sidebar=1]

*Requirements*

* Create a Steps & Ingredients tab to house all the recipe steps
** Enable this tab once a Description, Serving Size and Number of Servings are provided and saved in the summary card
* Provide a left aligned card for housing the Recipe steps
** By default, show the steps and Directions as the main rows in the Steps card
*** Directions will be the primary visual of the step
** Show the step ingredients in an expandable/collapsible sub-section
*** Allow the step ingredients to be expanded only for one Step at a time (a report will be given for all steps)
** Provide an ‘Edit Steps' (or Edit Recipe, Edit Recipe Steps, etc.) action on the card
*** this will enable the Edit Steps mode ([NXT-36358|https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-36358])
* Show a right side Ingredients card when in view mode
** This will house all ingredients added to the recipe regardless of Step
** The quantity will be the sum of the ingredient across the whole recipe (by default)
*** Add a label or icon indicating that this is the *Total* amount for the recipe
** Scale quantity to higher units for the purpose of recipe total
*** this can also be used for the updated Scale Recipe report, which currently is not scaling correctly

## Acceptance Criteria
* Display a left aligned card for housing the Recipe steps
* Display a right side Ingredients card that dynamically shows ingredients and quantities based on selected steps
