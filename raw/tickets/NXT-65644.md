# NXT-65644 — INV - Internal Vendors - Ordering Conditions - Indicate Unassigned sites

## Description
As an Inventory Distribution Manager I want the Ordering Conditions to indicate when an item is in the Unassigned delivery group so I can know when sites won’t be able to order from the internal vendor.

## Acceptance Criteria
* Update the *Vendor >* *Ordering Conditions* to warn about the Unassigned delivery group and associated sites
** Do not allow sites in the Unassigned group to order, but do NOT clear the ordering conditions
** Show an alert icon next to the checkbox
*** Tooltip: “Site is in the Unassigned delivery group for this internal vendor. Assign site to Delivery Group to restore ordering.”
* Update the *Configuration >* *Ordering Conditions* to warn about the sites in Unassigned delivery group
** Show the alert icon if the internal vendor is selected for any sites which are Unassigned
*** Tooltip: “Site is in the Unassigned delivery group for this internal vendor. Assign site to Delivery Group to restore ordering, or change vendor.”
* Both warnings are *soft stop* and user will still be able to save
