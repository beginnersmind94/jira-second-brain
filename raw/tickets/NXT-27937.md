# NXT-27937 — INV - Vendors - Items - Commodity Contract Items

## Description
As a FSD I want a modified Contract Items screen for Commodity Contracts so I can manually adjust the inventory quantities.

*Requirements*

+Change the Vendor Contract configuration+ (NXT-1839)

+Modify the Contract Items screen+

* Show Available Quantity for Ordering to the Contract Items list for Commodity contracts
** Current QOH (Quantity on Hand)

** Adjustment - *only in Adjust mode*
*** cannot open Contract Item side panel when in Adjust mode
*** probably display all items without pagination in Adjust mode
** Available Quantity (Current QOH - Open Orders +/- Adjustment)
* Provide an adjustment feature to manually adjust the available quantity
** this adds an adjustment column in between Open Orders and Available Quantity
* Side panel / Add Item to Contract changes
** Vendor Item # is not required for Commodity Contracts
** cannot open Contract Item side panel when in Adjust mode
