# NXT-38091 — IM - Items - Units - Inventory Readiness Banner Enhancement

## Description
As an Item Management user I want the Inventory Readiness banner to guide me to corrective actions so I can use a single tool to manage all inventory readiness related actions.

*Requirements*

* Provide multiple banner statuses and actions related to Inventory Readiness, in the approach taken for the [Pack-Serving Link Help story|https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-37982]
* +Inventory Ready statuses and available actions+
* *Inventory Ineligible* (actions covered in Pack Serving Link story)
** +Pack & Servings not linked+ 
*** Change banner message (to original story requirement): “Servings and pack units must be linked for Inventory eligibility”
**** this is what shows _before_ user clicks Edit
**** Show indicators on the relevant fields (highlight relevant fields _before_ edit mode)
*** Show the more detailed message after Edit:
**** if no more pack sizes can be added: ‘Please enter weight for [Pack] or sub-quantity for [Serving]’ 
**** (do not shorten words, i.e. ‘sub-qty’)
*** Remove improper tooltip with duplicate text
** +Pack sizes not present+ - no changes needed 
* *Inventory Incomplete* 
** Missing Costing 
*** Change ‘Missing Cost’ banner tooltip to ‘Missing Costing’
*** Change banner to correct pre-Edit message: “Please enter fair market value for Inventory and Production costing”
*** Show the more detailed message after Edit: “Please add costing for the most relevant unit.”
** Item categorization not configured
*** Display this banner in the _Summary_ card instead of Units
*** 'Configure item categories for Inventory eligibility”
**** Show indicators on the relevant fields (highlight relevant fields _before_ edit mode)
* *Inventory Ready*
** Use the Inventory Ready flag to call any the Base Unit confirmation/promotion (“Inventory Pre-Ready”)
*** Consider the Base Unit confirmation as a part of Publish 
*** Base Unit confirmation should *not* be executed on click of card level Save button
** Include the Decimals setting as part of base unit confirmation
*** Change the question or the answer (yes or no does not make sense for BU prompt)
** Enable Publish flag
*** Default inventory ready publish to on
