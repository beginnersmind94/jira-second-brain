*Notes:*

* This is a continuation to Part 1
* In this part, we are doing Add and Delete functionality
* Clone will be done in a separate story

*Requirements*:

Updates to R&P Dashboard:

* For the System > Roles & Permissions landing page, do the following:

* Complete the Delete button on the rows
** Verify there are no users assigned to the role first

** Do the usual confirmation
*** If confirm, then remove the role for this district only
