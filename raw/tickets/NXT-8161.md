# NXT-8161 — Accountability - Reports - Add Site Balance report

## Description
*User Story*

As a district admin I want to see how the district stands as far as overall account balance is concerned so that I can answer the question, “are we positive, or carrying a lot of student debt?”

*Mockup*

[https://www.figma.com/proto/Yo99YhQm5Xk91Ie7b0PRkD/Front-Office-6.0?node-id=1974%3A21431&scaling=scale-down-width&page-id=1974%3A21430&starting-point-node-id=1974%3A21431|https://www.figma.com/proto/Yo99YhQm5Xk91Ie7b0PRkD/Front-Office-6.0?node-id=1974%3A21431&scaling=scale-down-width&page-id=1974%3A21430&starting-point-node-id=1974%3A21431|smart-link] 

*Requirements*

* -Add a Reports landing page to Account Management (if we haven’t already done so--also, reports should continue being listed in alphabetical order).- 
* Add a report to the Accountability → Reports page called *Site Balance*
** Description: _Provides a summary of account balances per site on a selected date._
* Report has the following options:
** User can filter by Area (if we’ve integrated Areas yet)
** User can select sites, and a date.
** User can select if they want to include the special accounts.
*** Display a note at the bottom explaining the effects of checking this box.
* Add a report that shows the user a summary of account balances, broken out by:
** Account Type (Student, Adult)
** Positive balances
*** Sum of all balances > 0.00 in the given site, for the given account Type first, and then for both Students and Adults combined 
** Negative balances
*** Sum of all balances < 0.00 in the given site, for the given account Type, and then for both Students and Adults combined  
** Net balance
*** The difference between the Positive and Negative balances in the given site, for the given account Type first, and then for both Students and Adults combined 
** Average net balance 
*** Net balance divided by the number of Student accounts only
** Report should display the (grand) Total as well.
* Negative balances will be parentheses, i.e. Negative $5 would show as *($5.00)*
* Report should be exportable to Excel or PDF.
* Add a tooltip to the footer of the report: *Note*: *When the option to include special accounts is selected, the* *_Total_* *row may also include outstanding balances from special accounts who do not belong to an individual site.*

## Acceptance Criteria
Reports landing page added.

Site Balance report added.

User can select sites, date, and area (optional), and if they want to include special accounts.

* Site dropdown is dependent on what sites the user has access to.
* Default date: current date.
* Default status: All
* Include special accounts

Report added showing information for each site on the selected date.

* Information is broken down by site and patron type, showing: 
** sum of positive balances
** sum of negative balances
** net balance (add the 2 together)
** average net balance (divide the net balance by the number of students on that date per status)
* Report also shows the account balance totals - adding the Students and Adults together.
* Each column has a total at the bottom.

Report can be exported out to Excel or PDF.

Footer of the report includes this tooltip: *Note*: *When the option to include special accounts is selected, the* *_Total_* *row may also include outstanding balances from special accounts who do not belong to an individual site.*
