*requirements*

* Add a ‘Payments’ tab to the Accountability > Reports page.
* User can make use of standard search features for the report:
** Area / Sites / Grades / Homerooms
** Date Selection dropdown:
*** Today, Last 7 Days, Last 30 Days, Year to Date, Custom Date Range
**** Custom Date Range will let the user pick a start and end date.
* Additional search options / filters.
** Report Type
*** Summary, Detailed
** Patron Type
*** Adults or Students
**** Checkboxes for both, selected by default.
**** This should check the account type to see if we’re to return just students or just adults or everybody.
** Payment Type
*** Cash, Check, Card, Online
**** Checkboxes, all selected by default.
**** Filters to the selected payment methods.
** Ordering
*** Date, Patron
**** This will be used in the report for whether it should be ordered by Transaction Date first or by Patron first.

*Report requirements for Summary*

* Payments should be grouped by:
** Area
** Site
* Order by:
** Area
** Site
* Data columns to show:
** Site
** Payment Type
*** Cash
*** Check
*** Online
*** Card
** Total Amount for each site
** Additional totals to show:
*** Area Total
*** Date Total
*** Grand Total down each column
