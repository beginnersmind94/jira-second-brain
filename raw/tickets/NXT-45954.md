# NXT-45954 — PR- Plan- Food Required Display Rules

## Description
As a Production Manager, I would like to be able to see my food required at all times for all my items that I am responsible to make.

## Acceptance Criteria
* Food Required should be *displayed* for ALL prepared and non prepared items
** Food Required should be *displayed* for prepared items ONLY IF prep site and receiving site are the same
** Food Required should be *hidden* for prepared items IF prep site and receiving site are different
