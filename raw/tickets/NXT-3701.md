## Acceptance Criteria
* Per Serving size inputs are numerical
* ‘Missing Value' flag showing fields that doesn’t have any information filled out 
* Nutrient Information can be entered for an Item for a specific serving measure.
* Serving measures allowed are: 1. Weights 2. Any Unit specified within the Serving Measures.
* Serving measure Qty can be a fraction, decimal or multiple.
* Serving nutrient values can be a decimal.
* Every nutrient can be entered for its appropriate measurement (g, mg, mcg, kCals, or IU).
* Some nutrients (Iron 100%=18, Calcium 100%=1000, Vit A 100%=5000, C 100%=60, D 100%=20, and Potassium 100%=4700) can be entered via %DRV and the nutrient qty is calculated dynamically.
* For specific nutrients, when qty is entered %DRV is calculated dynamically.
* Most nutrients can be indicated as "missing" or "no value" (exclusions: Calories, Total Fat, Sat Fat, Carbs).
* % of Calories is displayed for Fat, Sat Fat, Trans Fat, Carb, and Protein (4 kCals/gram carb or protein and 9 kCals/gram any Fat).
* Per 100 grams column is calculated and displayed dynamically based on the weight of the serving measure.
** %Daily Value, % of calories, Per 100 gram will autofill based off of serving size and per serving calculation (more to come)
