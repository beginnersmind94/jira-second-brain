## Acceptance Criteria
* Redesign the Contributions card to more closely resemble the Allergens card
* List each applicable component on a dedicated row within the card
** Follow the sequence: Fruits, MMA, Grains, Milk, Veg
* Display the component icon circle for each applicable contribution
* Display the specific contributions in chips within the row
* Color the contribution chips accordingly
** Outline: 100% opacity; inside: 50% capacity
** for everything except Veg, use the color of the food component for the chip color
** for veg, use the following colors:
*** Dark Green: 009900
*** Red/Orange: FF8761
*** Beans, Peas & Lentils: 9966FF
*** Starchy: 82D5FA
*** Other: 92D050
*** Additional Vegetables: CCC0DA
* Color the variety (Fruit Juice, Vegetable Juice, Grain Based Dessert) as follows:
**  Outline: component color; inside: F4F7FA
* Use different icons for Cups vs. Ounce Equivalent
** [Material Icon link for scale icon|https://fonts.google.com/icons?selected=Material+Symbols+Outlined:scale:FILL@1;wght@400;GRAD@0;opsz@24&icon.query=scale&icon.size=24&icon.color=%235f6368] to use for Oz. Eq.
* Also update the Menu Items list Contributions column to use the same styling for the chips
** the list page uses the *short version* of the chips
** e.g. “1 WGR” instead of “1 Whole Grain Rich”
** Update the Milk chips to only use one chip (combine Fluid and Variety into a single chip)
