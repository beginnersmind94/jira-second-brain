# NXT-37345 — Elig - Verification - Updates for California to include extensions in MP-1 and MP-2

## Description
As John the California Director I need to include the number of extended students in sections MP-1 and MP-2 so I can report the numbers to the state.

!image-20230308-163854.png|width=831,height=456!

*requirements*

* For the California collection worksheet, we need to add the following:
** Confirm that Section 3-3 includes the DC-Medicaid Free students - if not, add.
** MP-1 needs a 2nd count, which is the number of students who have received an extension of DC-Medicaid Free benefits.
*** This count also needs to be included in section 3-3 for the California version of the report only.
*** Update text: MP-1: Students directly certified through Medi-Cal for FREE meals, and the number of benefit extensions:
*** Add the 2nd count in a 2nd cell next to the 1st count.
**** Example: | 49 | 81 |  
**** If we’re already including them in 3-3, make no additional changes. Do not double count the extensions.
*** User can click through the extension number to see the students who made up that count.
** MP-2 needs a 2nd count, the number of students who have received an extension of DC-Medicaid Reduced benefits.
*** This count is *not* to be included in any counts in section 3.
*** Update text: MP-2: Students directly certified through Medi-Cal for REDUCED-PRICE Meals and the number of benefit extensions:
*** Add the 2nd count in a 2nd cell next to the 1st count.
*** User can click through the extension number to see the students who made up that count.

## Acceptance Criteria
* Section 3-3 includes DC-Medicaid Free counts
* MP-1 and MP-2 updated
** Text updated
** 2nd count for number of extensions available for both MP-1 and MP-2
** User can click through to see the student details.
