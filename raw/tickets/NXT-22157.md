# NXT-22157 — INV - Vendor - Items - Changes to Add Item

## Description
As a FSD I need the Add Item screen updated to ensure all the right information is provided for contract items.

*Requirements*

* On the new Items tab, move the Add Item button to the top of the Contract Item List grid
** this will be a permission resource
* Open the Add Item screen on click of Add Item button
** this will remain under the Items tab of the Vendor, but the Add Item screen will replace what is under the tab
** Show the Contract # in the page title, e.g. ‘Add Item to Contract #JM-91283’
* Provide the same page sections but update any necessary components
** Size the columns appropriately
*** Item # should be roughly 12-14 characters wide
*** Item Description should have more width
*** Unit Type should be no bigger than the width of ‘Display Shipper’
**** with the rarity of ‘Display Shipper’ and ‘Mixed Pallet’ we could just size this column for ‘Package’ and wrap the text for DS and MX
*** GTIN numbers are exactly 14 characters
* Add the ‘Additional Details’ expand sub-row section to the item grid (dependency: [NXT-7241|https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-7241])
** Weight
** Brand Name
** Manufacturer Name
** Fair Market / Standard Price
* Update the side panel
** section should be named ‘Contract Item Information’
** no Pricing button needed 
** Delivery Fee, Processing Fee and Net Off Invoice should be listed after Purchase Price
** Vendor Item # should still be mandatory
** Purchase Price is still mandatory
** button should be ‘Add Item to Contract’
*** only enabled after a Vendor Item # and Purchase Price have been populated
**** highlight required fields on button click if they are not populated
* Provide a ‘Back to Contract Items’ button
