# NXT-60901 — INV - Distribution - Assign & Pick - Available Quantity

## Description
As a distribution manager I want the Assignment and other Distribution screens to provide an option for me to see my QOH with Committed Quantity, so I can see what I have alongside what I have already committed.

* Projected QOH, using In Transit

## Acceptance Criteria
* Add an icon to view the Available Quantity details on Assign & Pick
* Provide the popup to see the QOH breakdown (with Committed, In Transit, etc.)
* Update popup based on Assignment vs. Picking status
** use Projected QOH for Assignment
** use Actual QOH for Picking
* Modify the other QOH fly-out to align the values
** Update the labels to replace “QOH” with Quantity
** Display ‘Quantity Available’ instead of ‘Quantity Actual’ on the Distribution
