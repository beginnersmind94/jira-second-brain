# NXT-70067 — FIN - Failed Job Prioritization & Attention Routing

## Description
As a CN Director at a large district, I want failed jobs to surface immediately so I don't have to scroll through hundreds of completed jobs to find the ones that need fixing.

## Acceptance Criteria
* Failed jobs are pinned to the top of the job history table regardless of sort order
* A "Needs Attention" divider row appears above the first failed job; a "Recent Jobs" divider appears below the last failed job, before completed/running jobs
* Dividers only render when failed jobs exist; invisible when all jobs are healthy
* The "Failed" filter chip shows a red badge with the count of failed jobs (e.g., "Failed 2")
* Clicking the Needs Attention summary card scrolls to the table and activates the Failed filter
* Expanded failed job detail panels show: error message, a direct help line ("This one needs a fix before it's audit-ready. Here's what to do →"), and a Resolve Issue link
