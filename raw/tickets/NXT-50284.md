# NXT-50284 — PR- Receiving Sites- Do not consider Strict Batching on Received Counts (UI ONLY)

## Description
As a production manager, If I received less than a full batch because of damage or something happening at the prep site, the system should not force me to enter in multiples of my batch size on my received quantity.

## Acceptance Criteria
* Received quantity should not consider strict batch rules
* Users must still enter strict batch on their plan quantities
