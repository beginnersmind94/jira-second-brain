*requirements*

Track history for any action taken during Verification.

* User has a way to view an audit trail of actions for each academic year.
* History action available on the following pages:
** Sampling
** Tracking

*Sampling*

* Track the actions taken, what data changed if any, the user, and when.
** Actions:
*** Scheduled
*** Deleted
*** Add Date
*** -Recalculated-
**** -Need to track the outcome of this -- 
***** -did the number of apps change?- 
***** -Which apps moved in or out?-
***** -Did any apps move to Cause as a result?-
*** Sites Edited
**** Need to track which sites are added / removed as part of this.
** The Changed Value(s) information will be in an expander.
** History button available at all times.
** 75% drawer.

*Tracking*

* In-line history button that shows a log of every action taken for that application.
* Show the action taken, who, and when: 
* Actions: 
** Verification Confirmation
** -Sent First Notice-
** -Sent Follow-up Notice-
** Verification Complete as No Response
** Verification Completed
** -Sent Change Notice-
** -Sent Completed Notice-
** Verification Deleted
** Verification Deleted and Replaced
** Verification Rollback

*Verification Details*

* Rename ‘Application History’ to ‘Notifications and Comments’



Pagination - if more than 10, use pagination.

## Acceptance Criteria
History of actions available on the Sampling page.

History of actions available in-line on the Tracking pages.

‘Application History’ renamed to ‘Notifications and Comments’ on the Verification Details page.
