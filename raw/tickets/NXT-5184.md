Requirements

HACCP Process

No Cook \[DEFAULT] | Save day Service | Complex Food

Selection: ‘For Entire Recipe’ \[DEFAULT] or ‘For each step’

* When selecting ‘For Entire Recipe’ then the steps will show below as a table
** CCP | Critical Limit | Corrective Action
** Depends on the selection Above
*** No Cook
**** Line 1
***** CCP - ex. Cold Hold
***** Critical Limit - Hold at <=41 degrees °F (Not user editable, can be a label)
***** Corrective Action (*User text field*) - Discard the food if it cannot be determined how long the food temperature was above “41” (*user input field*) °F.
**** Line 2
***** CCP - ex. CRTE
***** Critical Limit - Chill prepared food from 70 degrees F to <= “41” (*user input field*) degrees F within 4 hours
***** Corrective Action (*User text field*) - Use limits in classic for this
*****# Use a different cooling method for prepared ready-to-eat foods when the food is above 41°F and less than 4 hours into the cooling process.  2. Discard prepared ready-to-eat foods when the food is above 41°F and more than 4 hours into the cooling process.
*** Same day Service

** Configuration handled in a separate story
** The values for in Classic to draw text from are Process 1: No Cook, Process 2: Same-day service, and Process 3: Complex Food
* When selecting ‘For each Step’ then the table will be hidden (How this looks will be handled in [https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-5746|https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-5746|smart-link] )
* If switching between ‘For Entire Recipe’ and ‘For each Step’ the inputs should be ‘saved’ in the database but not ‘active’. Similar concept to Allergens in Item Management 
* No need for negative value for temperature at this time, and no more than 4 digits before the decimal
