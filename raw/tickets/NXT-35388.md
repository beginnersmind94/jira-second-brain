# NXT-35388 — Password Reset - Update wording for deactivated accounts

## Description
While closing out https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-34879, I noticed some wording that needs to be improved in a couple of places.

First, on the login page, if the account is disabled, there is a red text error message that reads “Your user account has been disactivated. Please reach out to administrator to activate your account.” I would like to change this verbiage to: “Your account has been disactivated. Please click the “Forgot Password?” link and follow the reset process or reach out to your administrator to activate your account.“

 !image-2022-12-02-09-09-14-371.png|thumbnail! 

Second, After the reset process is done, the verbiage on the last page had me confused. In the green area, can you change the verbiage from “An email with instructions to reset your password will be sent to your registered email account.” to “An email with instructions to reset your password has been sent to your registered email.”
