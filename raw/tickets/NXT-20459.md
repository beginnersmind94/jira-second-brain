# NXT-20459 — System - Customer Onboarding - UI/UX enhancements to complete the feature

## Description
As a Customer Success mentor, I would like to be able to track my district users' progress on SchoolCafé onboarding, so that I can anticipate their needs and help them more quickly complete onboarding without having to contact me.

*Summary*

Make some UI/UX improvements to the Customer Onboarding page, so that it is more useful for Success team members to use after district creation and onboarding have begun.

*Mockup*

(Multiple--see below)

*Requirements*

* In System → Customer Onboarding, make the following visual enhancements and checks:
** District Operations card:
*** Change ‘District Operations’ title to ‘District Tasks’
*** Change ‘Activities’ column header to ‘Task Name’
*** Change ‘No of Completition’ column header to ‘Entries’
**** Additionally, remove the hover tooltip as well, since it should no longer be required
** Onboarding Status card:
*** Change label alignment from center to left-aligned, for better readability
*** Move the donut chart to the top of the card, for consistency with donut charts in other pages. Increase the size of the donut if possible.
*** (*Possible API* *change*) Verify that ‘Products’ is being updated. It should not show when there are either no licenses or there are 0 licenses assigned.
*** Change the status labels from:
**** PRODUCTS → Products Assigned
**** DISTRICT OPERATIONS → District Task
**** Settings → Settings - Custom
**** IMPORTS → Imports & Exports
**** Make sure that the related labels above (beneath donut chart) are updated as well. See mock-up:

!image-20230509-170925.png|width=90.08264462809917%!
** Products card:
*** Change ‘Module Name' column header to 'License Name’
*** Change ‘No of Licenses’ column header to ‘Assigned’
**** (*Possible API* *change*) Instead of total license count, display assigned license count
** Settings card
*** Change ‘Type’ column header to ‘Values’

* Imports card
** Change ‘Imports’ title to ‘Imports & Exports’
** Change ‘Students’ column header to ‘Type’
** Change ‘Status’ column header to ‘Latest Status’
