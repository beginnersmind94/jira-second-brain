# NXT-65487 —  View POS Postings

## Description
*As a* district finance user,
*I need* to post monthly POS revenue to the general ledger,
*so that* transactions are accurately recorded for financial reporting.

## Acceptance Criteria
* Create a table with these columns:
** *Site Name*
** *Month*
** *Total Revenue*
** *Total Transactions*
** *Status* (Not Ready, Ready to Post, Posted, Error)
** *Actions* (Post, View, Repost, Remove)
* Selecting a site displays a summary of various POS transactions.
