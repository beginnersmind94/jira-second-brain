# NXT-1740 — INV - Withdrawals - Create Withdrawal - Selections (Step 1)

## Description
As a Site Manager I want a Create Withdrawal page so I can create a new withdrawal transaction.

This is the first step in the Withdrawal process.

*Requirements*

* Provide a Create button on the Withdrawals list page
** This will open the Withdrawal stepper
* Use a stepper component for this process
** utilize the horizontal stepper
** Title the first step ‘Selections’
* Provide the following inputs on this first step
** Site selection dropdown(s)
*** this will be the standard site selection control which includes Area if they are configured for the district
** Withdrawal Type dropdown
*** mandatory, single selection
*** options (default to ‘Select’)
**** Standard
**** Production
**** POS A La Carte
** Transaction Date and Time pickers
*** auto-populated with the current date and time
** Reason dropdown 
*** this will list the reasons which are configured in the Withdrawal Reasons page ([NXT-1674|https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-1674])
*** default to ‘Select’ in dropdown
*** mandatory selection
** Comments textbox
*** *optional
*** allow up to 200 characters
*** display character limit
* Provide a Next button via the stepper tool 
** this will progress to the second step ([NXT-1675|https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-1675])

*Mockup Note:* Area is always before Site
