## Acceptance Criteria
The List of CEP and Provision 2 configurations are shown in a collapsed form
The line item can be expanded showing the card view
The raw data can be viewed by selecting the view icon
Once entered the configuration can't be changed. a new configuration must be created to change the percentages for a site(s)

The card shows the Site, Start, Stop, Identified Student Analysis, and Claiming percentages
If grouped the  Qualified %, Free% and Paid % per group. with a list of the sites in the group
