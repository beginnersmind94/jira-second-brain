# NXT-65483 — Income Statement Pt. 4 Expenses

## Description
*As a* district finance administrator
*I need* to view all operating expenses for the current, prior, and year-to-date periods in the Profit & Loss report
*So that* I can monitor costs, compare them against prior periods, and reconcile them to the general ledger.

## Acceptance Criteria
* *Columns to Display*
** Current Period
** Prior Period
** Current CPM (Cost per Meal)
** Prior CPM
** Current % of Revenue
** % Change from Prior Period
** Year-to-Date Total
** YTD CPM
** Item % of Category
* *Expense Categories*
** *Inventory-related expenses* (e.g., Cleaning Supplies, Supplies) from *Inventory Posting* module.
** *Payroll expenses* (e.g., Professional Salaries, Wages) from *payroll imports* or manual GL postings.
** *Purchased goods and services* (e.g., Professional Services, Operational Supplies) from *Accounts Payable* when invoices are posted to GL expense accounts.
** *Miscellaneous expenses* from *manual journal entries* or adjustments posted to expense codes.
* *Data Sources*
** General Ledger postings for accounts classified as “Expense” in the Chart of Accounts.
** Only include postings within the selected fiscal period that are in *open financial periods*.
** Group and display expenses by GL account code and description.
