# NXT-9311 — Verification - Tracking - Completed Applications

## Description
*User Story*

As a district admin I want to see the applications which were completed as part of the verification sample.

*Requirements*

Add a 3rd tab to the Tracking page.

* Third tab: Completed Applications
** Grid is populated by applications that are ‘Verification Complete’ for the selected academic year.
** Grid columns, typical search and filter options:
*** Application #
*** Eligibility
*** Basis
*** Result
**** This would be the outcome from verification - Paid to Reduced, No Change, etc.
*** Type
**** This will be either: Sampled or Cause
*** Confirmation
**** This is a placeholder for future functionality.
*** Selection Date
**** Date the app was added to verification sample.
*** 1st Notice
**** Date/time for when the 1st Notice was successfully created.
*** Follow Up Notice
**** Date/time for when the 2nd Notice was successfully created.
*** Completion Notice
**** Date/time for when the Completion Notice was successfully created.
*** Completion Date
**** Date/time for when the app was marked as Verification Complete, be it via No Response or the response flow.
*** Additional Columns (should be hidden by default, but can be made visible)
**** Student Names
***** single cell containing the name(s) of all students on the application
**** Applicant
***** applicant first + last name
**** Contact Information
***** contact information provided by the applicant
***** street address, phone numbers, email
* Add to the Notify options:
** Change / Completion Notice
** This is only available for applications that are ‘Verification Complete’d.
* Show the user how many of each letter are available to send.
** Change / Completion Notice (3)
* Depending on the selection, a different letter template needs to be used:
** Selection Notice → Verification Notice
** Follow-up Notice → Verification Subsequent Notice (can we rename this to Follow-up Notice?)
** Change / Completion Notice → If eligibility did not change → Verification Complete Notice, else → Verification Change Notice
* After making a selection, the typical Notify drawer continues.
* User can change their letter selection and counts should update appropriately.

## Acceptance Criteria
Complete Applications tab added.
Grid shows information by academic year, current AY by default.
Grid shows applications that are currently complete for verification.
Grid shows information for selected applications per the requirements.
