# NXT-22841 — INV - Physical Inventory - Display label for Interim inventories

## Description
As a District Admin I want the Period column to show the details of Interim inventories so I can see when the inventory was done without opening it.

*Requirements*

* On the Physical Inventory list page, in the Period column
** Provide a custom label for Interim inventories: Interim (Started Date)
*** Example: Interim (12-02-2021)
*** do not include time
** Display a mouseover tooltip on the date that says “Started Date”
