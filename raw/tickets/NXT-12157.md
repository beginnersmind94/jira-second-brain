# NXT-12157 — AI/ML - Expenditures

## Description
The allowable costs that can be identified specifically with the production and service of meals to school children.

*FACTORS THAT INFLUENCE:*

* Wages, salaries, and benefits costs
* Food costs
* Supply costs
* Centralized vs. decentralized production
* Scratch vs. convenience production
* Contract vs. self-operated districts/schools
* Food waste
* Purchasing practices
* Absenteeism rate

## Acceptance Criteria
* The system shall be able to predict the expenditures for a given district and site for a given period of time.
* The system shall provide recommendations to minimize expenditures for a given district and site using the following as factors:
** Food costs
** Supply costs
** Centralized vs. decentralized production
** Scratch vs. convenience production
** Contract vs. self-operated districts/schools
** Payment or non-payment of indirect costs
** Food waste
** Purchasing practices
** Absenteeism rate
