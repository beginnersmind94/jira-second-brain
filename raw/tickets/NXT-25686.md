As an Ordering Manager I want an Exceptions Report so I can see the list of Production items which are not orderable.

* On the Required Materials Report ([NXT-25488|https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-25488]) show the list of Exception items on the final page
* List the Production Item which is not orderable
** Add Reason
*** Item cannot be delivered by \[serve date] - Ex: ‘Item cannot be delivered by 2/15/2022’

***** Item has been unorderable on the contract
***** etc.

** no orderable item found in the hierarchy
** no orderability (via contract OR via Ordering Conditions) for the item
** delivery time is too great to receive product in time
