# NXT-2660 — Discover

## Description
Discover is a way for users to preview new feature or value offerings and choose to enable and adopt for their operation. All users can explore new additions to individual pages that they use and better understand how it work. An operation has the power to adopt new functionality for their users at their own pace. Gives us the opportunity market and brand effectively to promote maximum usage.

* Create new feature discovery entry
* Associate media
* Mandatory visibility
* Allow enabling, remind me later, or ignore for now
* Track interaction
* Manage TTL
