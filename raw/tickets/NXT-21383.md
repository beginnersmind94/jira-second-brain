# NXT-21383 — Reports UX Update

## Description
We need to update the reports section based upon new guidance on UX in order to be consistent with 2.0

*Application*: Face and Food Detection Website

*Background:*

* Originally, when reports were built, we made some design decisions that were inconsistent with 2.0 and need to update them now before we go to far in development
* What was developed in the reports are good and we want to keep it, but we just need to update the UI

 

*Flow:*

* Browse to PrimeroEdge and click the link for Food and Face Detection
* Browse to the Reports
* View data

 

*Reports:*

* In the left menu, remove the Accuracy Report link and the Trained Images Report link
* Turn Reports from a header to a link
* Create a new page called Reports that will receive the reports link
* In Accuracy Report, remove the tabs and break out each tab into its own page
** Page titles will be the same as the tab labels: Accuracy By Day and Accuracy by Transaction
*  
* The new Reports page will look like the Attachment 1 with a grid of “cards”
* Each column has a header and a list of “cards” under it
* Each card has a title that is linkable and a description
* Column 1:
** Header: Accuracy Reports
** Card 1:
*** Title: Summary Accuracy
*** Description: Detection Accuracy by Day
*** Links to Accuracy by Day report
** Card 2:
*** Title: Transaction Accuracy

*** Links to Accuracy by Transaction report
* Column 2:
** Header: Food Training Reports
** Card 1:
*** Title: Trained Menu Items
*** Description: Trained Images for Menu Items
*** Links to Trained Menu Items report
