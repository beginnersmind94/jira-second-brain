# NXT-32898 — PR- Pre/Post Production Analysis- Vegetable Juice Percentage with Clear Indicator (Food Component Report)

## Description
As a Auditor, I would like to clearly see how much Vegetable Juice was offered for the week and what percentage juice was it in relation to my total vegetables offered. I would also like a way to see any additional vegetables that may have been indicated.

## Acceptance Criteria
* Display row with information related to Additional vegetable and Vegetable Juice information
** Display- Additional Vegetables (This is when a user indicates as additional vegetable total for the week, There is no weekly standard for this but it will be included as part of the total weekly vegetables offered
** Display- Weekly Vegetables Offering (This is the total amount of vegetables offered for the week)
** Display- Weekly Vegetable Juice Offering (This is the total amount of Vegetable juice offered for the week)
** Display- % of Weekly Vegetable that is a Juice (This is the amount of weekly vegetable juice offering divided by Weekly vegetables offering) 
*** This should line up with the weekly total column
** Display- Weekly requirement percentage
** Display weekly check with a pass or fail and color associated with each
