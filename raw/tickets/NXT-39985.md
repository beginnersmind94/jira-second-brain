## Acceptance Criteria
* Update the copy popup to accommodate new item structure
* Reorganize the fields/sections which can be copied
** organize into two main sections: Product Info and Nutrient Info
** Product Info: Summary, Tags, Pack Size, Images
** Nutrient Info: Serving Sizes, Nutrients, Contributions, Allergens
*** Nutrients and Contributions are dependent on the selection of Serving Sizes
*** Disable the copy checkbox for these two fields if Serving Sizes is not also selected
** Require at least Summary Info, Pack Size Units, or Serving Sizes be selected in order to copy
* Copy Pack Sizes and Serving Sizes separately 
** a “dual unit” would only be copied as a dual unit if both Pack and Servings are being copied
* Default copied items to ‘Do Not Publish’ for Inventory readiness
* Disable the Nutrition Info column for non-food items
** make everything gray/disabled and deselected
