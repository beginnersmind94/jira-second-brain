## Acceptance Criteria
* Add the item type drawer panel to the Menu Item Details screen (like items)
** DMI (panel already exists), Recipe (panel being added as part of 40699), Custom Menu Item, Variety Menu Item
* Provide the Active flag for Menu Items
* Provide a prompt on click of the Deactivate button
** “Do you wish to make this menu item inactive? [Yes, Deactivate], [No, Cancel]
** On click of ‘Yes, Deactivate’ check for all active menus containing the menu item
* If the menu item is not used on any menus, move the status directly to *Inactive*
* If the menu item is used on menus
** move the status into *Pending Deactivation* 
** Provide the Find & Replace button to open the Menu Planning Find & Replace screen, with the menu item already applied to the ‘Find’ search
* Use a job to automatically convert ‘Pending Deactivation’ menu items to ‘Inactive’ status once all menus are updated
* Once the status is Inactive, provide a small button for reactivating
