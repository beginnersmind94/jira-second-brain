# NXT-7241 — INV - Vendor - Items - Add Pricing Conditions to Item

## Description
As a Procurement Specialist I need the ability to add pricing conditions per date range so I can configure all the prices my items may have.

*Requirements*

* On the Contract Items side panel, add a Pricing button
** Price, Delivery Fee, Processing Fee, and Net Off Invoice will no longer be edited in the side panel. They will be edited in this slide out.
* Open a slide-out when user clicks Pricing Conditions
* Show the Price Details grid in the slide-out
** Start Date 
** End Date
** Price
** Delivery Fee
** Processing Fee
** Net Off Invoice
** Edit icon
*** users can edit price for all date ranges, but existing transactions will not have their prices updated
*** controlled by permission
** Delete Icon
*** same situation as editing
*** controlled by permission
* Provide a tool to add a new price (this will be controlled by permission)
** Start Date (mandatory)
** End Date
*** assume 12/31/9999 if no End Date is provided (price lasts forever until a new one is added)
** Price (mandatory)
** Delivery Fee
** Processing Fee
** Net Off Invoice
** Add button
* Validations
** date ranges can never overlap
** if a new price is inserted that conflicts with an existing price, the existing price must be *automatically* modified to have an End Date the day before the new condition
*** Example
**** Current price: 1/1/2020 - 12/31/9999
**** New price: 1/1/2022 - 12/31/2024
**** The previous price should be modified so the End Date is 12/31/2021
