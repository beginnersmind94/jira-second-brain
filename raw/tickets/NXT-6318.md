# NXT-6318 — Eligibility - Applications - Application Details - Tags

## Description
*Story* 

I want to conveniently use tags to easily sort and filter through applications based on the tags below. 

*Mockup* 

[https://www.figma.com/proto/yrkJeGfd3f9hWjqm0qvfir/Edge2.0_V1?node-id=87%3A2599&scaling=min-zoom|https://www.figma.com/proto/yrkJeGfd3f9hWjqm0qvfir/Edge2.0_V1?node-id=87%3A2599&scaling=min-zoom]

*Requirements*

* Tags
** Set Options (phase 1, custom tags to come later) > Error Prone, Active Benefits, Pending, Verification, Unconfirmed
** Multi-select available
** No text search 
* Applications > Tags
** Error Prone
*** indicates applications considered ‘error prone.'
**** Applications where total household income falls within $100 of an applicable income eligibility guideline.
**** Example: The free threshold for a household of 3 is $1400 monthly. If the total household income is within $100 of that amount, this would be an error-prone application.
** Active Benefits
*** Options: Active, Inactive
*** Filters for there being an active student receiving benefits from the application. Active = at least 1 student has benefits from the app, Inactive = no students are receiving benefits from the application.
** Verification
*** tag indicates apps that are part of verification.
** Pending 
*** Pending students included in this application
** Unconfirmed 
*** Student(s) in the application have categorical status which is NOT confirmed via documentation.
**** Show in work list(?) or Pending List(?) as Pending Categorical Confirmation
*** _Pg. 15 Except for a foster child, the household indication of Other Source Categorically Eligible Status must be_ *_confirmed through documentation_* _prior to certifying the child’s eligibility for free meals. If documentation does not confirm eligibility for an Other Source Categorically Eligibility, the household should be asked to resubmit the application and include income information._
*** GOAL > need a way to search unconfirmed categorically eligible students part of processed applications.

## Acceptance Criteria
The tags above are available in the corresponding screens with the ability to filter and search by the tags listed.
