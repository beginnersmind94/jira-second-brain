# NXT-11157 — SchoolCafe Integration - Real-time purchase synchronization

## Description
* Purchase information shall be kept synchronized with SchoolCafe in real-time.

## Acceptance Criteria
Changes to purchase information shall be synchronized with SchoolCafe within 5 seconds.

If SchoolCafe or Perseus are unavailable to be synchronized for a period of time, there shall be a mechanism to synchronize them once the 2 systems are available.

This mechanism shall be performant and scalable.
