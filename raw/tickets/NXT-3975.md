# NXT-3975 — AcctMgt - Patron Detailed - Restrictions - Spending Limits

## Description
*User Story*

As a district administrator I want to see and set a la carte spending limits and special instructions for an account, so that the the cashier will be alerted when the patron comes through the line.

*Mockup*

[https://nqyc9v.axshare.com/start.html#p=category_based_on_patron_view&id=jytwv5&view=default&g=4|https://nqyc9v.axshare.com/start.html#p=category_based_on_patron_view&id=jytwv5&view=default&g=4]

*Requirements*

* On the Restrictions panel in the Patron Details page, add an area for _Spending Limits_.
* If present, the section will show information on certain spending restrictions for the patron:
** Spending limits per frequency (day/week/month).
** Maximum a la carte purchases allowed per day.
** Blocking a la carte purchase restrictions
*** These are split by Cash and Debit purchases.
*** They’re also split out by day of the week.
** Special Instructions
*** Text field entry, 250 characters.
* Via the Edit button/edit mode, allow adjustments to the fields and checkboxes above so that with the Save button, data saves appropriately on the account.
* Behavior for all of these sections is the *same* as Classic, with +one+ exception: the Special Instructions section should be a single field with a maximum of 250 characters, rather than 4 fields of 25-characters each.

## Acceptance Criteria
Spending Limits section expanded upon in the Restrictions tab.
Page shows spending limit details.
Edit mode allows user to check/uncheck boxes, insert numbers into Max A la Carte field, and insert a text string in Special Instructions field.
Data saves appropriately.
