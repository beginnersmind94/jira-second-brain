# NXT-61055 — AI Onboarding - Digital Recipe - Recipe Screen Update

## Description
As a customer support person, I would like to use AI to digitize a recipe document so that I can speed up onboarding

*Notes:*

* This is a part of the onboarding AI project and is needed for subsequent stories

*User Flow:*

* User browses to Item Management and then Recipes
* System checks if the district has enabled AI and shows the AI button if they have
* User clicks on the AI button and it opens the AI Recipe drawer

*Requirements:*

* On the Recipes screen, do the following:
* Add a button:
** Icon: reflects that it is related to AI
** Placement: top right next to the + New (see screenshot)
** Visibility:
*** Visible to Customer Support and Technical support role always
*** For users, check if the district has enabled AI in the System Settings
**** If Yes, show
**** Else, hide
** Security: 
*** User must have Create permission for Recipes if district user if district has enabled AI and not in CS or tech support role
**** If Yes, show
**** Else, no
** Tooltip: Recipe AI
** On click: Open the Recipe AI drawer
* 
* Create a new drawer:
** Title: Recipe AI
** Content: leave blank, will add content in other stories to follow
** No additional security requirements needed
** Use 3/4 drawer width
** Use sliding motion when opening
