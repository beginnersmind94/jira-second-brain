# NXT-14423 — Eligibility - Applications - Form households for students on processed applications

## Description
As a district admin, I want students on an application/verification response together to be merged into a household for easier processing in future years and for letters.

*Requirements*

* Place students together into the same household after an application/verification response is processed.
** If students were already in households prior, merge them together into a new household.
*** Example: 1 student in household A vs 3 students in household B. Household C should be created and contain all 4 students.
* Scenarios where student households may be impacted:
** Application Submission
** Application Re-processing
** Verification Complete
** Pending Student - Process Matches

## Acceptance Criteria
Verify that after processing an application with multiple students, the students are all merged into the same (new) household

Verify that if the students were already in separate households, that a new household with all students in it at the time of processing is created

Verify the households are combined in the above scenarios where student households may be impacted (Income Surveys are no included in the scope of this story)
