# NXT-29114 — IM > Recipes > Scaling > Measurements

## Description
Requirement: Check and correct method for adjusting the yield of a recipe (scaling a recipe).

a.) evaluator scaled test recipe from 25 servings to 425 servings - software is not calculating the serving size correctly when scaling the recipe, therefore nutrient information is also incorrect.

b.) measuring amounts for ingredients in scaled recipe are not appropriate (2 ounces of mushrooms converted to Liters and Pints, as were other non-liquid items using ounce as measurements)

|4. Never move across imperial vs metric, keep consistency|
