## Acceptance Criteria
* Modify the Assign & Pick tab to support progressing from Assignment to Picking
** Provide a Start Picking button
*** only enabled if all items have sufficient QOH
* Prevent Picking from being started if any items drop below sufficient QOH
** Check QOH logic while distribution is in Assignment Complete status
** Display Reassign button
** Disable Start Picking button
* Display the header with updated Stage chip and Status
* *Reserve* Picked Quantity at the time of Picking Started
** reserve quantities. but do not reserve specific batches
* *Lock* the distribution from any additional orders
** any more orders for that day would automatically create a new Unassigned distribution
* Provide the item grid for picking
** Item #
** Item Description
** Quantity on Hand
*** always Actual QOH on this step
*** reduce QOH to consider Committed Quantity of other distributions/transactions
**** but include in the QOH the reserved quantity for the current distribution
** Quantity Assigned 
** Quantity Picked
** Action - to view site level details
* Emphasize items with insufficient QOH
** Display the QOH and Quantity Picked values in red
** color the action icon red
* Provide same grid options from Assignment
** 'Show only QOH errors' toggle
** Add Item (future)
** View Options (slideout)
* Update the sticky footer
** Back to Calendar button remains present
** Update messaging to communicate picking details
** provide a Pick Tickets report dropdown button
*** Provide two versions of the report
**** By Item
**** By Item & Site
** Provide a ‘Complete Picking’ button
*** this is disabled until all items have sufficient QOH
