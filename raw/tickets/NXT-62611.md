# NXT-62611 — Acc - Reports - Menu Item Sales - Performance Report

## Description
As Dana the Director I want to see item sales day-over-day and compare in a variety of combinations to make better purchasing decisions. This will be a potential mild revenue driver if it leads to increased participation.

*requirements*

* Add a new Report + Report Type to Menu Item Sales
** Name: Item Performance
** Report Options:
*** Sort by: Item, Site 
*** Include Price Types (nice to have)
**** Checkbox, off by default
*** Exclude:
**** Weekends and Holidays
***** Do not show Weekends, Configured Holidays
* Report format:
** Will depend on the sorting order that the customer establishes, but we’re aiming to show them day-over-day sale figures. Some examples are below.
** If the user includes price types - the number of columns will bloat tremendously
*** Discuss: Should this be a nice to have?
** Patron Criteria will determine if the Students and Adult counts are shown respectively.
