## Acceptance Criteria
Display disclaimers based on click event.

Gray out the ability to click disclaimers until criteria is selected. 



Disclaimers: Meal Pattern 14+ (K-5) (K-8) (6-8) (9-12) Lunch, Supper

All nutrient analysis is calculated against the daily nutrient standards set for the meal type and serving group selected

All component colors are turned on based on the daily nutrient standards

Milk component color will turn on only if the combination fluid milk and variety of milk standards are met

Sodium standards are based on Sodium Target 1
