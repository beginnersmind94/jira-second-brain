# NXT-62669 — Planner AI - Menu Item Breakdown (District/Site)

## Description
As a district admin, I would like a way to see my menu item break down to see what days perform better and what the cost, waste and participation is on those days.

## Acceptance Criteria
* Display menu item details when user clicks on the menu item from the Item Performance List
** Participation Score
** Waste Dollar amount (By Date Range and School Filter) and percentage with the number of wasted servings
** Cost (Average Cost per Serving in by Range and School Filter) 
*** Display what the serving size is, unless their are multiple “Show Multiple Servings Served”
* Provide Breakdown of Days of week Analysis 
** Each Day Monday- Friday
*** Average Produced Servings
*** Average Served Servings
*** Participation Score for each Day
*** Leftover Percentage for each Day
*** Waste Percentage for each Day
*** Waste Cost Per Day (Wasted Servings X cost per serving
* Provide School Analysis
** Each School
*** Average Produced Servings
*** Average Served Servings
*** Participation Score for each Day
*** Leftover Percentage for each Day
*** Waste Percentage for each Day
*** Waste Cost Per Day (Wasted Servings X cost per serving
