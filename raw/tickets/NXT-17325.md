# NXT-17325 — AI Data Insights – Eligibility and Participation AI/ML Model 

## Description
As a system, AI insights need AI/ML model development to achieve eligibility recommendation and predict participation based on influence factors.	

*Requirement:*

# Recommendation model for Eligibility
# PAID Not Applied (PNA) prediction based on eligibility key data and eligibility influence factors 
# Prediction on participation based on influence factors
# Positive and Negative impact analysis on participation based on influenced factors
# Dependency factors to define model definition: Influence factors data collection and analysis

*Pre-Requisite:*
Workshop with AI/ML team to evaluate Key data (MVDs) and Influence factors/Alternate data - TBD

## Acceptance Criteria
*AI/ML Model*
1.	The system shall provide recommendation for eligibility based on a District/Site with the following factors:
a.	Students qualify per site (by percentage and by number)
b.	Students qualify per district (by percentage)

2.	The system shall provide Paid not applied (PNA) recommendation for eligibility based on a District/Site with the following factors:
a.	Students qualify per site (by percentage and by number)
b.	Students qualify per district (by percentage)

3.	The system shall predict participation for a given district/site with following:
a.	Participation increases due to Eligibility recommendation
b.	Participation Increase due to influence factor <define factor rate> 
c.	Participation affected due to an influence factor <define factor rate>

4.	The system shall recommend influence factor changes due to participation 
a.	Influence factor – percentage affected in participation
b.	ADP (Breakfast) – rate 
c.	ADP (Lunch) – rate
d.	MEQ - change by week, month, and year

*AI/ML Data Output:*
1.	The system shall output the student list based on recommendation for eligibility
a.	New students list qualified per site based on eligibility recommendation
b.	New students list qualified by Geo area within the district
c.	New students list qualified by affordability by site
d.	New students list qualified by Ethnicity/Age/Grade

2.	The system shall output the student list based on Paid Not applied (PNA) recommendation
a.	New students list qualified through PNA eligibility recommendation
b.	Student list qualified but application not filled for eligibility (application not on file)
c.	Student list qualified but marked for ‘rejected to apply’ 

3.	The system shall output the list of districts/schools based on participation influence factors
a.	List of districts/schools can increase participation based on <influence factors>
b.	List of districts/schools where participation is affected based on <influence factors>
