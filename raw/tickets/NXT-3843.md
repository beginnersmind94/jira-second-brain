# NXT-3843 — Site Scorecard

## Description
*User Story*
As a Site level School Nutrition Professional, I want to view a Site level Scorecard provided by PrimeroEdge that scores individual Sites as a whole using default KPIs and Metrics based on PrimeroEdge’s wealth of industry knowledge and data.

*Mockup*


*Requirements*

* Standard scorecard using a specific set of KPIs and Metrics are used by PrimeroEdge for all Districts.
** [ICN KPI study|https://www.google.com/url?sa=t&rct=j&q=&esrc=s&source=web&cd=2&ved=2ahUKEwi24eL77YvpAhXuna0KHRmeDSYQFjABegQIAhAB&url=https%3A%2F%2Ftheicn.org%2Fresources%2F347%2Ftechnical-reports%2F106606%2Fessential-key-performance-indicators-kpis-for-school-nutrition-success-2.pdf&usg=AOvVaw3DPw6nNjMkOFnFd2BZSUqx] to be used as a starting point.
* Use industry standards/national averages based on PrimeroEdge customer data to establish scoring calculations.
* Enrollment, FRE %, Site Type, and other variables maybe be automatically factored into calculations as schools may have different expectations.
** Calculations may differ by Site Type.
* Scoring is presented in an easy-to-understand scale
** A-F
** 0-100
* Each section (KPI, Metric) is scored individually.
* Overall weighted score presented at the top of the scorecard.
