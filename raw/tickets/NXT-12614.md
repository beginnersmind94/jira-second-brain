# NXT-12614 — AI/ML - Staff Turnover Rate (Ignore this story for now)

## Description
*KPI:* Staff Turnover Rate

*DESCRIPTION:* Staff turnover rate is the rate at which staff members leave employment, either voluntary or involuntary, and are replaced by new employees.

----

*WHY CALCULATE:* The timing of recruiting efforts can affect a school nutrition (SN) program’s ability to hire and train new employees to be ready for work when needed. It is extremely important to calculate this in areas with low unemployment rates. Hiring and training new employees is a costly process, and high turnover rates are indicative of internal problems, such as poor work environment, lack of opportunities for development and advancement, and poor supervision.

----

*HOW OFTEN TO CALCULATE:* Weekly, monthly, and annually.
