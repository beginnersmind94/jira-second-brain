# NXT-6024 — Application Entry - Guided Process (3. Household Members)

## Description
*User Story* 

As an admin, i am guided to the next step of the process where i can enter additional household members.

*Mock Ups* 

[https://www.figma.com/proto/yrkJeGfd3f9hWjqm0qvfir/Edge2.0_V1?node-id=1306%3A26124&scaling=min-zoom|https://www.figma.com/proto/yrkJeGfd3f9hWjqm0qvfir/Edge2.0_V1?node-id=1306%3A26124&scaling=min-zoom]

*Requirements* 

* *Add household members button*
** popup for details shows
*** Name
*** Does this member receive income? 
**** Yes > Enter income amount under the appropriate income type (work, assistance, other) and choose frequency from drop down 
***** If Yes indicated but no amount provided > highlight income amount as required field
**** No (default)  > Save
* *Household Members table*
** Name, income type, income amount, frequency
** Option to Edit and Remove household members
*** Are you sure you want to remove this household member from this application?
**** Yes/Cancel
** Students prepopulated in the table on load from step 1
*** Cannot edit or remove students from this table 
*** must go back to student section to edit
* *Household Size and Total Income* 
** Entry field for household size (see 6687 conditionals)
** Total Income (Frequency) - total income will show the value calculated for all members and students
*** **If there are varying frequencies, the Total Income should roll up to annual “Total Income (Annual)”
**** Dynamic number - as incomes are added to the grid, the number calculates and updates correctly
*** **If a household has only one source of income or if all sources are the same frequency, show that frequency. “Total Income (Monthly)”
**** PG 62 eligibility guidelines 
* *Income Eligibility Guidelines (link not needed)*
* -Show Income Eligibility Outcome, dynamically updates as incomes are added-

* Option to go to previous step

## Acceptance Criteria
- applicant is guided to next step 
- applicant has option to go to previous step 
- applicant can add household members 
- household info is name, income type amount and frequency
- display of members shown in screen
- students and their income if indicated in step 1 are shown in this page
- total household size is an entry field with a validation check 
- total income is calculated based on income amounts
- total income frequency is dependent on entry frequency: variance rolls up to annual, no variance shows as is.
- applicant can edit and remove members (not students) as needed
- Multi-language, ADA compliant.
