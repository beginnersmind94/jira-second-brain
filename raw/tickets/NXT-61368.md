# NXT-61368 — INV - Distribution - Shipping - Modify Shipments - Combine Shipments

## Description
As a warehouse distribution manager I want a tool to make changes to the default shipments loaded on a distribution, so I can customize according to my specific shipping/delivery needs.

h3. Shipping tab - Modify Shipments - Combine Shipments

!Shipping - Modify - Combine Shipments Rev-20250529-195011.png|width=91.66666666666666%,alt="Shipping - Modify - Combine Shipments Rev-20250529-195011.png"!

h3.

## Acceptance Criteria
* Open a slideout when the ‘Modify Shipments’ button is clicked
* Display a ‘Modify Shipments By’ heading with 3 options
** Reset to Original (default)
*** disabled when user is on default data setup
** Move Orders
** Combine Shipments
* When *Combine Shipments* is selected, provide dropdown tools to merge two shipments together into one shipment
** display a ‘Combine’ heading with a dropdown containing all shipments
** display ‘and’ as a heading and provide a second dropdown containing all other shipments
** display a ‘Combine Shipments' button in the footer
*** only enabled after all selections are made
*** display a confirmation prompt on click: “Are you sure you want to combine these two shipments?” 
*** button icon: [https://fonts.google.com/icons?selected=Material+Symbols+Outlined:call_merge:FILL@1;wght@400;GRAD@0;opsz@24&icon.query=merge&icon.size=24&icon.color=%235f6368|https://fonts.google.com/icons?selected=Material+Symbols+Outlined:call_merge:FILL@1;wght@400;GRAD@0;opsz@24&icon.query=merge&icon.size=24&icon.color=%235f6368|smart-link] 
* Exclude from the dropdowns any shipments which are marked ‘Ready to Ship’
* After combining shipments, put the orders from the second selected shipment below the orders from the first selected shipment
* After combining shipments
** delete old shipments
** modify the Delivery Group column
*** show italicized # of stops
*** add info icon, tooltip: Modified Route
* If different Shipping Groups are combined, show them comma separated in the SG column
