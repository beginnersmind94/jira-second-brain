## Acceptance Criteria
Business Rule Updates

* Consume all inventory statuses for items added to Bulk Update sessions
** including Synced and Locked
* Update the Cost/Value and Publishing sub-steps to lock down relevant selections based on the inventory status
* Cost/Value
** Cost/Value can always be modified, but cannot be removed for Locked/Synced items
** Cost/Value can be removed for Published or lower status items, but may cause a change in the inventory status (e.g. Published to Incomplete)
* Inventory Publishing 
** add ‘Publish to Inventory’ grid label
** Update toggle label: ‘Only items that can be Published’
** Include Locked and Synced items with chip display
** filter out Locked and Synced items when ‘Only items that can be Published’ is toggled on

UX Updates

* Use single Item column with *(LI-1234) Item Name* formatting used on other sections
* Add Inventory status column at end of grid
* Remove click event from unclickable elements
** or plan to add some click interaction (like status chip flyouts)
