# NXT-20748 — Camera Alignment

## Description
As a Cashier, I want to pre-configure my camera to point to a specific location to capture food trays at the time of sale.

*Application*: POS

*Flow:*

* Browse to the Food Detection Settings page
* Click on the Camera Setup button which takes user to Camera Setup page
* View the video output on the page
* Manually adjust the camera by moving it until it shows the area desired
* Close the page

 *Assumptions:*

* This page exists in the classic ExpressPoint
* You can copy all the code and try to copy as-is
* Talk to Saketh if you have any questions

*Requirements*:

* Copy everything from classic as-is
* Update Settings screen:
* Add this field:
** Configure Camera
*** Hyperlink or button
*** On click, go to the Camera setup screen
*  
* Create new Camera screen:
** Page Title: Camera Setup
** Be able to click on a link to print the target sheet
** Be able to view the video output on the screen
** Be able to close the page
**  
** Fields:
** Video section
*** Live stream from the video camera
** Print Target Sheet
*** Hyperlink or button
*** On click, opens the print dialogue box to print out the target sheet
* Security
** Anyone with rights to use the Terminal has rights to this screen
