# NXT-3771 — Item Management - Recipe - On Save Calculating Allergens

## Description
*User Story*

As a Item Management Super User, I need my item allergies to save to the recipe

N/A

*Requirements*

* When clicking the ‘Save’ button after adding items to a recipe, the allergen information needs to save to the recipe
** For example, if a user selected an item with 2 ‘Wheat - Contains’ and 1 ‘Wheat - May Contain’ and 1 ‘Wheat - Free From', then it would take the highest priority
** Contains
** Processed in a Facility…
** May Contain
** Free From
* For now, when the recipe is saved, check for items with allergens. Check all the items that had allergen information.
* The only time that No allergens indicated would be selected is if ALL the items have ‘No Allergens Indicated’ as true.

## Acceptance Criteria
Allergen information will update from the item record to the recipe record
