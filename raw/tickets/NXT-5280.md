# NXT-5280 — Eligibility - Notifications - Carryover of Eligibility

## Description
As a central office user I want to send notices of carryover eligibility (grace period) to households with students who ended the prior school year with a free or reduced eligibility so that they can know they have 30 days to submit an application before their eligibility runs out and they are set to a paid status.

*Mockup*

!image-20210226-173137.png|width=1254,height=701!

*Requirements*

* Located at Eligibility (Module) > Notifications (Page) > Carryover (tab)
* This is to trigger the Email and Print functionalities
* Notify Selected and Notify All buttons should work as expected, with the emails going out when those boxes are checked, or the letters being printed when those boxes are checked.
* Filters
** -Academic Year-
*** *Note 3/10/21: Academic Year selector not required, only use current academic year.*
** Site (All is default)
** -Checkbox (unselected by default) - “Exclude students enrolled at sites with Community Eligibility Provision (CEP)”-
*** -Note: At this time, CEP is not available for configuration. We will add the checkbox selection and will program for the added logic at a later time.-
** -Checkbox (unselected by default) - “Exclude students with previous notifications”-
*** -Does not show students with a prior notification (indicated by the “Notified On” column).-
* Results - A grid displaying the results of those filters which includes:
** ID #, First Name, Last Name, Site, Grade, Eligibility, Expiring On (will be the date the carryover eligibility is set to expire), -Notified On (will be the date of the last notification)-
** Print/Email Selection - The ability to *check boxes* to email OR print Carryover letters for one, more, or all patrons in that list. Email checkboxes should only display for patrons who have email addresses in their primary guardian’s profile in Patron Details. Header checkbox selection for each will select all within the column that are available for selection. Unchecking will remove selections for all within the column.
** View - The ability to *view* a Carryover letter, using the active _Grace Period_ letter template from the Templates page (for one, more, or all patrons in that list). This should open a pop-up letter viewer window.
* Notify - {color:#ff5630}Email and Print functionality (Notify) can be handled in using the common control from the Platform team.{color}

*Information*:

_Carryover Letters_ are used to notify households via email and/or printed letters that their student(s) Grace Period is close to expiration. These letters also advise the parent/guardian to submit a new application for their student(s), intending to provide uninterrupted eligibility benefits for qualified households and continued reimbursement for the District. District Student Eligibility personnel generally send Grace Period Letters to the parents/guardians of the households at the beginning of the new school year.
