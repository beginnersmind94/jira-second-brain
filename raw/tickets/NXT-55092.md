# NXT-55092 — Acc/POS - Receipts/Transaction History - Show Quantity Sold

## Description
*Internal findings*

* We’re not recording the quantity amount when multiple items are sold
** Individual SaleDetail entries are being added, each with quantity of 1
* Receipts do not show the quantity amount
** They are either showing 10x line items or just showing the total



*requirements*

* Show the quantity sold in receipts
** Both POS and Accountability
* Show the quantity sold while reviewing the Transaction Details
** POS
* Use the Quantity column properly in SaleDetail instead of recording individual lines for each item
* Still need to support the existing way of displaying this data in Accountability (see screenshot 4) after this change is made
