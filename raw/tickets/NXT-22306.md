# NXT-22306 — INV - Management - Contract Items

## Description
As a District Admin I want a dedicated Contract Items page so I can view and make changes to many items at once regardless of their contract.

*Requirements*

* Create a new Contract Items page in the Management category
** list this under Vendors
* Create filters which allow certain items to be returned
** Vendor + Contract (current)
*** require single selection (default to ‘Select’)
*** only show Active vendors with at least on active contract
*** Ex: ‘JM Vendor - C10112’
** Item Category
*** default to ‘All’
** *Status*
*** *Orderable*
*** *Unorderable*
* List items based on results
** only items on currently active contracts
** display all possible contract info in a single row
*** Item #
*** Item Description
*** Vendor Item #
*** Current Price
**** provide a way to quickly view the Price Conditions in order to see Charges & Discounts 
*** Lead Time
**** “3 days”
**** display ---- if empty
*** Min/Max Order Quantity
**** try to use one field
**** Min/Max, e.g. 5/50
**** display ---- if empty 
* Allow mass update actions
