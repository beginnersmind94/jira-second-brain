# NXT-54927 — MP- Site Groups- Site Name with additional sites and effective dates

## Description
As a District admin, It is very confusing when I am adding sites to a site group at with different effective dates but only one effective date shows up on the grid.

## Acceptance Criteria
* Remove effective date from the landing page grid
* Provide a slide out for all sites instead of a tool tip when clicking on the +(number)
* On the slide out provide the site group name as the slide out header
* Provide Site Name, Effective date and end date  as the three columns in the grid 



!image-20240918-053857.png|width=449,height=887,alt="image-20240918-053857.png"!



* Each site should have its own effective date based on when it is added or supposed to take into effective as part of that site group
* -If a site has been switched to another site group or removed from a site group, provide an end date as to when that takes into effective-
** -IF added to another site group, the end date of the old site group should be the day before the effective date of the new group- 
** -This should apply if being added from the site group tab or from the site configuration tab-
** -Clear all menu assignments that are available to be deleted (NOT RECORD COMPLETE OR CONSOLIDATED PLAN COMPLETE) or after effective date from previous site group config-
* Correct all data that is currently repeating because of the effective date issue (QA check)
