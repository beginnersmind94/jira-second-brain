## Acceptance Criteria
* Add a *Delivery Groups* column to the Vendor > Contracts grid
** for internal vendors only 
** put the column after Shipping Groups
** move Active Date to the sub-grid (only for internal vendors!)
* Put an edit pencil in the Delivery Groups column
* Replace Shipping Groups truck icon with a pencil icon as well
** use purple for both Groups columns
** use gray for Action column
** keep using existing icon for calendar, but put it at the front in front of the days for consistency with other columns
* Display the Delivery Groups in a slideout on click of the edit pencil
** Provide a simple add control to enter new Delivery Groups (like Shipping Group slideout)
** Display 3 columns
*** Delivery Group (group description)
*** Assigned Sites (number of sites in group / number of total sites ordering from that vendor)
*** Action
** Display Default record
*** by default, all sites are associated with this group
** Show an expandable sub-grid with the associated sites and their Stop Order 
** Provide an Edit Stop Order icon in the Action column
*** Use drag & drop to reorder sites (Confirm with UI team)
** Provide a Delete icon in the Action column
*** prompt: “Are you sure you want to delete this Delivery Group? The associated sites will be moved to the Default group.”
*** move associated sites to Default group after prompt confirmation
