## Acceptance Criteria
* Review and update existing Transaction (Withdrawal, Addition, Transfer) resources
* Identifying new resources required, to be created by Platform
* Identify resources to “remove” or hide from Permissions page
* Provide updated Resource Type for each remaining resource
* Provide details on View/Edit/Add/Delete permissions based on resource needs
** for each resource shown on Permissions page
** Platform will configure these details
* Identify nesting structure for Permissions page
** *Transfers* - Page
*** Transfer Details (including History) - Sub-Page
*** Approve Transfers - Action / maybe special
** *Additions & Withdrawals* - Page (must have at least one of the tabs)
*** Additions - Tab
**** Addition Details - Sub-Page
**** Addition Reasons - Sub-Page
*** Withdrawals - Tab
**** Withdrawal Details - Sub-Page
**** Withdrawal Reasons - Sub-Page
** *Physical Inventory -* Page (main ‘Search’ tab)
*** Physical Inventory Status - Tab
*** Physical Inventory Details - Sub-Page
**** Reconcile Physical Inventory - Action 
**** Reopen Physical Inventory - Action / maybe special
