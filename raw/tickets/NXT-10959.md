# NXT-10959 — Dashboard (Insights) - Accountability - Revenue Widget (Advanced)

## Description
As a central office user or cafeteria manager I want the ability to see the revenue generated by my site or district. I want the data by Student/Adult then Meal Type (Breakfast/Lunch/Snack/Ala-Carte). There should be totals by Student and Adult. In addition the sales can be broken down into Cash or Debit sales and include tax if applicable.
