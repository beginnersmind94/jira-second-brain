# NXT-24102 — Photo Import - Bulk Import Capability

## Description
This story is to cover the tasks related to importing the photos in bulk without any limitations.

## Acceptance Criteria
* The existing web-based photo import shall be used.
* As an end-user, I would like the ability to upload photos by selecting a folder on my filesystem and all photos inside the folder shall be imported.
* As an end-user, I would like to be able to select a single folder on my filesystem in order to import the photos contained in each of them
* As an end-user, I would like the ability to upload up to 5GB of files at one time.
** This will be for testing purposes only to prove that the “chunking” upload implementation works.
* As a performance target, all 17,984 photos provided by Columbia School District (as part of their go-live) shall upload in less than 8 minutes.
* If any file within the selected folder is not an image file, it will be ignored, will not be a candidate for upload, and will not be reflected in the total file count.
* -The UX shall follow the attached Figma- The screenshots in the comments from July 7, 2022, supersede the attached Figma.
** While the upload is in progress, the “Uploading Photos” section shall be displayed
** Once the upload is complete, the “Uploading Photos” section shall be dismissed and the “Upload of Photos is Complete” section shall be displayed
** Errors shall be displayed beneath the the above-mentioned sections.
