# NXT-3837 — View Custom KPIs

## Description
*User Story*
As an Insights user, I can view a list of Custom KPIs.

*Mockup*
[https://qvv7ze.axshare.com/#id=sb8exr&p=custom_kpis|https://qvv7ze.axshare.com/#id=sb8exr&p=custom_kpis] (Mockup created before having visibility into existing 2.0 mockups)

*Requirements*

* Cutom KPIs are separated into 3 distinct categories
** Meal Counts and Participation
** Financial and Inventory Management
** Productivity and Labor
* KPIs display multiple points of information
** Name
** Definition
** Calculation
** Level
*** District
*** Site
*** Employee
* KPIs can be Enabled or Disabled from this screen.
** Enabled KPIs will be tracked and displayed on multiple screens.
*** Home
*** Dashboards
*** Goals
*** Reports
*** Notifications
*** Recommendations
