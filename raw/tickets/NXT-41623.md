## Acceptance Criteria
* Provide a confirmation view of the affected recipes where the ingredient before & after can be viewed
* At time of loading Confirm step, re-verify the replacement details
** Ingredient must still exist on recipe to be replaced
** Throw an error if any replacement issues exist
** Reset user’s recipe list or move user back to Recipe selection step
* Show serving info of old ingredient and new ingredient
** include weight/volume for both old and new serving
* Provide sticky footer button for official replacement
* Once the final action is clicked and the ingredients are officially removed/replaced/etc., leave the user on the confirm step with actions removed
** adjust sticky footer message to be in past tense (reference mockup)
** Display ‘Start Over’ button in place of sticky footer cancel/back
