## Description
As Anna the Application Processor I want to see the State Eligibility on a patron account so I have faith in the program.

*requirements*

* Show the State Eligibility in the following areas if the setting is turned on:
** Patrons Quick View drawer
** Patron Details (Student)

*** In the Eligibility/Benefits section.

** Grids:
*** Accounts > Basic - Add State Eligibility column.
*** Accounts > Advanced - Add search filter after Eligibility, add column to the grid.

**** Options: Free, Reduced, N/A
