# NXT-29264 — INV - Consolidated Perpetual - All Transactions

## Description
As a Site Manager I want all my Inventory management functionality to be logically grouped or combined in such a way that it doesn’t clutter my navigation to perform the various transactions that affect my perpetual inventory.



!Super Perpetual - All Transactions-20260221-180538.png|width=497,alt="Super Perpetual - All Transactions-20260221-180538.png"!

## Acceptance Criteria
* Add a new ‘All Transactions’ tab after the Items tab of the new consolidated Perpetual Inventory page
** Require a single site to be selected
** Provide Date Range filters
** Provide a Transaction Type dropdown
*** Receipts, Withdrawals, Additions, Transfers, Physical Inventories
* Display a ‘Perpetual Transactions’ simplified grid listing all transactions for the selected site
** Transaction #
** Transaction Type
*** display the associated color icon 
** Date 
** Status
** Details - display highlight information depending on transaction type
*** Withdrawal/Addition: reason
*** Transfer: other site
*** Order Receipt/Quick Receipt: vendor
*** Physical Inventory: period
** Updated On
** Updated By
** Action - view icon to open the transaction
* Assign colored icons to all transactions
** Color rules:
*** Green for product received (added to perpetual)
*** Orange for product sent or withdrawn (removed from perpetual)
*** Purple for reconciliations
