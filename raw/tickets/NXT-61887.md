# NXT-61887 — Planner AI - Overall Menu Item Waste (District/Site)

## Description
As a district/site admin, I would like to have a dashboard that provides me information about the menu items that are being wasted the most either by percentage of servings or cost.

## Acceptance Criteria
* Provide a District view of Menu Item waste with the 5 highest wasted items  and lowest wasted menu items in a chart grid to allow for quick overview of best and worst performing
** Allow users to toggle between a percentage view or cost view, this could alter the menu items due to possibly having a high waste percentage but a low cost per menu item or vice virsa
* Provide a filter to allow users to look at YTD, Academic Year, Last Month, Last Week
* Provide a filter to allow users to choose Menu item Category
* Provide a way for users to look at the total menu item list to see both waste percentage and waste cost and sort based highest to lowest or vice virsa
** Default to All Categories
* *Waste by percentage* is calculated by Produced count - (Carryover & Return to Stock) - Served = Waste
** Waste Count/ Produced count *100 = Percentage of Waste
** Calculate every instance of Menu Item on a production record and compute against the of each record to get an aggregated percentage of waste
* *Waste by cost* is calculated by Produced count - (Carryover & Return to Stock) - Served = Waste
** Waste Count * Cost per Serving = Total Waste Cost
** Calculate every instance of Menu Item on a production record and combine all the wasted servings * cost per serving to give a total amount of wasted dollars
