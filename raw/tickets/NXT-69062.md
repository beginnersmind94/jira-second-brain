# NXT-69062 — Ledger Entry Rules for POS-related Transactions

## Description
When a POS period is closed for a site, the system generates the sales and prepayment entry groups for that site and period so they can be approved and posted.

## Acceptance Criteria
* when a pos period is set to *closed* for a site, the system creates a revenue entries batch for that *site + period* with *status = new* and *dirty = false*
* the batch stores two dates for the ledger entry:
** *entered date* = date/time the pos period was closed
** *posting date* = the pos period end date
* the system generates the following entry groups and creates the ledger lines *at the site level* using the *gl accounts mapped to the listed system accounts*
** *meal sales*
*** amount = *cash sale + debit sale* totals for the site/period from the revenue report
**** exclude any sales paid using prepaid meal balance
*** credit: the gl accounts mapped to the *meal sales revenue system accounts* for the site
**** create one credit line per mapped revenue system account (program/service + a-la-carte + adult meals), matching the revenue report split
*** the sum of all credit lines must equal the debit total within each entry
** *prepayment*
*** debit: the gl account mapped to *prepaid account (Liability)*
**** amount = total sales paid using *prepaid meal balance* for the site/period from the revenue report/rollups
*** credit: the gl accounts mapped to the *meal sales revenue system accounts* for the site
**** create one credit line per mapped revenue system account (program/service + a-la-carte + adult meals), matching the prepaid-paid split
*** the sum of all credit lines must equal the debit total within each entry
** *cash taxes collected*
*** debit: the GL account mapped to *Cash in Bank (Asset)*
*** credit: the GL account mapped to *Sales Tax Owed (Liability)*
*** amount = total *cash tax* for the site/period from the revenue report (adult taxable sales only)
** *prepayment taxes collected*
*** debit: the GL account mapped to *Prepaid Account (Liability)*
*** credit: the GL account mapped to *Sales Tax Owed (Liability)*
*** amount = total *prepaid tax* for the site/period from the revenue report (adult taxable sales only)
** *account adjustments (GLEntryTypeCd = ACCTADJ)*
*** debit: the GL account mapped to *Account Adjustments (Expenditure)*
*** credit: the GL account mapped to *Prepaid Account (Liability)*
*** amount = total account adjustments for the site/period from rollups
** *additional income (GLEntryTypeCd = ADDINCM)*
*** debit: the GL account mapped to *Cash in Bank (Asset)*
*** credit: the GL account mapped to *Additional Income (Revenue)*
*** amount = total additional income for the site/period from rollups
** *bank fees (GLEntryTypeCd = BNKFEE)*
*** debit: the GL account mapped to *Prepaid Account (Liability)*
*** credit: the GL account mapped to *Bank Fees (Revenue)*
*** amount = total bank fees for the site/period from rollups
** *bonus (GLEntryTypeCd = BONUS)*
*** debit: the GL account mapped to *Bonus (Expenditure)*
*** credit: the GL account mapped to *Prepaid Account (Liability)*
*** amount = total bonus for the site/period from rollups
** *deposit variances (GLEntryTypeCd = DPSTVAR)*
*** debit: the GL account mapped to *Cash in Bank (Asset)*
*** credit: the GL account mapped to *Deposit Variances (Revenue)*
*** amount = total deposit variance for the site/period from rollups
** *refunds (GLEntryTypeCd = REFUND)*
*** debit: the GL account mapped to *Prepaid Account (Liability)*
*** credit: the GL account mapped to *Refunds (Liability)*
*** amount = total refunds for the site/period from rollups
*** validation: *Prepaid Account* and *Refunds* system accounts must not be mapped to the same GL account
** *returned checks (GLEntryTypeCd = RTNCHCK)*
*** debit: the GL account mapped to *Prepaid Account (Liability)*
*** credit: the GL account mapped to *Returned Checks (Liability)*
*** amount = total returned checks for the site/period from rollups
*** validation: *Prepaid Account* and *Returned Checks* system accounts must not be mapped to the same GL account
* mapping validation
** if the gl mapping is missing for cash in bank, prepaid account, or any required meal sales revenue system account, the system must fail generation for that site/period and return a message listing the missing mapping(s) (no partial entries)
