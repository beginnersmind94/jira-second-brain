# NXT-35079 — MP- Menu Analysis- Display varieties of Milk with pass or fail and Sodium Target 1 change (USDA CORRECTIONS)

## Description
USDA is requesting that we display varieties of Milk in a pass or fail fashion and not a numerical way. 

* Display Pass or Fail on the varieties of Milk based on validations that are calculating the amount varieties and if the varieties are meeting all requirement
** This should apply on the slide out and the nutrition report
* Hide all milk related rows except for Fluid Milk and Varieties of Milk (This should apply on the slideout)
* Display the Sodium Target 1 with => to match the Sodium Target1A look
** Make sure the analysis is taking into account passing the sodium target 1 if the sodium amount equals the allotted sodium target
