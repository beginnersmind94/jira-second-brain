## Acceptance Criteria
* Upon Period selection, show a list of other inventories for the period
** display the inventory, the number of items, and an icon to open that inventory (make it look better than mockup please)
* Update the Physical Inventory Options (displayed after Continue)
** Remove Valuation Group option
** Remove Item Category from New Physical selection options
** Show # of items being selected based on Storage Category selections
** Change ‘Items with Positive/Negative QOH’ to just be “Items with QOH”
* Provide an Inventory Name field
** pre-populate with Period - Date 
* Start button to generate the new inventory
