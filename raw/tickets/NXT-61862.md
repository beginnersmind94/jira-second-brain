# NXT-61862 — IM - Item Onboarding - Complete Session for ALL items Published

## Description
As a district admin using the item onboarding tool, once all my items are published from a session, I would like for there to still be a way that I can go back and take a look at what items I published through that session.

!image-20250519-140506.png|width=1083,height=695,alt="image-20250519-140506.png"!

## Acceptance Criteria
* Automatically Mark session as “Completed” once all items have been published
** There will not be a way to “reopen” session
* On the slideout where all the sessions are showing, provide a way to look at historical session that have been completed
* Allow a read only mode of the items that were part of the session
** This is published items slideout
* Always display completed sessions at the bottom of the list, by most recently completed
