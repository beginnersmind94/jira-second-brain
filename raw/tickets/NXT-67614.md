# NXT-67614 — INV - Hotshot Distributions - Pre-generate Order & Receipt

## Description
As a Distribution Manager I want the ability to create a manual distribution so I can distribute products to sites without needing orders to be placed.

## Acceptance Criteria
* Generate Hotshot orders when the shipments are marked as *Shipped*
** use similar structure to Quick Receipts
*** use new Entry Method of ‘Hotshot' 
** Generate order & receipt on Shipping stage
** If the Hotshot (manual) Distribution is cancelled, cancel all the associated orders
** Do not allow sites to edit the Hotshot orders
