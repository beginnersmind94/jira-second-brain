# NXT-37898 — IM - Recipes - Create Recipe - Updated flow (Units card)

## Description
As a recipe menu planner I want an updated Create Recipe flow similar to Items so I can manage all my serving related info in the consolidated Units card.

*Requirements*

* Redesign the initial Create Recipe flow to accommodated the completed Units card (from Items)
** Update the initial general Recipe Info card to provide only the remaining general info attributes
*** [https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-37844|https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-37844|smart-link] 
* Insert the Units card into the Recipe header
** relabel the card as ‘Servings’ in this case
*** hide Unit Type column
*** do not ask Food/Fluid questions
*** relabel Sub-Qty as ‘# of Servings’
*** label Weight as Weight only (no Volume)
*** relabel Cost/Value as simply ‘Cost’
*** provide only the +Serving button
**** Note: we will provide the Pack Size functionality on Recipes as part of Item Master related work for Centrally Produced Items
** Provide the Strict Batching option somewhere in the Units card header
* Require the same attributes for continuing with Recipe creation
** Description (in 1st card)
** Serving Size + Description (Units card)
** # of Servings (Units card)
* Accommodate the dynamic Image card on Recipes as well
** Should have adequate space to stay expanded most of the time
* Load the remaining Recipe screen and tabs as currently defined
** by default move to Steps & Ingredients
** maintain existing rules on Nutrient Info tab
