# NXT-64497 — Sys > Reim. Rates - CACFP Updates

## Description
As Dana the Director I want to see an estimate on my reimbursement claim - and I need to have proper reimbursement rates to do that. The current entries do not match with actual CACFP programs. 

h3. *Reimbursement Rates*

!image-20250718-181558.png|width=50%,alt="image-20250718-181558.png"!

Current screenshot above. Lot of meal types in there that shouldn’t be.

*Requirements*

* Default the Academic Year selector to the current year.
* Lock the editing of Federal rates to the Customer Support and Tech Support admin roles.
* User can set reimbursement rates for the following for the typical State Category (Contiguous, Alaska, Hawaii):
** Cash-in-Lieu of Commodities
*** Flat amount, money, 2 decimals. Example: $0.30
** Centers (Adult Day Care Centers and Child Care Centers)
*** Meal Types: Breakfast, ‘Lunch and Supper’, Supplement
**** Individual rates for Free, Reduced, Paid
** At Risk After School Meals
*** Meal Types: Breakfast, Lunch/Supper, Snacks
**** 1 rate only, no sub-eligibility.
** Day Care Homes
*** Tiers: Tier I, Tier II
**** Meal Types: Breakfast, Lunch/Supper, Supplement
** Administrative Rates for Sponsors of Day Care Homes
*** Initial 50 (1 through 50), Next 150 (51 through 200), Next 800 (201 through 1000), Additional (1001 and over)
**** 1 rate only for each

## Acceptance Criteria
* Default Academic Year set to current year on page load.
* Editing of Federal rates locked to the Customer Support AND Tech Support admin roles.
* User can set reimbursement rates per:
** State Category (Contiguous, Alaska, Hawaii)
** per academic year
** for both Federal and State types
* User can set rates for Centers
** by Meal Type (Breakfast, Lunch/Supper, Supplement)
** by Eligibility (Free, Reduced, Paid)
* User can set rates for At-Risk After School Meals
** by Meal Types: Breakfast, Lunch/Supper, Snacks
** by Eligibility (Free)
* User can set rates for Day Care Homes
** by Tiers: Tier I, Tier II
** by Meal Types: Breakfast, Lunch/Supper, Supplement
* User can set Administrative Rates for Sponsors of Day Care Homes
** by number of homes: Initial 50 (1 through 50), Next 150 (51 through 200), Next 800 (201 through 1000), Additional (1001 and over)
** just 1 reimbursement rate for each range of homes
