# NXT-47727 — PR-  Prep Site- Different Menu line Prep site scenario

## Description
As a production manager, When I am a prep site for sites that I do not have the same menu line for, I would like to always see the consolidated and prep site distribution regardless of whether the records have been completed at the receiving sites or not.

## Acceptance Criteria
* Always display the “Plan” on the calendar regardless of all the records being completed for the receiving sites
* Display a Received status below each site with the quantity received based on what was entered on the receiving site record (Previously existed)
** Received: 80
* Once all records have been completed and the prep site distribution has all the “received” statuses from the receiving sites, display the plan on the calendar in the Record complete color (Green Gradiant--- copy the same color as record complete with the style of prep site different menu line)
* Remove the box from being around the Shipping date and calendar
