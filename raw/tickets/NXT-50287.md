# NXT-50287 — PR- Grocery List- Prep site and receiving site items on report (API ONLY)

## Description
As a prep site, when I am pulling the grocery list for all the items I need to prepare for all other sites, they are currently not showing up on the grocery list for me to know.

## Acceptance Criteria
* All prepared menu item ingredients should show on grocery list on prep site report
** Display total for all menu item counts on the amount needed as a combined total
** Display all additional non prepared menu item ingredients for prep site
** Display total quantity needed for the combined total
* On receiving site, *only* display non prepared item ingredients
* THIS ONLY APPLIES IF PREP SITE AND RECEIVING SITE ARE DIFFERENT
