# NXT-34175 — PR- Production Record- Carryovers display on page and Page Redesign

## Description
As a production manager, currently, when I carryover a menu item from one day to another day, it is showing on the production plan, I would also like to be able to see it on the production record as to how much of the amount I have is carryover vs produced.

## Acceptance Criteria
* Display Carryover information for the menu item on the record 
** How much was carried over
** Who carried it over
** What date and menu line it came from 
* When bringing the carryover, calculate the carryover quantity as part of the total served once they enter a produced quantity 
** Carryover from =5 and total produced is 10 then the served amount would be 15
* Update the colors of the comment icons to match the new colors of the statuses
* Update the look for the serve with functionality along with the complimentary items
* Show basic information for complimentary items and show the contribution and amount in a hover
* Flip the X and Y axis on the planned and actual count to show the serving group vertically and the the actual and planned counts horizontal
