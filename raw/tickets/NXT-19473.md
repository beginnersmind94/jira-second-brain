# NXT-19473 — Production- Production Record- Navigate to another serving date

## Description
As a Kitchen Manager, I want the ability to be able to navigate to another serving date from a current production plan or record that I am on. 

Give the user a calendar pop out that they can be able to select another menu line either from the same day or a different day.
They should not have to go back to the production calendar to navigate to another day to fill production plans or records. 
Option for them to select any day they would like to navigate to as well as a quick view of the last serving day and next serving day from current date selection to give them quick access to all menu lines.

## Acceptance Criteria
Give calendar Icon next to the date on UI.

Give the user a calendar pop out that they can be able to select another menu line either from the same day or a different day.
They should not have to go back to the production calendar to navigate to another day to fill production plans or records. 
Option for them to select any day they would like to navigate to as well as a quick view of the last serving day and next serving day from current date selection to give them quick access to all menu lines.
