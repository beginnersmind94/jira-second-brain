# NXT-52865 — System - Special Programs - CEP out to 4 Decimals

## Description
Some states are now encouraging districts to perform CEP calculations out to 4 decimals before rounding. Compliance issue - we need to allow them to do this so they can enter accurate percentages.

!image-20240719-194207.png|width=701,height=414,alt="image-20240719-194207.png"!



*requirements*

* Add a setting:
** CEP Rounding - 2 or 4 Decimals
*** Code: CEPROUND
*** Options: 2, 4
*** Default: 2
*** Description: This controls whether CEP percentages are calculated out to 2 or 4 decimals before rounding takes place.
* If the setting is 2, current functionality is fine.
* If the setting is 4, carry out the Identified Student Percentage calculation to 4 decimal places before continuing on.

## Acceptance Criteria
* Setting for 2 or 4 Decimals added.
* Setting used for CEP Calculations and setup.
