# NXT-23815 — INV - Vendors - Contracts - Provide a proper view/manage screen for Contracts

## Description
Provide a proper view or screen for viewing and updating an existing contract.

* Currently the view/edit mode is simply reopening the Add Contract stepper 
* This may also include separating the management of Delivery Schedule from the overall contract attributes

+*Contract tab*+

* Add Delivery Schedule column
** show the days component
** optionally provide a shortcut action to open the delivery schedule for updating
* Remove the ‘Go to Items’ and ‘Go to Ordering Conditions’ actions

+*Contract Details*+

* Provide a dedicated screen that is opened when the view icon is clicked for an individual contract
* Show all contract attributes on one page
* Show the vendor delivery schedule 
** provide an icon/button to open/manage the delivery schedule (including sites)
