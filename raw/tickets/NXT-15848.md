# NXT-15848 — Accounts - Account Info - Make Payments for Multiple Students

## Description
As a central office admin, I would like to be able to provide patrons with the option to split one payment among multiple accounts, so that I do not have to enter the same details 2 or more times for different accounts.

*Summary*

Currently, in Account Info, only a single payment for a single payment can be made. We need to implement the ability to make a single payment for multiple accounts, such as a parent who wants to pay with one check but have the money split between 2 or 3 (or sometimes more) children.

*Details*

* In Account Management → Accounts → Quick Preview/Account Info, in the Account Actions list, when a user clicks the button to Make a Payment:
** For each of the options (Cash, Check, Card), add the ability for the user to add multiple other accounts to the payment +*only if*+ the student has other (active or inactive) students in their household:
*** In this case only, display a checkbox: “_Split payment between other students in this household”_ (unchecked by default)
*** When the user checks this box, display their fellow student household members in a list, along with an ‘Amount’ field for each student.
*** Beneath them, display a (*read-only*) 'Total Payment Amount' field.
*** When the user enters a value in the amount field, update the ‘Total Payment Amount’ field with the sum of all amounts.
*** The ‘Amount’/‘Total Payment Amount’ fields should be subject to the district’s min/max payment settings. i.e. if there are 3 students in a household and the maximum payment allowed is $100, then the sum of all ‘Amount’ values must be <= 100.
*** If one or more individual students in the household have checks blocked, disable their Amount fields.
** The Previous Balance, Payment Amount, and Bonus Amount values at the bottom should be unchanged--they should still only apply to the student where we first clicked the ‘Make Payment’ button. i.e. if student A is the primary student, but we split a $100 payment (with a 10% bonus) giving $60 to student A and $40 to student B, we should only show the payment amount of $60 and the bonus amount of $6 here (while student B’s transaction history would show a $40 payment with a $4 bonus). And the previous/new balances should belong to student A also. 
** If the district setting for bonus is a flat amount, split the bonus evenly along the students in the payment (rather than as a percentage of their individual payment like in the above case).
** When the payment is saved, a transaction+receipt should be created that applies the payment to each of the accounts listed
* This story is complete when the specific functionality requested above is accessible in *both* the Quick Preview and the full Account Info page.

## Acceptance Criteria
For each of the Account Action payment options (Cash, Check, Card) verify that:

* The user can add multiple other accounts to receive a share of the payment, if those other accounts are a part of the same household
* The user can indicate the portion of the payment amount that would go toward each account added, with the total amount for all accounts being equal to the total payment amount
* The usual payment validation cases (maximum amounts, no checks, etc.) still apply
* If one or more individual students in the household have checks blocked, disable their Amount fields.
* The user can save the payment and view a created transaction+receipt that applies the payment to each of the accounts listed
* The specific functionality requested above is accessible in *both* the Quick Preview and the full Account Info page.
