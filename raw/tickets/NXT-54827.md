# NXT-54827 — Workspace - MWD - Acc - High Level Stats

## Description
As Dana the Director I want to see a high level total for site statistics to get a quick perspective on site performance before I drill down for more information.

*requirements*

User has established site(s) and date(s) with the prior story

* User can see high level totals for the following stats:
** Meals
*** Total number of meals served to students
*** This should include second meals to students
** Payments
*** Total $$ of payments made
** Revenue
*** Total revenue $$ made
** Participation %
*** % of eligible students who have obtained a meal
* User can mouseover / click on each stat and see a brief explanation and to reference the report they can check to verify counts:
** Meals: Meals shown here are across all meal types for students and include second meals. The Meal Count report will split this count by meal type.
** Payments: Payments shown here are inclusive of cash, card, check, and online payments. The Payments report will split this by payment method.
** Revenue: Revenue shown here includes all meals and a la cartes to both students and adults. The Revenue report will split this out further.
** Participation %: Percentage shown here is the average across all meals served. The Participation report will show a raw count, and the Edit Check will provide a percentage against attendance factor.

Notes

* We are not doing this by a particular meal type - we are showing a high level total.
