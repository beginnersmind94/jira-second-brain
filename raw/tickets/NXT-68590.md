# NXT-68590 — IM - Bulk Item - Check Updates

## Description
As a District Manager updating my items through the bulk update tool, I would like for a screen that would allow me to check the items that I have made updates to and what sections were updated.

## Acceptance Criteria
* Provide 3 Sections within the Check Updates 
** Pending Updates
*** All items that had changes made that are valid and or not  missing Mandatory information
** Partial Updates
*** Items that have had changes made that are still missing Mandatory Information
** No Updates
*** Items that have not been changed/altered within the session
* Provide an “Update Items” button for the Pending Updates section
* Provide a “Remove From Session” for the No Updates section
** This will allow for items to be removed out of the session and “released” from being locked
*** Provide prompt  “Are you sure you want to remove items from session? Yes/No
