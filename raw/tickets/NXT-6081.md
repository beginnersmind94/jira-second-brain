# NXT-6081 — Application Entry - Guided Process (5. Details)

## Description
*User Story* 

As an applicant, i can enter optional information not required to determine eligibility.

*Mock Ups* 

[https://www.figma.com/proto/yrkJeGfd3f9hWjqm0qvfir/Edge2.0_V1?node-id=1306%3A27072&scaling=min-zoom|https://www.figma.com/proto/yrkJeGfd3f9hWjqm0qvfir/Edge2.0_V1?node-id=1306%3A27072&scaling=min-zoom]

*Requirements* 

* Details page is organized into sections for optional Info 
* Optional Info selections
** Ethnicity
*** Hispanic or Latino
*** Not Hispanic or Latino
*** Not Provided (default)
** Racial Identity 
*** Asian
*** American Indian or Alaskan Native
*** Black or African American
*** Native Hawaiian or Other Pacific Islander
*** White
** Consent to Release Meal Eligibility/Other Benefits --- Default: not checked
*** Other benefits set by district for the current AY - previous apps will show previous benefits based on the AY. Shown based on *Benefits Configuration* page.
**** [https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-5208|https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-5208|smart-link] 
***** Show benefit name pulled from Configuration (above)
*** Information Sharing 
**** Checkbox 
* Applicant can go to previous page or next step

## Acceptance Criteria
- applicant is guided to next step 
- applicant has option to go to previous step 
- applicant can enter optional info questions
- optional info includes sections listed above
- Multi-language, ADA compliant.
