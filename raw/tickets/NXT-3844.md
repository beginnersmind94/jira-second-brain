# NXT-3844 — User Scorecard

## Description
*User Story*
As a School Nutrition professional, I want to view an Employee level Scorecard provided by PrimeroEdge that scores individual Employees as a whole using default KPIs and Metrics based on PrimeroEdge’s wealth of industry knowledge and data.

*Mockup*


*Requirements*

* Standard scorecard using a specific set of KPIs and Metrics are used by PrimeroEdge for all Districts.
* Use industry standards/national averages based on PrimeroEdge customer data to establish scoring calculations.
* Enrollment, FRE %, Site Type, and other variables maybe be automatically factored into calculations as schools may have different expectations for their Employees.
* Scoring is presented in an easy-to-understand scale
** A-F
** 0-100
* Each section (KPI, Metric) is scored individually.
* Overall weighted score presented at the top of the scorecard
