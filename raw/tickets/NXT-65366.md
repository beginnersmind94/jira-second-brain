# NXT-65366 — MP- Menu Cycle Analysis- CACFP - Use MMA as Grain (Breakfast)

## Description
As a USDA requirement for CACFP. districts are able to use MMA as grain for breakfast up to 3 times a week. Currently, our analysis does not check against this or provide the correct calculations for the substitutions.

## Acceptance Criteria
* On the *Menu Cycle Analysis*, if user toggles MMA to use as grain, consider the component amount towards the grain calculation and not the MMA, currently the analysis is considering it as part of the MMA calculation
** This applies to the *slide-out*, *nutrition report* and *Food component report*
* Change % Total to “Daily Check” and Change the Actual to “Weekly Total” on the *slide-out* and *nutrition report* on the weekly analysis 
!image-20250811-121233.png|width=1888,height=850,alt="image-20250811-121233.png"!

** Daily Check should be a pass/fail based on if the menu cycle has passed every daily standard for the week
*** Slide Pass/Fail for Milk Variety as part of the daily check and do not show any actual value
*** Any daily fail should trigger the overall fail on the *slide out* and the *nutrition report*
**** Do not show pass/fail for components that have no daily standards
* Districts are only allowed to use MMA as a grain up to 3 times a week
** Provide a *weekly check* on the M/MMA acting as a grain to ensure users can see if they are using MMA as a grain more than 3 times a week
*** This applies to the *Food Component Report*
**** This will also fail the entire analysis
*** Apply this weekly check on the *slide out* and *nutrition report* and highlight the Weekly Total if district has used MMA as a grain more than 3 times for the week
**** This will also fail the entire analysis
* Update calculations to show M/MA acting as a grains per day and the grains calculations should be a combination of Actual Grains (GBD already included) + M/MA acting as Grains = Total Grains
** This applies to the *slide-out*, *nutrition report* and *Food component report*
* Grain Based Desserts may not be used to meet the grains requirement but may be offered in additional to a grain or MMA acting as a grain
** This applies to the *slide-out*, *nutrition report* and *Food component report*
* Remove weekly checks as well as all veg subgroups as those do not apply to CACFP
** This applies to the *slide-out*, *nutrition report* and *Food component report*
* Remove all checks against MMA for pass or fail
** This applies to the *Food Component Report*
* Remove all checks against Actual Grains and M/MA acting as grains and only check against grains total  per day
** This applies to the *Food Component Report*
