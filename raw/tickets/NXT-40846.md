# NXT-40846 — PR- Carryover Logic with Record Complete

## Description
As a production manager, if I do not complete my record, do not move my carryover information to the future date. This should only happen once I have confirmed that the record is complete.

## Acceptance Criteria
* DO not push “Carryover to” information to the future plan unless the “carryover from” record is complete
** Maintain a flag for “is carried over” 
* IF menu item is carried over but not in record complete, need to check the “can serve” or “ Menu item Planned” when sending the information to the future “Carryover to” plan when record is finally completed
