# NXT-3339 — System - Management - Roles - Allow user to view standard roles

## Description
As a district administrator, I would like to be able to easily view a list of all standard roles within my organization as needed.



+*Summary:*+

* Within the Management category, add a *Roles* page 
* When the user (with permission) selects the link, redirect them to the Roles page
* Display available rows by default, broken into two sections:
** Standard Roles
*** A standard role is a role that is system-generated as an out-of-the-box role for users getting started with PrimeroEdge, or a role that has been created at the central realm.
** Custom Roles
*** A custom role is a role that a user has created themselves, either based off a copy of another role or from scratch.
* Display their Role Name, Organization Level, number of module the role provides access to, and Role Description (may not yet be reflected on the mock-up). The following will be our standard roles for now:
** Name: *Director*
*** Org. Level: District
*** Description: For users who require full control over all module permissions, district-wide.
** Name: *Central Office Administrator*
*** Org. Level: District, Area
*** Description: For users who assist Director-level users with most operations, but do not have any miscellaneous  permissions.
** Name: *Manager* 
*** Org. Level: Area, Site
*** Description: For users who manage all aspects within one or more sites, but do not have any miscellaneous or special permissions.  
** Name: *Cashier*
*** Org. Level: Site
*** Description: For users who have limited permissions within a single site.
** Name: *Read-Only*
*** Org. Level: District
*** Description: For users who only need to view information throughout the district.
* Each role should have an accordion that opens to show which specific modules the role provides access to
