# NXT-4053 — Security - Password Constraints Framework

## Description
*User Story*
As a PrimeroEdge user, I have constraints when creating a password to ensure it matches certain strength requirements
As a PrimeroEdge admin, I have the ability to configure constraints based on my own security preferences.

*Requirements*

* Passwords must meet certain constraints when being created
** These constraints can be configured by an organization admin
** PrimeroEdge can set a minimum standard that organization admins cannot go around
* Passwords can be constrained in the following ways:
** Minimum Length
*** Range: 8-20
** Require Mixed Password (both upper and lower case letters)
*** Yes/No Toggle
** Require certain number of numbers
*** Range: 0-10
** Require certain number of special characters
*** Special characters defined as any non-alphanumeric characters appearing on a US English keyboard
*** Range: 0-10
** Maximum Password Age
*** Values - 30 Days, 45 Days, 60 Days, 75 Days, 90 Days
** Refusal of Previously Used Passwords
*** Can be previous x number of passwords - x is configurable
*** Range: 3-10, Never
** Max Failed Attempts
*** Range: 3-10
** Failed Attempt Lockout Time
*** Values: 15 min, 30 min, 45 min, 60 min, 75 min, 90 min
** Inactivity Timeout
*** Range: 5-60 in increments of 5 (5, 10, 15, etc.)
** Inactivity Warning
*** Range: 1-10, None
** Exclude certain words (password or primeroedge, for example)
*** This requirement should check for all letter cases
* An error should be present that includes all requirements based on configuration
* Passwords can be set to expire after a certain amount of time
