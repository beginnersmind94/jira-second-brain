# NXT-3254 — Employee Details - View Training (Pop Over)

## Description
Ability to view training records for an employee in their profile 

* default current academic year records
* drop down for previous academic years with training records (only show those years with training events recorded) 
* clicking on the Training Event chip shows topic, subtopics, method, source (pulled from Training Events configuration/page)
* Common component

## Acceptance Criteria
Training event records are displayed in with pop over component - click Event name to see details of that training event 
Default to current academic year 
admin can choose from drop down for previous year with records (only those years with records are displayed in drop down)
