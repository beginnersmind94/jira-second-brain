# NXT-2998 — Employee Details - History

## Description
As an admin, i want to see the history of changes made to the employee profile to easily audit updates since there's more than one admin in our district. 

* Show history of changes made to employee details.
* FYI - History not in current version of team work but here’s a quick mock up to show what it looks like in other modules 
* need to know- who, what, when was changed
** Time Stamp <Date, Time> - <User/User Name> made changes to <updated field(s)> from <old value> to <new value>

* Tracks the CEU hours needed with the current/past role of employee
* Example:
For example, if the admin runs a report in September 2019 and Employee A = program staff with 6 hours complete, they are in compliance. If that same employee is promoted in February 2020 to program manager (10 hours), then when the auditor runs the report in Feb the NOW manager will be short 4 hours because they only got 6 originally. Employee A will have until June 30th to get the remaining 4 hours with the mid-year promotion difference

## Acceptance Criteria
History of changes made to the employee profile are tracked and shown in the employee details 
Demo that history is only shown on view/edit, not add
Reporting shows that past CEU level(s) for employee are considered and accurate when running a report from the past (example above)
