# NXT-16997 — INV - Orders - Order Detail - Receipts tab - Edit Receipt Details - Items

## Description
As an operations manager I want the ability to edit items on an existing receipt so I can update item received quantities or lot info.

This is for only loading the editable Items section of a receipt. There are separate stories for loading the receipt details and editing the header ([NXT-15379|https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-15379]), Charges & Discounts ([NXT-16998|https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-16998]) , and saving the receipt ([NXT-17418|https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-17418])

Note: this functionality is dependent on the user having the ‘Edit Receipt’ permission (separate story)

**Note:* no API for this story (covered in NXT-15379)

*Requirements*

* When Edit button is clicked, re-enable the item receipt fields
** Receive Quantity
*** Confirm checkboxes
** Unit Price
*** editable based on permission
** Charge/Discount columns
*** Processing Fee
*** Delivery Fee
*** Net Off Invoice
** Total Price (Qty * Unit Price)
** Lot Info - as a sub-grid
*** Lot Number
*** Date on Product (Best By, Expires, Packed)
*** Date - date picker
*** Receive Quantity - numeric entry field
*** Add button to add another row for Lot Info
