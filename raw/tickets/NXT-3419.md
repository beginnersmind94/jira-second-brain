# NXT-3419 — Acct Mgmt - Groups - Create new combined page with Rosters & Homerooms

## Description
*Summary:*

Just like with the Notifications page housing the various types of letters, let’s create a Groups page to house the various types of groups.

*Mockup*:

!GroupsPage.png|width=783,height=494!



*Details*:

* In Account Management, create/enable a page for *Groups*
* Within this page, add two tabs:
** Rosters
*** Within this tab, move the content from the former Rosters page 
** Homerooms
*** Within this tab, move the content from the former Homerooms page 
* When the user selects a tab, load the content of those pages respectively.
* Also, maintain an ‘Add New’ button. 
** The previous Add New button actions for the two page can be combined into one, just like we’re doing in the Patrons/Account page (i.e. ‘Add New’ should give the user the option of adding a Roster or a Homeroom, and then trigger the respective pop-up based on the user’s selection)
* Remove/disable the old Rosters and Homerooms pages from the navigation.

## Acceptance Criteria
* Verify that, in Account Management, a page for Groups has been created or enabled
* Verify that selecting that menu directs the user to a Groups page with two tabs:
  * Rosters 
  * Homerooms
    
* Verify that when the user selects a tab, the application loads the content of those two pages respectively.

* Verify ‘Add New’ button gives the user the option of adding a Roster or a Homeroom, and then triggers the respective pop-up based on the user’s selection

* Verify that we have removed/disabled the old Rosters and Homerooms pages from the navigation
