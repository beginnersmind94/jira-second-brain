# NXT-65090 — MP - Menu Planner - Update and Combining Grains and MMA for Daily and Weekly (SBP 14+)

## Description
Based on new requirements, analysis for breakfast should be combining the Grains and MMA values when making sure a district is compliant with USDA standards. 

!beansandgrain.svg|alt="beansandgrain.svg"!

## Acceptance Criteria
* On the *daily analysis*, update the Component of Grains to Grains/MMA and no longer display Meat/Meat Alternate on the card
* On the *weekly analysis*, update the Component of Grains to Grains/MMA and no longer display Meat/Meat Alternate on the card
* Calculations for this will be a combination of both components together (Use same logic as before but combine the calculations)
** Continue to calculate the grains 80% WGR rule on any grains offered within the combined totals
*** Per USDA “School may offer grains, meats/meat alternates, or a combination of both to meet the daily and weekly ounce equivalents for this combined component. At least 80 percent of grains offered weekly at breakfast must be whole grain-rich”
* No longer display disclaimers if applicable
** 6. Schools may substitute 1 oz. M/MA for Grains after the minimum daily Grains requirement is met.
** 7. The daily requirement for Grains must be met before substitution of M/MA for Grains is allowed.
** 8. USDA is offering flexibility by allowing SFAs to exceed the maximum amount of grains and meat/meat meal alternates as long as the daily and weekly minimums for these components are met and the calories are within the required range.
