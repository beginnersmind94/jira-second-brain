# NXT-68660 — Implement Site-by-Site Performance Table

## Description
District leadership needs a consolidated, at-a-glance view of financial performance across sites and key revenue/expense drivers. This dashboard section allows administrators to quickly identify high-performing or underperforming sites and understand which revenue streams and expense categories contribute most to the district’s financial position for the selected period.

## Acceptance Criteria
h2. *1. Site-by-Site Performance Table*

h3. *Data Included*

* Revenue, expense, and net income totals for each site.
* Aggregated district total row at the bottom.
* Only posted GL transactions included.

h3. *Columns*

* *Site Name*
* *Revenue*
* *Expenses*
* *Net Income* (Revenue – Expenses)

h3. *Behavior*

* Sortable by Net Income (default: descending)
** Also by site name
* Any site with no data for the selected period:
** Show {{- }}in all columns.
** Only show data for active sites
* *Filtering Logic*
** Uses the *selected period* from the period dropdown at the top of the dashboard

----

h2. *-2. Revenue Analysis Card — Top 5 Revenue Sources-*

h3. *-Purpose-*

-Show the top 5 revenue categories contributing to the district’s total revenue for the selected period.-

h3. *-Data Rules-*

* -Use GL accounts categorized with a- *-Revenue-* -base type.-
* -Group by- *-category-*
* -Calculate:-
** -Total revenue for each category.-
** -Percentage share of total district revenue.-
* -Display- *-top 5-* -categories sorted descending by revenue amount.-

h3. *-UI Elements-*

* -Category name-
* -Dollar value-
* -Percentage contribution-

h3. *-Behavior-*

* -If fewer than 5 categories exist, show all available.-
* -If no revenue exists, show empty-state with “No revenue data for the selected period.”-

----

h2. *-3. Expense Analysis Card — Top 5 Expense Categories-*

h3. *-Purpose-*

-Show the top 5 categories driving district expense for the selected period.-

h3. *-Data Rules-*

* -Use GL accounts categorized with an- *-Expense-* -base type.-
* -Group by category.-
* -Calculate:-
** -Total expenses per category.-
** -Percentage of total district expenses.-
* -Display- *-top 5-* -categories sorted descending.-

h3. *-UI Elements-*

* -Category name-
* -Dollar amount-
* -Percentage share-
* -Progress bar-

h3. *-Behavior-*

* -If fewer than 5 categories exist, show all.-
* -If no expenses exist, show empty-state.-
