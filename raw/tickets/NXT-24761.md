# NXT-24761 — MP > Menu Planner > Time Stamp (Label & Edit)

## Description
When viewing the Menu Planner page, there is a date/time stamp visible on the right hand side of the page.

1.) This time stamp needs to have the AM & PM in capital letters... as AM PM  and NOT am pm.

2.) Prior to creating a new menu on this page, the date/time stamp needs to be "invisible" or Not Appear to the users, until such time as they actually save data on the page. 

3.) There should be a label in front of that date/time stamp, so that when a user comes back to the page and sees it, they understand what the purpose of the date/time stamp is. (i.e. it is not a "current" date/time stamp, and is not intended to match the date/time on the computer) -- Label should be "Last Saved" and appear to the left of the date/time stamp.
