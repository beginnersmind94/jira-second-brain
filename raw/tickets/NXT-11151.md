# NXT-11151 — SchoolCafe Integration - Real-time payment synchronization

## Description
Payment information made in SchoolCafe shall be kept synchronized with Perseus in real-time.

## Acceptance Criteria
Changes to payment information in SchoolCafe shall be synchronized with Perseus within 5 seconds.

If SchoolCafe or Perseus are unavailable to be synchronized for a period of time, there shall be a mechanism to synchronize them once the 2 systems are available.

This mechanism shall be performant and scalable.
