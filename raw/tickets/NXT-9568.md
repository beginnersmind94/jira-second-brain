# NXT-9568 — INV - Transfers (Site) - Create Transfer - Confirm logic

## Description
As a site level Inventory user I want a confirmation screen for my site-to-site transfers so I can view only the items which I am transferring and confirm the transfer.

After the user has created a transfer and clicked Next, they will be brought to the Transfer Confirmation screen.

Continuation of [https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-9409|https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-9409|smart-link]

+Action buttons:+

* Confirm Transfer
** this will confirm and officially save the transfer
*** Transfer # is created 
*** reload the page and provide the user with the Transfer Number
*** message: Transfer #12345 has been created with a status of “Sent”
*** Perpetual is updated at both sites
** once this is done, no more edits are allowed
*** remove the Edit Transfer and Confirm Transfer button
** after the Transfer is confirmed, the page will provide additional actions:
*** Transfer Ticket
*** Price Details
