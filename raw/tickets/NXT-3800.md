# NXT-3800 — AcctMgt - Patron Split View - Eligibility / Benefits

## Description
*User Story*

As a district administrator I want to see the eligibility of a patron along with benefits and surveys. 

*Mockup*

[https://nqyc9v.axshare.com/start.html#p=category_based_on_patron_view&id=jytwv5&g=1|https://nqyc9v.axshare.com/start.html#p=category_based_on_patron_view&id=jytwv5&g=1|https://nqyc9v.axshare.com/start.html#p=category_based_on_patron_view&id=jytwv5&g=1]

*Requirements*

* Add an Eligibility / Benefits section to the Patron Details panel. 
* Three-way toggle to change the information between Eligibility, Surveys and Benefits. 
** Eligibility should display by default. 
** Section will show information on historical eligibility 
** Grid showing: 
*** Eligibility (Free/Reduced/Paid) 
*** Reason 
*** Start Date 
*** End Date 
*** Source 
**** This is the eligibility source. Application #, DC File #, Manual. 
**** Eligibility Module will handle the first two. 
*** Name of the processor 
*** Process Date 
*** Comments 
** Grid should show the top 3 entries only. The rest are hidden behind a ‘View More' button. 
** This button will bring in a drawer that shows the full eligibility history on an account. 
* Benefits and Surveys are placeholders for now and should cause the panel to reload dynamically.
* New Edit requirements (7/17/20):
** While viewing the full history in the side-drawer, the top entry should have an edit option available.
*** Only the top entry can be edited.
*** Only the Start Date can be adjusted.
**** Start Date cannot go past the start of the Academic Year (7/1/XX)
** When saving, the 2nd from top record will update to have an End Date of ‘Start Date - 1’ if there is a need to adjust the dates.
* -Add an Edit button to the section.-
** -This activates an edit mode.-
** -Eligibility entries can be adjusted/edited, soft-deleted. Buttons are in-line.-
*** -Dates must make sense - there cannot be overlapping dates.-
*** -User needs appropriate messaging / feedback if the dates are going to overlap.-
**** -The eligibility dates cannot overlap. Edit the existing records so that there is an open date range available.-
*** -Available changes must be appropriate - the Reason dropdown is controlled by the chosen Eligibility for instance.-
** -Editable information:-
*** -Eligibility (Free/Reduced/Paid)- 
*** -Reason- 
*** -Start Date- 
*** -End Date- 
*** -Comments- 
** -Save/cancel buttons.-

## Acceptance Criteria
Eligibility section added.
Data displays appropriately.
Most recent entry can have its start date edited - the 2nd most recent has its end date changed if needed.
Multi-language support enabled.
