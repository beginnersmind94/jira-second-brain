# NXT-3819 — Account Management - Advanced Search - Restrictions Section

## Description
The Restrictions Section is a collection of three subsections, Allergens, Charge Restrictions, and No Charge reasons.

!Screen Shot 2020-04-29 at 11.36.28 AM.png|width=286,height=171!

## Acceptance Criteria
When the Restrictin section is expanded the 3 sub sections are available.

When values are selected from multiple subsections they are 'AND'ed together

When a filter is selected from one of the subsections the filter selection count is increased by 1.

When a filter is removed from one of the subsection the filter selection count is decreased by 1.
