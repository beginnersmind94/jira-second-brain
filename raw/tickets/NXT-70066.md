# NXT-70066 — FIN - Summary Cards Redesign

## Description
As a CN Director, I want the top-of-page cards to tell me what matters right now — progress, problems, and dollars — not system job counts.

## Acceptance Criteria
* Replace the four Phase 1 cards (Total Jobs, Completed, Failed, Running) with three cards in a horizontal grid
* Card 1 — Posting Progress: label is the current fiscal period name (uppercase), value shows "X of Y sites posted", thin green progress bar underneath proportional to completion. A site counts as "posted" only when all its posting types are complete. When all posted: "All Y sites posted ✓"
* Card 2 — Needs Attention: value shows failed/unresolved count in red. Clickable — scrolls to and filters the job history table to failed jobs. When zero: "No issues ✓" in green
* Card 3 — Posted This Period: value shows total dollar amount of ledger entries for the current period in green. Subtitle shows delta vs prior period (e.g., "↑ $4,200 vs Dec 2025")
* Cards are compact (matching Phase 1 density), white background, text only — no icons, no illustrations, no decorative graphics
* The old job counts (total, completed, failed, running) move to the job history table section header as inline muted text: "Posting Jobs (Last 30 Days) · 25 total · 21 completed · 2 failed · 1 running"
* Cards update dynamically after posting actions
