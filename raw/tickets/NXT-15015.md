# NXT-15015 — Acct.Mgmt - Outreach - Add Outreach Center page, migrate Household Letters tab into it

## Description
As a central office admin, I would like to be able to have a single location to send all of my marketing material out to households in my district, so that I always know where to go to send those types of letters. 

*Details*

* There are 3 parts to this story: 
** In Account Management, create a new page/navigation link: *Outreach.*
*** Navigation menu: *Outreach*
*** URL: *outreach-center*
*** Page title: *Outreach Center*
*** Breadcrumbs: Account Management > Outreach
** The *Household Letters* page is currently housed in Eligibility → Notifications, but belongs in Outreach. Let’s move it out of that page and into the Outreach Center as a dedicated tab.
** !image-20211210-185338.png|width=888,height=209!
Minor UI/UX changes:
*** The Site/Site Name fields should be changed to show the ‘+1+,’+2', etc. styling instead of each site name overflowing the field.
*** The vertical checkboxes should be changed to horizontal, and the text descriptions shortened also.
*** Rename ID column to ‘Account ID’, View column to ‘Actions’
*** Add Field Search functionality.
*** Stretch goal, but not required: add a proper ‘All’ option to the Site drop down, still selected by default (instead of blank functioning as ‘All’).
** Lastly, let’s add 2 temporary tabs to the page (will be fleshed out in future stories):
*** Quick Broadcast
*** Campaign
