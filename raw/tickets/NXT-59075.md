## Acceptance Criteria
* Make page updates to Standard Ordering
** Move the Back to List button from top right to left side of sticky footer (like other Inventory pages)
** Change the Cart button to gray/disabled until items have been loaded
* Update the Order Criteria header
** this should not be a collapsible “Filters” card
** make this a normal card as small as possible to fit required fields
** make Date fields no wider than minimum necessary length
** disable Order Date by default; user cannot modify
*** (TBD - Backdate permission)
* Modify Delivery Date to ‘Delivery Due Date’
** make this a mandatory attribute 
** move to left side of header vertical divider
* Display an ‘Exact Delivery Date’ toggle switch under the Delivery Due Date field after date selection
** default to Off
* Populate the items according to updated DDD logic
** Load all items with delivery dates as close possible to the selected Due Date
** If ‘Exact Delivery Date’ is selected, load only the items deliverable on that date 
*** this behavior is how the page currently works when Delivery Date is provided
* Show Available Quantity column based on internal vendor contract settings
** consider ‘Show QOH to Site’ contract setting
** then display Actual or Projected based on setting
** *Note*: consider appending to Unit Description column instead of making a dedicated column
*** preferred approach
* Display soft warning when site orders more than internal vendor available quantity
** Use the orange alert icon for existing Min/Max Qty soft warnings
** “Warning: order quantity exceeds vendor available quantity.”
