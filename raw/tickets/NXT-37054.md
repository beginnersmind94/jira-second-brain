# NXT-37054 — IM - Item Master - Serving Sizes - Provide Volume for fluid items

## Description
As a Nutrionist I need the ability to identify fluid items and use volume instead of weight so I can scale my ingredients according to volume conversions.

*Requirements*

* Add the concept of Volume to replace Weight for fluid items
** Fluid items are identified in the new Create Item flow ([NXT-37051|https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-37051]) in the context of the combined Servings and Packs section
* Set *Milliliter (ml)* as the gram-equivalent basis for Volume
* Save the volume conversions so they can be used for scaling
** See attached volume conversion chart image
* Assign a conversion rate from milliliter to gram (we still need to use weight for recipe calculation)
** right now this will have to be 1:1
** in the future we may save gram weight per volume if we know the measure fluid type
*** ex: olive oil weighs 90 grams for every 100 ml, but milk weighs 103 grams
