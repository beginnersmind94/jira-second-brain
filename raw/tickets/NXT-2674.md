# NXT-2674 — Training Events - Add New

## Description
At the Central and District Realm, administrators can create training events and add necessary details along with supporting documentation. 

* ‘Add New Training' fields 
** Name*, 
** Description/Title*, 
** Method* (how it’s being presented/where - webinar, classroom, etc),
**  Source* (who is offering the training), 
** Hours*, 
** Mandatory*, 
** Active* (default), 
** Topic(s)*, Subtopic(s), 
** Created Date (autopopulate)
** Documents - scan/upload/history, make recurring event option/radio button (include ppt, mp4 formats)
*** * indicates required field
