# NXT-24940 — PR- Prep Site Shipping Quantities to receiving Site

## Description
Once the prep site has entered in their shipping counts and the counts have shipped, those counts need to come over to the receiving site production records.

## Acceptance Criteria
* Shipped counts should appear on the production record of the receiving sites
* Add a shipped column that indicates how much was Shipped from the prep site. This is prepopulated with the shipped count the prep site sent \[Read only]
* Remove the Produced Column on prepared items and replace with a received  (They should be the same but could potentially be different)
* The received quantity will act as the “produced” quantity and will do the same functionalities as far as calculations with total served, carryover, reimbursable and non reimbursable columns 
** When there are received items, Category and record totals should show : Produced / Received
* IF distribution is not completed and the record is available, display – in the shipped column, do not allow user to enter the received amount until distribution is complete.
**  Hover “Prep site has not shipped this menu item”
* Do not display quantity prepared for prep site items
* Once status of shipping in progress, allow counts to show on the receiving site
** If any site completes their record with the received amount, disable the prep site distribution tab from any edits being made to counts or date (Individual Disabling of site counts will be handled in another story)
**  Disable the consolidated plan tab from any edits being made if any item is in shipped status and or a receiving site has received the item

These changes should reflect on these reports:

*Production Record*

*Production Form*
