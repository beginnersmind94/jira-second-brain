# NXT-26046 — UX - Share the saved view with other roles/groups -- Custom Views

## Description
As a user I need a way to share the save views I've created with other roles/groups based on their role/permissions.

*Note*: Screenshot for role/groups are attached for reference.

## Acceptance Criteria
Acceptance criteria will be met when the mock up for below functionality is ready:
1. When I click on share button of a particular saved view, I should see a popup containing a search bar where I can search for other roles/groups based on their role/permissions.

2. After clicking share button the view should be shared with the other roles/groups.
