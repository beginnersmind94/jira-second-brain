## Acceptance Criteria
* Provide a confirmation view of the affected menus where the menu item before & after can be viewed
* At time of loading Confirm step, re-verify the replacement details
** menu item must still exist on menu to be replaced
** Throw an error if any replacement issues exist
*** “One or more Menu items do not exist on selected Menus”
** Reset user’s menu list or move user back to menu selection step
*** Remove menu that menu item no longer exists on
*** Hold selections that were previously made
* Provide sticky footer button for official replacement
* Once the final action is clicked and the menu items are officially removed/replaced/etc., leave the user on the confirm step with actions removed
** adjust sticky footer message to be in past tense (reference mockup) (Replaced/Removed)
** Display ‘Start Over’ button in place of sticky footer cancel/back
* On the Menu item Pending Deactivation slideout in IM , the find and replace link should continue to be opened in a new tab but landing on the menu item tab of F&R
