# NXT-104 — Accounts - Merge

## Description
Ability to merge 2 account records. In the case of duplication and separate accounts having various transactions and balance, we want the ability to merge the 2 records while choosing the primary account to keep.

This functionality can be reviewed for inclusion in 2.0. We may not have a need and should definitely wait until further down the road (Phase 2).
