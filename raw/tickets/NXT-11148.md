# NXT-11148 — ADF - SchoolCafe Integration - Real-time Patron synchronization - Site and Persons 

## Description
Patron information shall be kept synchronized with Perseus in real-time.

## Acceptance Criteria
* If SchoolCafe or Perseus are unavailable to be synchronized for a period of time, there shall be a mechanism to synchronize them once the 2 systems are available.
* This mechanism shall be performant and scalable.
* Account balances shall be synchronized with student information
* Food restrictions shall be synchronized with student information
* At student onboarding time in Perseus, student information from an import shall propagate to SchoolCafe (batch support).
* Eligibility and Direct Certification updates shall be synchronized with SchoolCafe as part of the import
