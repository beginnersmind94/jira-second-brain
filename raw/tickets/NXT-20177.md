# NXT-20177 — Income Survey - Pending Surveys

## Description
As a district admin, I would like to be able to filter out my list of Income Surveys to only those that are pending, so that I easily know which ones to continue trying to process.

*No UX Required*

*Summary*

Enable the ‘Pending’ tab of the Income Surveys page, which is how to view all surveys in ‘Pending’ status.

*Requirements*

* When the user selects the ‘Pending’ tab of the Income Surveys page, only display those surveys in ‘Pending’ status.
* Default columns:
** Survey #
** Entry Method
** Received Date
** Student Name(s)
** Actions
*** View Survey (icon)
*** Out of scope: Delete. We will add a separate story for this.
* Hidden columns:
** Same remaining columns as on the All tab. 
*** Minus Received Date, since it is showing above, and minus Processed Date since it is unnecessary.
*** If possible, display Added Date also - {color:#ff5630}this is being skipped as it will be in survey history{color}
* Also out of scope: Pending Students. We will also address pending students at a later time.
