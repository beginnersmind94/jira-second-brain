# NXT-175 — Accountability - Reports - Paid Lunch Equity

## Description
As Vera the Director I want to collect information pertaining to paid lunch prices so I can make adjustments to pricing in the future.

*requirements*

* Add the Paid Lunch Equity page under Reports.
** User can select a date range and run the report.
** Date range should not exceed 1 year.

The report will take the date range and:

* Gather the following data:
** Paid Lunch Count (this is for students only)
** The amount paid for those lunches
** Calculate the revenue (Paid Lunch Count * Paid Lunch Price = Revenue)
* Report should provide totals.
* Report should also provide a Weighted Average Price that is just the average of the Paid Lunch Prices rounded down.
