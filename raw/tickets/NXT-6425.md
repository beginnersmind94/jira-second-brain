# NXT-6425 — INV - Vendor - Add Contract Details (2nd step) - Page and Contract Values

## Description
As a Procurement Specialist I want to be able to add contract details to a vendor contract so that I can specify the item level requirements and attributes before adding items.

*Requirements*:

* *Terms* 
** Contract Amount (Specifies the total dollar amount of the contract)
*** Optional field
*** Numeric value (dollars)
**** two decimal places
** Minimum Purchase amount (This is in $ amounts)
*** This is an optional field
*** this will be a minimum for a single order
**** two decimal places
** Maximum Purchase amount (This is in $ amounts)
*** This is an Optional field
*** this will be a maximum for a single order
