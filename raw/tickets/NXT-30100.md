# NXT-30100 — IM - Items - Create New Item - Food Item - Ingredient Info

## Description
As a District Admin I want an Ingredient Info section so I can specify a few attributes specific to the district and how we use the item.

*Requirements*

* At the time the Food tab is loaded (per [NXT-30099|https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-30099]), display the Ingredient (TBD) Info section 
** this is also when the Serving Measures section is displayed
* Provide the miscellaneous food attributes in a 1/3rd width card
** Edible Yield Factor - numeric field
*** *Note: we could make this a slider from 0-100 ?
** Sub Ingredients - comments popup with up to 500 characters
** Standard Recipe Directions - comments popup with up to 500 characters
** Buy American - checkbox
** Exemption Letter - upload control
*** need to make sure this looks good once a form is uploaded
** Locally Grown - checkbox
* Provide a Save field
** this section does not affect the loading of any other sections
