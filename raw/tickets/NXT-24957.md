# NXT-24957 — PR- Display Units required based on weight on grocery list

## Description
This is a continuation story on the grocery list where we need to display the total units needed based on total weights needed.

## Acceptance Criteria
Calculation to get total units needed.

 Total Weight Per Item/ Unit weight = Total with unit description 

Example:

Pineapple Juice: Amount needed: 1 pound 12 ounce

Unit weight: 80 gram

Unit Description: Bottle

Since the Unit Weight is in gram, it would make the most since to convert all weight to gram to make it easier to calculate

1 pound 12 ounces in grams: 793.7866

793.7866/80 (unit weight)=9.92 

TOTAL to DISPLAY: 9.92 Bottle OR 1 pound 12 ounce

Also on the current weight, round to only 2 decimal points.
