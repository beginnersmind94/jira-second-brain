# NXT-66228 — MP/PR - USDA Corrections for Analysis 2025

## Description
Based on Feedback provided to PE, we are making these corrections before our submission.

## Acceptance Criteria
* Add RAE after mcg for Vitamin A on all displays including Item, Recipe, Menu Item Nutrients, All Analysis pages and all reports associated with nutrients
* Remove Disclaimer For Trans Fat from all pages 
* Remove Trans Fat as a required nutrient on any slide out and also on the *simplified* versions of both the nutrient report and nutrient summary report 
** Applies to *Menu/ Menu cycle analysis* and *pre and post production analysis*
* Display Add Sugars as a Required Nutrient regardless of whether or not product based flag is there or not
** Applies to *Menu/ Menu cycle analysis* and *pre and post production analysis*
** This applies to the slide out also the *simplified* versions of both the nutrient and nutrient summary report
* Provide weekly standard of < 10% of calories for added sugars and provide calculations of percentage of calories (Exact approach as Saturated Fat Calculations)
** Do not fail and only show this as informational purposes until further notice
*** Product Based is still the only way add sugars can fail
** Applies to all versions of reports and applies to *Menu/ Menu cycle analysis* and *pre and post production analysis*
* Remove any reference of Sodium Target 1A by replacing Sodium Target 1A label with Sodium Target 1
** Current Sodium Target 1 can be removed
** Applies to all versions of reports and applies to *Menu/ Menu cycle analysis* and *pre and post production analysis*
* All Nutrient Reports, we must show the unit of nutrient (g, mg, mcg, etc) (Currently not showing that information)
