# NXT-14932 — Data Migration: Import data of customers with NO/Duplicate email address

## Description
+*Summary*+:

During migration of existing customers if

* Any user names (users) have no email address available
** Post migration, users with null email addresses must be notified to update their email address
** When users with null email address logs into Perseus for first time, they shall be forced to update their valid email address
** Updated email address must be valid and unique
* Users with duplicate email addresses
** Post migration, users with duplicate email addresses must be notified to update their email address
** When users with duplicate email address logs into Perseus for first time, they shall be forced to update their valid email address
** Updated email address must be valid and unique

## Acceptance Criteria
* Users with no email address shall be forced to add email during first time login after migration
* Users with NULL or DUPLICATE email address must be notified via email & dashboard to update their email address/profile
* User email address updates shall be validated & uniqueness shall be checked
