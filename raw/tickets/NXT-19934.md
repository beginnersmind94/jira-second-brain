# NXT-19934 — Release 4 - AI Data Insights – PNA AI/ML Model

## Description
As a system, AI insights need AI model to achieve PNA eligibility forecasting rate. 

*Prediction model:*
Paid Not Applied (PNA) eligibility prediction based on eligibility key data and eligibility influence factors

## Acceptance Criteria
*AI/ML Model*

# The system shall provide PNA eligibility forecasting rate 
# The system shall provide Paid not applied (PNA) recommendation for eligibility based on a District/Site with the following factors:
## Students qualify per site (by percentage and by number)
## Students qualify per district (by percentage)
# The system shall recommend influence factor changes for PNA 
a.	Influence factor – percentage affected in PNA converted to eligibility
b.	ADP (Breakfast) – percentage change due to PNA eligibility
c.	ADP (Lunch) – percentage change due to PNA eligibility
d.	MEQ - change by week, month, and year

*Data Output:*

# The system shall output the student list based on PNA eligibility prediction
a.	New students list qualified per site based on PNA eligibility recommendation
b.	New students list qualified by Geo area within the district
c.	New students list qualified by affordability by site
d.	New students list qualified by Ethnicity/Age/Grade
# The system shall output the student list based on Paid Not applied (PNA) recommendation
a.	Student list qualified but application not filled for eligibility (application not on file)
b.	Student list qualified but marked for ‘rejected to apply’ 
# The system shall output the list of districts/schools based on PNA influence factors
a.	List of districts/schools can increase participation based on <influence factors>
b.	List of districts/schools where participation is affected based on <influence factors>
