# NXT-35494 — (Part 2) New District Setup - Improve new user email

## Description
As an SchoolCafé Support user, I need to designate the contact person for a new district that we have created, so that this person will automatically get a welcome email and be able to get started with onboarding rather than waiting for us to manually grant them access in KeyCloak.

*Mockups*

N/A 

*Requirements:*

* When a new district is added in System → Add District, send the email to the new user that has been created:
* Template:

{quote}*Subject:* Welcome to SchoolCafé!

*Body:* 
Greetings, {contactPersonfirstName}!

Your account has been created successfully. To begin the onboarding process, please click or tap on the link below:

{localSchoolCaféInstallationHyperlink}


Best Regards,

The SchoolCafé Customer Success Team {quote}

* When the user clicks on the hyperlink in that email, take them to SchoolCafé where they can presumably update their password, login, and be redirected to the first screen of the Onboarding Wizard.

## Acceptance Criteria
Verify that when that info is saved along with the new district details, we send them a ‘welcome’ email with instructions on how they can sign in and get started.

Verify that when they click the link in the email and update their password, that they are logged in and taken directly to the Onboarding wizard.
