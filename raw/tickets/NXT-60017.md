# NXT-60017 — TH - WS - Workspace Screen

## Description
*As a* TeamHub user *I want* a visually appealing workspace landing screen that provides quick access to key communication features *So that* I can efficiently navigate to the areas of the app I use most and stay informed about my current tasks and notifications.

h2. Comments

* Workspace screen is basically a landing or dashboard page to jump into other areas below it
* All the other workspace children (inbox, CR, Tasks, Team) will need this page to exist

h2. User Flow

* User opens the application and is directed to login screen
* On login success, user is redirected to home screen
* User clicks Workspace and is redirected there

h2. Requirements

* Ensure the screen supports dark mode

_Quick Actions_

* *Section Title*: “Quick Actions” indicates this area is for frequently used features.
* *Action Cards* (4 Cards):
** *Inbox*
*** Title: “Inbox”
*** Subtitle: “Manage your emails and communications”
*** On Tap: Opens Inbox screen (covered in another story)
*** badge: total Unread Inbox Messages
** *Custom Reports*
*** Title: “Custom Reports”
*** Subtitle: “View starred & distributed reports”
*** On Tap: Opens Custom Reports screen (covered in another story)
*** badge: total unread Distributed Reports
** *Tasks*
*** Title: “Tasks”
*** Subtitle: “Manage your daily tasks”
*** On Tap: Opens Tasks screen (covered in another story)
*** badge: total Open Tasks
** *Team*
*** Title: “Team”
*** Subtitle: “View team members and status”
*** On Tap: Opens Team screen (covered in another story)

h4. *Data Requirements*

* Badge counts for each card must be retrieved from appropriate data sources or APIs:
** Inbox: total unread messages
** Custom Reports: total unread distributed reports
** Tasks: total open tasks

h4. *Performance & Usability*

* The screen must load within *2 seconds* under normal conditions
* The layout must be responsive and render correctly on: Mobile, Tablet, Desktop, Compact and expanded views
* All tap interactions must be responsive, with clear visual feedback

h4. *Error Handling*

* Use *try/catch* in all asynchronous calls (e.g., for badge counts).
* Use *error boundaries* for any unexpected render failures.
* Errors must be communicated using *user-friendly messages*:
** Use *toast notifications, modals, or inline messages* without disrupting the user experience.
* Log any API or rendering errors to the *error log system/database* for further analysis.



_Security_

* Any authenticated user can view this screen
