## Acceptance Criteria
* Provide a Product Based Flag that gives selections for what type of product to check for added sugars
** Breakfast Cereals
** Yogurt
** Flavored Milk
* Flag is always optional
* Flag updates to production should only consider plan incomplete and plan complete records (DO NOT affect Record Complete)
* Update analysis flag for any affected menus or records that are plan complete (Dirty Flag)
* Based on these flags, Menu and Production Analysis will run the requirements and provide a pass or fail (Separate Stories)
