# NXT-61363 — INV - Distribution Calendar - Distribution Preview

## Description
As a Distribution Site Manager I want a Preview panel on the calendar so I can get more info on a particular distribution without opening the full Distribution Details page.

## Acceptance Criteria
* Change the OnClick event of the Distribution Calendar to open a slideout instead of directly opening the details
* Provide high level info in the slideout based on the status
** show the message from the footer
** show the last modified user and date
** show the Comments
* Display any relevant alerts
** Ex: Additional Orders after Assignment
* Provide quick access to applicable reports based on the status
** Pick Tickets for Picking statuses
** Delivery Tickets for Shipping statuses
