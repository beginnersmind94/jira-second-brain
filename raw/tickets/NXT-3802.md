# NXT-3802 — AcctMgt - Patron Split View - Restrictions

## Description
*User Story*

As a district administrator I want to see and set any restrictions on an account.

*Mockup*

[https://nqyc9v.axshare.com/start.html#p=category_based_on_patron_view&id=jytwv5&g=1|https://nqyc9v.axshare.com/start.html#p=category_based_on_patron_view&id=jytwv5&g=1]

*Requirements*

* Add a Restrictions section to the Patron Details panel.
* Account restrictions should be clearly outlined if present.
** There are 2 account restrictions that will be needed for MVP.
** 'Charges not accepted' 
*** Helper text: Patron cannot pay at the POS by charging to their account - they will be prevented from going into a negative balance. 
** ‘Checks not accepted'
*** Helper text: This will block payments in the form of a check from being accepted.
* Add an Edit button to the sub-page.
** This activates an edit drawer.
** User can adjust the Account Restrictions outlined above. 
** Save / cancel buttons.
** Data saves appropriately on the account.
* Data consideration:
** We need to save flags for whether the account is marked as ‘No Charges Allowed’ and ‘No Checks Allowed'.

## Acceptance Criteria
Restrictions tab added.
Panel shows information on whether the account is blocked from charging / checks.
Add/Edit mode available.
Data saves appropriately.
