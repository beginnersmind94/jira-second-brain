# NXT-3794 — AcctMgt - Patron Split View - Patron General Info - View/Edit

## Description
*User Story*

As a district administrator I want to see top-level details of a patron without being bogged down by all the finer details.

*Mockup*

[https://nqyc9v.axshare.com/start.html#p=category_based_on_patron_view&id=jytwv5&g=1|https://nqyc9v.axshare.com/start.html#p=category_based_on_patron_view&id=jytwv5&g=1]

*Requirements*

* When selecting a patron account in either split or table view, load an area to the right of the list that shows top-level, current information on the patron.
** Photo
** ID #s (Patron ID, State ID, PIN)
** Full Name (First, Middle, Last)
** Preferred Name
** -SSN / Last 4 Digits of SSN-
** Current Enrollment (Current Site, Grade)
** Homeroom
** Status (Active/Inactive/Merged/Deleted)
** Current Balance
** Current Eligibility + Reason
** Birthday
** Ethnicity
** Race
** Gender
** Primary Language
