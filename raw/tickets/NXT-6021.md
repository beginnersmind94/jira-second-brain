# NXT-6021 — Application Details - Highlights/Determination Panel

## Description
*User Story* 

As an applications processor, I want to see at a high level, the state of an application so i can quickly determine next steps.

*Mockups* 

[https://www.figma.com/proto/yrkJeGfd3f9hWjqm0qvfir/Edge2.0_V1?node-id=152560%3A181926&scaling=min-zoom|https://www.figma.com/proto/yrkJeGfd3f9hWjqm0qvfir/Edge2.0_V1?node-id=152560%3A181926&scaling=min-zoom|smart-link] 

*Requirements* 

* Action at the top of the application details 
** The next step/action in the application process (i.e. Notify - takes them to the notification page to send notification letter) 

!image-20201204-145148.png|width=75%!

* Eligibility Determination Panel (what shows when the application is submitted - state of app)
** ‘Eligibility’ designation
*** Free, Reduced, Denied 
** ‘Basis’ of determination
*** Income
*** Categorical: \[Source]
**** Assistance Programs 
***** (Configuration for local name) - SNAP, TANF, FDPIR
**** Other Source 
***** Homeless
***** Migrant
***** Head Start
***** Runaway 
***** Foster
*** Refused Benefits
** ‘Start Date’ of eligibility 
* -If Denied, Eligibility panel and Eligibility section will show differently- 
** -Top panel > Denied- 
** -Determination > Paid- 
* If Pending (Phase 2) status will show Pending and '_Due to the pending reasons, we cannot reach an eligibility determination.”_

## Acceptance Criteria
- Multi-language, ADA compliant
- actions lead to coordinating pages
- eligibility determination is dynamic and accurate based on status/actions
