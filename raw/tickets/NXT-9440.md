# NXT-9440 — INV - Transfers (District) - Create Transfer - Confirmation

## Description
Once user has created their Transfer ([NXT-9439|https://primeroedgenext.atlassian.net/browse/NXT-9439]) they will click Next and land on the (District) Transfer Confirmation screen.

This screen will provide a read-only summary of the transfer details and provide report options.

After the user has created a transfer and clicked Next, they will be brought to the Transfer Confirmation screen.

+Header section:+

* To Site
* Transaction Date
* Comments

+Transfer Item List:+

* Item #
* Item Description
* Whole Unit Request Qty
* WU Description
* Broken Unit Request Qty
* BU Description
* From Site
* Comments

+Action buttons:+

* Edit Transfer 
** this will take the user back to the Transfer screen to adjust the items & quantities
* Confirm Transfer 
** this will confirm and save the transfer 
** once this is done, no more edits are allowed
*** remove the Edit Transfer and Confirm Transfer button
** after the Transfer is confirmed, the page will provide additional actions:
*** Transfer Ticket
*** Price Details
