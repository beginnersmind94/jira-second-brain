# NXT-24875 — INV - Reports - Site Ordering Guide - Improvements

## Description
As an Inventory user checking a Site Ordering Guide, I want options to see less or more on the report.

*Requirements*

* Add header info from Contract
** Site - show code/number and name in one label
** Vendor - show code/number and name in one label
** Phone Number
** Delivery Schedule (from Contract)
** Lead Time
** Preferred Ordering Method
** Min/Max Purchase Amount (from Contract)
** Comments (from Contract, not Vendor)
* Provide additional report format options
** Items w/ Price
*** Item #, Item Desc, VI#, Contract Period, Current Price
** Items w/ all attributes
*** all above columns + Lead Time, Min/Max Qty, Fees, NOI, etc.
** Custom
*** allows users to select specific columns
*** options:
**** Current Price
**** Lead Time
**** Min/Max Order Quantity
**** Fees & NOI
**** Par Level Quantity
*** *Note: in future, custom reporting will replace this
