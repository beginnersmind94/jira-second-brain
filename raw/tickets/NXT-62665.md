# NXT-62665 — Planner AI - Menu Item Overall Performance (Dsitrict/Site)

## Description
As a District Admin, I would like for there to be a score that shows me my overall best performing menu items based on a combination of metrics.

## Acceptance Criteria
+*Calculate the values:*+

*  *Served Ratio* : Served count/ Produced count
*   *Efficiency Ratio*: (Produced count – Waste)/Produced count
* *Cost Efficiency*: 1- (Menu item cost per serving/Cost per meal)
* *Preference Share :* (Served count /Served Meal Count)* (# of Menu items offered in this Category/Choose count)

+*Factors for calculating the overall performance of the menu item:.*+

* Served Ratio , Efficiency Ratio, Cost Efficiency, Preference Share
* Get the normalized value for each factor.

|*Component*|*Weight*|*Meaning*|
|Served Ratio|0.25|25% of the score comes from how much of the produced item was actually served (measures demand/acceptance).|
|Efficiency Ratio|0.25|25% rewards lower waste — how much of what was made wasn't thrown away.|
|Cost Efficiency|0.20|20% rewards items that are lower cost relative to the total meal cost.|
|Preference Share|0.30|30% rewards items picked over other options in the same category (student choice).|

+*Daily score :*+  

0.25*Normalized value of Served Ratio+ 0.25*Normalized value of Efficiency Ratio+0.20*Normalized value of Cost Efficiency+  0.30*Normalized value of Preference Share

+*District Score for overall performance of a menu item:*+

* Get the normalized value for Daily Score
* Weighted score = Average of normalized daily score of a menu item at  school * school enrollment
* District score of a menu item in percentage = (sum of weighted scores of a menu item from all schools/sum of enrollment from all schools )*100
