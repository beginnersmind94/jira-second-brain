# NXT-3000 — Add Team Work Permissions

## Description
Role permission(s) are assigned to every user - Permissions will determine what the admin has access to view/edit within the module.

!image-20200115-234457.png|width=799,height=241!

Additional:

* *Employee mobility* - ability to move employees across sites at the district realm. Ability to move employees across sites (within a different district) at the central/state realm. 
** At CR, State ID is the unique identifier to move employees across districts (to avoid duplicate IDs/records) - *State ID* setting must be enabled for admins to transfer employees to a site within another district. 
* *batch limit* - is a limit needed to help performance? if not, batch limit isnt needed

## Acceptance Criteria
