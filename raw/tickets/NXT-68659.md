# NXT-68659 — Fin - Display Net Income, Revenue, and Expenses Summary Card on Financial Dashboard

## Description
h3. Business Goal

* Provide district leadership with a *single glance* view of:
** Net income
** Total revenue
** Total expenses
for a selected period (Current Month, Current Quarter, Current Fiscal Year).
* Use this card as the *primary KPI* on the Financial Dashboard for Financials 2.0.

## Acceptance Criteria
*Period selector is a standard dropdown labeled “Period:” in the top right of the dashboard.*

* Period options: Current Month (MMM YYYY), Current Quarter (Q# YYYY), Current Fiscal Year (FY YYYY), Custom Range.
* Default period on dashboard load: Current Fiscal Year (FY ) as of today.
* Changing the period recalculates Net Income, Total Revenue, and Total Expenses and updates the card label (e.g., “Net Income (June 2025)”).

*Current Fiscal Year date range comes from Fiscal Year Settings (start and end dates).*

* Current Month = 1st to last day of the current calendar month, within the fiscal year that includes today.
* Current Quarter uses fiscal quarters (Q1–Q4) defined by Fiscal Year Settings.
** All “Current …” options must fall inside the active fiscal year
* Period date ranges for the quarter, year-to-date and current month are system-defined and not user-editable.

*Data source is posted financial transactions from the General Ledger.*

* Calculations include all active sites.
* Only GL accounts with base type Revenue or Expense are included.
* Include all posted transactions whose transaction/posting date falls within the selected period, even if the fiscal period is still open.

*Total Revenue = sum of GL balances for all Revenue accounts in the selected date range, shown as a positive whole dollar amount.*

*Total Revenue formula: Sum of all credits minus debits for GL accounts whose base type = Revenue within the selected date range.*

*Total Expenses = sum of GL balances for all Expense accounts in the selected date range, shown as a positive whole dollar amount.*

*Total Expenses formula: Sum of all debits minus credits for GL accounts whose base type = Expense within the selected date range.*

*Net Income = Total Revenue – Total Expenses; if negative, show with a minus sign (e.g., -$125,000).*

*Net Income card title format: “Net Income ()”.*

* Main Net Income number is shown in large text with currency formatting and thousands separators.
* Total Revenue and Total Expenses are shown below as smaller values, rounded to the nearest dollar.

*If there are no posted Revenue or Expense transactions in the selected period, show “0” for the respective column and calculate net income assuming it’s value as zero.*

*Trend arrow compares Net Income for the latest completed fiscal month vs the immediately previous fiscal month.*

* A completed month = a fiscal month that has fully passed in real time.
* If latest > previous show ↑; if less show ↓; if equal show no arrow.
* Hide arrow if fewer than two completed fiscal months exist in the current fiscal year.
* Trend arrow is not affected by the selected reporting period.
* Tooltip: *“Trend compares the two most recently completed fiscal months.”*

*Site-by-site performance table shows Revenue, Expenses, and Net Income (Revenue – Expenses) for each active site, plus a district total row at the bottom.*

* Columns: Location, Revenue, Expenses, Net Income.
* For the district-level entry, use the district name.
* Net Income only appears when both Revenue and Expense totals are present; otherwise show “–”.
* Tooltip: *“Net Income is unavailable because Revenue and/or Expenses are missing for this period.”*
* Table uses only posted GL transactions and the same selected period as the dashboard.
* Default sort is Net Income descending; secondary sort by Location name.
* Sites with no data show “–” in numeric fields.
* Only show *active* sites; do not display inactive sites.
