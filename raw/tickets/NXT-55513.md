# NXT-55513 — FH - Payments - Info for other Payment methods

## Description
To follow USDA regulation, we need to provide updated information to users indicating how to contact the school district for alternative food service payment options. 

The current "Make a Payment" page includes a popup that displays each time the page is accessed, or the hyperlink is clicked for the payment guidelines. It contains district level settings for fees. We would like to add another sentence to this popup with the following text:  

"For alternative payment methods to your child's food service account, please contact your school district at [district profile phone number]." 

!image-20241002-223536.png|width=1531,height=590,alt="image-20241002-223536.png"!

In the example message above, we would use the 999-888-7777 number in the district profile. 

!image-20241002-223603.png|width=790,height=502,alt="image-20241002-223603.png"!

The payment popup also does not auto-translate.
