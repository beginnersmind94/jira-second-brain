# NXT-5228 — Application Entry - Processed: Determination Panel 

## Description
*User Story* 

As an applicant, i can see the determination panel based on complete application information.

*Mock Ups* 

!image-20201120-223017.png|width=675,height=100!

*Requirements* 

* Determination Panel shows details based on data entered in application
** Eligibility, Basis, Start Date 
* Application must be in ‘*Processed’* (Complete) state to make a determination 
** No pending reasons in the Pending Reasons section
* Applications entered via manual entry extend benefits to matched students included in the app
* Updating Eligibility/Basis for student already on apps
* Consider the Received Date/Processed (Setting) 
** Look at current AY price types for student 
** Start/End Dates for eligibility window should update accordingly 
* If categorical benefits for student(s) exist in the application, the application will look at income for remaining student(s) and the basis of the application will be income. 
** Categorical: \[benefit] will be the designation for the individual student eligibility – currently Direct Approval in classic 
** If the benefit is not confirmed, the default is to look at income. Once the categorical benefit is confirmed (other than foster selection), the student’s eligibility basis will be reflected accordingly (from income to categorical)
