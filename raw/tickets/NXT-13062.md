# NXT-13062 — POS - Service - Do not serve items containing allergens associated to the account

## Description
As a cashier I want the POS terminal to flag and restrict the sale of items to accounts where the item/account have matching allergens so that patrons are safe from consuming things that would be bad for their health.

When selecting an item, we need to check against the student allergies and the allergens within the item. If there is a match of the allergen, we should restrict the item from being sold and display a message to the cashier that "This item contains allergens associated with this this account and cannot be sold."
