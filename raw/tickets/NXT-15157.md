# NXT-15157 — Accountability - Programs - SFSP Claims - Calendar year rate claims

## Description
As an SFA Admin I want to have a better idea on what my reimbursement total will be. While the State does have the final say in this, it is nice to compare apples with apples. Seeing an accurate reimbursement total in PrimeroEdge will increase my confidence in the product's ability to generate claims.

Requirements
* SFSP Claim Creation needs to check for calendar year when gathering the rates to determine claim $$.
* Example - in January 2021, it will need to check the rates configured for AY 21-22.
