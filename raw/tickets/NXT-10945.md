# NXT-10945 — Year Begin - Student Eligibility Updates

## Description
As a district admin I am required to issue students a carryover period for their eligibility into the new academic year. I expect the system to handle this for me automatically.

*mockups*

n/a

*business info*

Another automated task that needs to happen as of 7/1. Students need to carryover their existing status (previously called grace period) for their eligibility into the new school year, with an expiration date set by the district. This is to give households time to reapply without having to begin paying for meals.

*requirements*

* System needs to automatically insert new eligibility records for students who are Free and Reduced as of 6/30.
** Same eligibility (free or reduced)
** Start date of 7/1, end date per the carryover end date for the site they’re enrolled at.
** Reason is ‘Carryover’
* If this system setting is set to yes, then the system also needs to insert a free + carryover eligibility for any student at a CEP site as of 6/30.
** CEP Sites - Automatically apply Free Grace Period (Carryover) eligibility during Year Begin
* All other accounts are given a new entry with Paid - Default.
