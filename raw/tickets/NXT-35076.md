# NXT-35076 — PR- Pre and Post Production Analysis- Slide out and Nutrition and Nutrition Summary Report sodium and Milk display changes (USDA CORRECTIONS)

## Description
USDA has requested some changes to be made on the slide out and the nutrition, and nutrition summary report 

* Display Sodium Target 1 should be less than or equal to the value (Same as Target 1A)
** This should be changed on the Analysis Slide out, Nutrition Report and Nutrition Summary Report
** Logic needs to be changed to allow the sodium target to be equal to the amount of sodium and still pass (DB CHANGE NEEDED)
* On the show Required on the Slide out, Remove “Low-fat (1% or less), unflavored”, only Fluid Milk should display in regards to any milk information on the slide out
* On Show required toggle off, Remove All Milk related information except for Fluid Milk and Varieties of Milk
** Varieties of Milk should be a simple Pass or Fail based on whether all the days in the analysis are passing the variety of milk per day
*** If Fail Display in the red border to stay consistent with the rest of the slide out display
* Apply The Removing of All milk related information except for Fluid Milk and Varieties of Milk on the Nutrition Report
** Varieties of Milk should be a simple Pass or Fail based on whether all the days in the analysis are passing the variety of milk per day
*** If Fail Display in the red border to stay consistent with the rest of the display
