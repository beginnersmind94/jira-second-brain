# NXT-68734 — Insights - Insights Dashboard Page - Inventory Value KPI (Backend)

## Description
As a district inventory manager, I want to view the total Inventory value at any given time and for all or selected school, so that I can monitor stock levels and make decisions on purchasing or redistribution. Helps maintain optimal inventory levels to avoid overstocking or stock outs.

## Acceptance Criteria
* Using the Inventory Valuation Report calculations
** Inventory value:
*** For today, this week, this month, year to date - it will be calculated as of today and 11.59pm
*** yesterday - as of yesterday 11.59pm
*** last week - as of last day in the last calendar week at 11.59pm
*** last month- as of last day in the last month at 11.59pm
*** prior year, any numbered previous years -  end of the year inventory value taking the time at 11.59pm
*** For Custom date range: its going to be calculated as of the selected "to date" date at 11.59pm
* Benchmark/Expected value/Inventory total goal:
** when all schools selected (or multi site selection if its allowed) and for the selected Academic year: sum of  site benchmark values for all schools 
** when a specific school selected for the selected academic year: display the value set for the selected school and year.
** Today, yesterday, last week, this week, this month, last month, year to date, -takes the current academic year benchmark
** prior year: one year prior to current academic year
** numbered years in the past -takes the value from the specific year
** Custom date range: Display the benchmark value set for the academic year for the "to date" (ideally from and to dates will be in the same academic year , if not we will take the academic year for the to date selected in the custom date range. )
