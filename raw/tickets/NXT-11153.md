# NXT-11153 — SchoolCafe Integration -  Student information synchronization - Batch support

## Description
+*Acceptance criteria*+

* If SchoolCafe or Perseus are unavailable to be synchronized for a period of time, there shall be a mechanism to synchronize large amounts of student information once the 2 systems are available.
** This mechanism shall be performant and scalable.
* Account balances shall be synchronized with student information
* Food restrictions shall be synchronized with student information
