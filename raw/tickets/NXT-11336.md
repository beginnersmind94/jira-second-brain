# NXT-11336 — SchoolCafe Integration - Real-time food restrictions synchronization

## Description
* Food restriction information between SchoolCafe and Perseus shall be kept synchronized in real-time.

## Acceptance Criteria
Changes to food restriction information in SchoolCafe shall be synchronized with Perseus within 5 seconds.
Changes to food restriction information in Perseus shall be synchronized with SchoolCafe within 5 seconds.

If SchoolCafe or Perseus are unavailable to be synchronized for a period of time, there shall be a mechanism to synchronize them once the 2 systems are available.

This mechanism shall be performant and scalable.
