# NXT-18804 — Platform - Licensing - Summary Page Enhancements

## Description
*Persona*: CyberSoft/ISD Administrator

*Access level:* School District/Site

*Description*:

As a Operational site admin, I should be able to view list of products we are enrolled with along with recommended products and related products. 

*Requirements*:

* Site admin logs into System/Licensing Summary page for list of products displayed in “My Products” section
** +My products section table with below additional columns+
*** Actions
** Types of licenses
*** District
** VIEW icon (eye-icon) is enabled for users to click and open the detailed view of product tab.
** HISTORY icon is enabled for each products displayed in My products displayed

## Acceptance Criteria
* List of products procured by the customers shall be displayed with actions enabled in actions column
* User shall be able to see VIEW and HISTORY icons for each product displayed in the table
* User shall be able to click on VIEW button that brings up the drawer with sites and licensing details with a summary
* User shall be able to close the details drawer and return to product summary table
* User shall be able to click on HISTORY icon and view the detailed audit history of the product.
