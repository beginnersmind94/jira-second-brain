### Edit 1 — GUIDE-011 Edit and Add Transactions

NXT-54012 (resolved 2024-11-04). Insert under step 1 of *Change Payment Amount or Method*.

**Current**

> 1. On the right-hand panel, enter the Amount Tendered, Deposit Amount, or Change Returned in the New column, if needed

**Proposed**

Partial payment, previously only available at the POS, now applies on the Add/Edit Transaction screen and is gated by an override permission when charge limits or restrictions intervene (Appendix A.1).

### Edit 3 — GUIDE-013 Reconciliation

NXT-36439 (resolved 2023-04-18) + NXT-53996 (resolved 2024-08-23). New subsection after *Reconcile a Closed Session*.

**Current**

> *(no equivalent — new subsection)*

**Proposed**

> ### Delete a Session
> The Delete Session option removes a session and adjusts off its transactions. Online payment sessions cannot be deleted, and sessions at Central Office are blocked from deletion (these typically contain Family Hub payments and refunds); contact Support to remove one.
>
> 1. Select Delete Session
>    A confirmation prompt requires a comment and shows the number of transactions and dollar payments in the session.
> 2. Confirm the deletion
>    A 30-second Undo is available. After that, all transactions are adjusted off and the session moves to Deleted status. Support cannot reverse the deletion.
> 3. View the resulting adjustments on the Transactions / Adjustments page
>    The adjustment uses the user's comment in the format "Session Deleted: <comment>".

**Why**
Delete Session was added with confirmation, comment, and 30-second Undo, and is permanently blocked for sessions containing online payments — including the Central Office sessions that typically carry them (Appendix A.3).

**Open questions** (2)
- Exact UI control that opens Delete Session (Description: "More Actions dropdown on the Reconciliation page" — not in AC or RN).
- Should Restore Session (NXT-54040, resolved 2024-10-25) be documented as a sibling subsection? NXT-53996's RN explicitly anticipates it.
