# How to Create a Staff Account Quick Guide

> Seeded from the raw extraction. Edit freely. When the raw drifts, review the diff and update this file, then mark it reviewed with `python scripts/mark_guide_reviewed.py GUIDE-033`.

The following steps outline the process of creating a staff account in Family Hub.
 Only creating a parent account is recommended if you are a staff member and the parent of a child in the district.
Once you create a parent account, you can add yourself to it.
Process / Descriptions Images
To view the district homepage, follow the steps below. If you already have a link to the homepage, proceed to step 4.
In the Need to create an account or contact us? section of the login page
1. Select your State
2. Enter the name of your School District
   School district names will populate as you enter characters into the field.
3. Click the Go to My District button
   The district’s homepage displays.

4. Click the Create a new account button
   The registration page displays.

5. Select the I’m an Employee of this District option
6. Click the Next button to continue

7. Enter your School Name
   School names will populate as you enter characters into the field.
8. Click the Next button to continue

9. Click the Continue with Apple button to link your existing Apple account OR
10. Enter the following information:
- First Name
- Last Name
- Email
- Phone Number
11. Click the Next button to continue

12. Enter the following information:
- Username
The system will indicate if the username is available.
- Password
Passwords must contain more than eight characters, both upper and lowercase letters, and at least one number.
- Confirm the password
- Select a Security Question
- Enter an Answer for the security question
- Select the preferred Language
13. Click the Checkbox to accept the Terms &
   Conditions
14. Click the Create My Account button to continue
   A security code will be sent to your email to verify your account.

15. Enter the 6-digit verification code
16. Click the Verify button to continue
   You will receive a message saying your email has been verified.

On the Dashboard
17. Click the Link icon to connect your cafeteria account

18. Enter the following information:
- School ID
- Select your School
- Last Name
19. Click the Find My Cafeteria Account button to link your SchoolCafé account with your
   Cafeteria ID account
   If you cannot find your cafeteria account, contact your nutrition office.
