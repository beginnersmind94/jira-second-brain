Receipts w/o Orders page
 to 2 decimals.

Click on the settings icon.

In the Management tab, select System Settings and go to the Inventory tab.

Search for the setting 'Orders - Number of Price Decimals'. It is currently set to 4 decimals. Update it to 2 decimals and save the setting.

Log out and log back in, then check the Receipts w/o Orders page to verify that the prices are rounding to 2 decimals.

Please follow the above steps and let us know if it works or if you require any further assistance.
