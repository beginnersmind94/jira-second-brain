# Potential Matches — Quick Reference

**Goal:** clear today's Potential Matches queue.
**Who:** child-nutrition manager.
**When:** every business day until the queue is empty after a new DC file import.

## Key fields (in the drawer)

| Field | What it means | Where to look |
|---|---|---|
| Photo, full name, preferred name | Identifies the student | Top of drawer |
| Student ID | Your district's enrollment ID | Top of drawer |
| Current Eligibility + Reason | What the student is on right now | Top of drawer |
| Current Enrollment Site | Site they're at right now (may differ from import day) | Top of drawer |
| Match Points | Total score across all categories | Middle of drawer |
| Personal / Contact / ID Numbers | The three scoring categories | Middle of drawer |
| Comments | Free-text notes from prior reviewers | Bottom of drawer |

Source: [[raw/tickets/NXT-15058|NXT-15058]].

## Do this

1. Open **Eligibility → Direct Certification → Potential Matches**.
2. Click a row to open the drawer.
3. Read **Match Points**. Open **Comparison Scorecard** if you need the field-by-field breakdown.
4. Decide: **Match** (comment required) or **Mark as Reviewed**.
5. Repeat until the queue is empty.

## Watch out for

- **Comment is required to Match** — the action will not save without one. Source: [[raw/tickets/NXT-15058|NXT-15058]].
- **Current Enrollment Site may differ** from the site on the DC file if the student moved.
- **"Reviewed" is not the same as "Not a Match"** — it just means a human has handled the row. You can reopen and Match a Reviewed row later. Source: [[raw/tickets/NXT-15058|NXT-15058]].
