# SOP — {{Procedure name}}

> **Trigger:** what event starts this procedure.
> **Owner:** which role runs it.
> **Frequency:** daily / weekly / monthly / on-demand.
> **Time:** typical duration.

## When to run this

One or two sentences. Be specific about the trigger. Avoid "as needed."

## Steps

Numbered, action-first. Each step says **who** does **what**, on **which page**, with **which fields**.

1. **{Role}** — does X on **{Page}**. Fields: A, B, C.
2. **{Role}** — does Y on **{Page}**. …
3. …

## Checks before you finish

How the runner confirms the procedure worked. Be testable — "the count on the dashboard matches the count on the report" beats "everything looks right."

- [ ] Check 1
- [ ] Check 2

## Escalation

If a check fails or a step blocks, who do you contact and with what information?

- Block at step N → escalate to {role} with {evidence}.
